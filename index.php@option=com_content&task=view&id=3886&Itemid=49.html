Interview Melanie Rene (Switzerland 2015):  Die große Entscheidungsshow took place on Saturday, 31 January 2015 in the Bodenseearena of Kreuzlingen in Switzerland. It is the show in which Switzerland selected their representative to the Eurovision Song Contest and for 2015 Melanie Rene won the Swiss National Final with the song "Time to Shine". In the allocation draw Switzerland was placed in the second half of the Second Semi Final. Radio International was present in the arena when Melanie won and could conduct an interview with the singer on stage after the show. Listen to this interview on this week's edition of Radio International.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here

Interview with Sebalter (Switzerland 2014): Switzerland was represented by Sebalter at the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 with the song "Hunter of Stars" which ranked 13th in the Grand Final after successfully qualifying from the Semi Final. Being the winner of Die Große Entscheidungsshow 2014 Sebalter was invited to be part of the jury of the Swiss National Final in Kreuzlingen. This year Swiss TV decided to count the result as 50% Televoting and 50% Jury and the winner became Melanie Rene with the song "Time to Shine". After the show Sebalter came to the Press Centre where the Team of Radio International interviewed the Swiss Ambassador to the Eurovision Song Contest 2014. Listen to this interview on this week's edition of Radio International.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview with Hersi Matmuja (Albanis 2014):  A lady with a great voice and charme on stage also was at the OGAE Germany convention on 24 January 2015. Hersi Matmuja represented Albania at the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 with the song "One night's anger" which unfortunately did not manage to get in the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contet. Albania charted at Number 15 in Semi Final 1 when only the Top 10 advance to the Grand Final. But HErsi with her voice and charme convinced the German fans who invited her for the annual OGAE Germany convention where Radio International had a chance to interview the young amazing singer. Listen to the interview on this week's edition of Radio Internationa.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - The National Final Season 2015: Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - The National Final Season 2015:  The National Final Season to the Eurovision Song Contest 2015 is well under way.  During the last weeks Radio International went through the history of Austria at the Eurovision Song Contest. Austria will host the Eurovision Song Contest 2015 in the Wiener Stadthalle in Vienna next May with the Grand Final on the 23 May 2015. Like in previous years the Radio International Eurovision Experts will be assessing the National Finals 2015 live on Radio International. Ross Bennett continues with the new season taking a look at the National Finals and entries Cyprus, Switzerland, Albania and Georgia.
 
The Eurovision Top 40 Semi Final entries that failed to make it into the grand final of the Eurovision Song Contest but according to our listeners should have made it:  Over the past couple of years Eurovision Radio International has on a monthly basis featured a selection of listeners's choices in the third hour of what they thought should have made it to the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest. Marcus and JP then played the choices in form of a Top 20 Chart in the show.  Now at the end of 2014 Radio International entered all the listeners' choices into the computer and has now worked out a Top 40.  For the next three weeks JP will be counting down the Top 40 Semi Final entries in the third hour of the show. This week listen to the final positions 11 to Number One. What will be Number One? This will also be the end of the series.
