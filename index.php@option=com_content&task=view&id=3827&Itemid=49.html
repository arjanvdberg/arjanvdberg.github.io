Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2014 - Interview with Krisia (Bulgaria 2014): The Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2014 took place in Malta on 15 November 2014 with debutant Italy winning the event. The song "Tu primo grande amore" was performed so passionately by Vinzenco Cantiello on stage and won the event with 159 points on the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2014 stage in the Marsa Ship Yard. The runners up at Number 2 and Number 3 were Bulgaria and Armenia. Listen to the interview with Krisia from Bulgaria who received a total of 147 points for the song "Planet of the Children". The interview was done after the Winner's Press Conference and listen to it on this week's edition of Radio International. You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here

Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2015 - Interview with Franklin: The Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2015 took place on 21 & 22 November 2014 from the Marsa Ship Yard in Malta with 20 songs entering into the Semi Final then narrowed down to a Top 14 and then on Saturday the winner was the public favourite as well as the jury favourite - Amber Bondin will be representing Malta at the Eurovision Song Contest with the song "Warrior" written by Elton Zarb and Matt Muxu, who also were responsible for the victory of Gaia Cauchi at the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013 with the song "The Start". On this week's edition of Radio International listen to the interview with Franklin during the rehearsals. Franklin's song "Still here" was penned by Norway's Eurovision Winner 2009 - Alexander Rybak who also came to Malta to support Franklin who qualified for the Final the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2014 and at the end of the Final "Still here" ranked 5th. This was Franklin's third attempt to obtain the ticket to the Eurovision Song Contest for Malta. Enjoy the indept interview with the singer on this week's edition of Radio International.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 

The Malta Experience 2014 with David Mann:  David Mann is a regular contributor to the weekly edition of Radio International when his voice is heard on Eurovision Undercover. Also there is no exception this week however, David will be joining JP and telling us all about his experience being in Malta and working with PBS (Public Broadcasting Service, Malta) at the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2014 and also at the Malta Eurovision Song Contest. Enjoy this trip to the Maltese Islands as told by David Mann. Last week there was not enough time to tell the entire Malta story - so David will be rejoining JP on this week's edtion of Radio International as the story continues.
 
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - Austria in Eurovision:  Continuing this week when Radio International takes you right to the National Selections to the Eurovision Song Contest 2015. Austria initially won the Eurovision Song Contest in 1966 for the very first time with Udo Juergens singing "Merci Cherie" and then the second victory came in 2014 with Conchita Wurst and "Rise like a Phoenix" and with this will be hosting the 2015 edition of the Eurovision Song Contest. As in previous years the Eurovision Spotlight has reflected on the songs of the next host country and this year is no exception. The Radio International Eurovision Experts will be taking a look at Austria's history at the Eurovision Song Contest. The series continues this week with Dermot Manning. Enjoy the flashback into Austria's entries to the Eurovision Song Contest.
