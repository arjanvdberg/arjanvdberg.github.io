Eurovision Song Contest 2015 - The POST EUROVISION DEPRESSION Cure:  What an amazing 60th Eurovision Song Contest ORF and EBU staged in the Wiener Stadthalle in three amazing shows with two Semi Finals and the Grand Final on Saturday 23 May 2015. 40 countries competed with 20 countries making it into the Grand Final meeting the the Host Country Austria as well as the Big 5 (France, Germany, Italy, Spain and United Kingdom) plus special guest Australia. The 60th contest was won by Måns Zelmerloew from Sweden with the song "Heroes"  Many fans and journalists left Vienna with tears in their eyes saying farewell to two weeks of a real good time departing from the Eurovision Family for yet another year. The POST EUROVISION DEPRESSION (PED) has conquered Europe and beyond and Eurovision Radio International is the best cure for it week by week as we reflect on the Eurovision Song Contest 2015 with broadcasting the many interviews done in Vienna. For the results of the Semi Finals and the Grand Final please go to eurovision.tv or click here.

Interview with Maraaya (Slovenia 2015): What a great success for Slovenia qualifying for the second year in a row for the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest as Maraaya performed the song "Here for you" in Semi Final 2 where it ranked at Number 5. At the end of the Grand Final Voting Slovenia ranked at Number 14 which is better than last year's result with Tinakara Kovac and the song "Round and Roud". Always performing with their headphones on stage this is Maraaya's trademark. JP and Eric met up with the couple after their press conference in Vienna for an interview which you can hear on this week's edition of Radio International. You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview with Genealogy (Armenia 2015): Armenia was represented by a multi-national group of singers all routing to Armania including Inga who was part of Inga and Anush at the Eurovision Song Contest 2009 who performed the song "Nor par (New Dance)" reaching Number 10 back then in the Grand Final. Armenia is a country that almost always qualified for the Grand Final apart from 2011 and no participation in 2012. Armenia's song this year is called "Face the Shadow" and also qualified for the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest 2015 and then at the end of the night ranked at Number 16. JP met the group after their first press conference in Vienna for an interview you can hear on this week's edition of Radio International.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview with The Makemakes (Austria 2015): The Host Country of the Eurovision Song Contest 2015 - Austria was represented by The Makemakes following in the footsteps of Conchita Wurst, well not quite as they did not win the Eurovision Song Contest but made a record in the history of Europe's biggest music competition - as host country receiving no points at all. The Makemakes performed the song "I am yours" and did not have to go through the Semi Final stages. JP caught up with the singers right after their press conference in the Press Centre in Vienna. Listen to an interview with the group on this week's edition of Radio International. You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
