Eurovision Song Contest 2012 Participants
Below you will find the list of participating countries of the 57th Eurovision Song Contest coming to you from Baku, Azerbaijan on 22, 24 & 26 May 2012.  Semi Final 1 is on Tuesday, 22 May 2012, Semi Final 2 on 24 May 2012 and the Grand Final on Saturday, 26 May 2012.  Where there is a video available, you can see it by clicking on (watch video) behind each country. Have fun!
