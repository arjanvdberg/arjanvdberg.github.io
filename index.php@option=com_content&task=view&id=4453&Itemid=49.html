Hightlights of the show

Eurovision Preview Events 2016 - Riga Eurovision Pre-Party and Eurovision in Concert - Live Interview with Rene Romkes:  The National Final Season 2016 is over and all the songs that are competing at the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 in Stockholm, Sweden come May have been selected. Various countries started to invite participating countries to their Preview or Promo Events such as Eurovision in Concert in Amsterdam on 09 April 2016, The London Preview Event on 17 April 2016 as well as the latest being added is Tel Aviv in Israel and for the second time in a row the annual Eurovision Pre-Party in Riga, Latvia which took place on Saturday 02 April 2016. JP of Radio International was present at this and in collaboration with joinusoneurovision.com interviewed all the artists that were invited. The first lot of interviews will be broadcast on Radio International this week and more to come within the next weeks.  The next Promo event is Eurovision in Concert this weekend and Rene Romkes who is part of the organising team will be joining JP on the show this week to talk about this weekend's event. 
 
Interview with Ira Losco (Malta 2002 & 2016):  Back in 2002 Malta came second at he Eurovision Song Contest  with Ira Losco and her entry "7th Wonder". In 2014, together with the smiling doctor Gianluca Bezzina (Malta 2013), Ira Losco hosted the Maltese National Final. Then in January 2016 Ira Losco entered the Malta Eurovision Song Contest competing with two songs out of which "Cameleon" made it to the Final and won it. Shortly after, Maltese broadcaster pbs made use of its rule to change the song which was publish on 13 March 2016, Now Malta will be represented at the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 by Ira Losco and the song "Walk on Water" and will compete in Semi Final 1 on Tuesday, 10 May 2016. JP met Ira in Riga for an interview you can hear on this week's edition of Radio International.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here

Interview with Poli Genova (Bulgaria 2011 & 2016): Also the Bulgarian entrant is a repeat offender to the Eurovision Song Contest as Poli Genova already represented Bulgaria at the Eurovision Song Contest 2011 with the song "Na na'inat" which failed to qualify for the Grand Final. In November 2015 Poli Genova hosted the Junior Eurovision Song Contest from Sofia, Bulgaria and shortly afterwards Bulgarian TV decided to send again Poli to the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 with the song "If love was a crime" which competes in Semi Final 2 of the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 on Thursday, 12 May 2016. JP chatted with Poli in Riga right after the Press Conference of the event. You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here

Interview with Hovi Star (Israel 2016):  One of the most funniest representative Israel has sent into the Eurovision Song Contest is this year's hopeful Hovi Star who will be representing Israel with the song "Made of Stars" which competes in Sem Final 2 of the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 on Thursday, 12 May 2016. JP had the pleasure to chat with the young singer at Riga's Eurovision Pre-Party 2016. Listen to the interview on this week's edition of Radio International.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here

Interview with Jüri Pootsmann (Estonia 2016):  On of the mosy impressive voices of the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 is Juri Pootsmann who won the nationa final in Estonia and with this the right to represent his country at the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 in Stockholm, Sweden with the song "Play". Juri was also part of the Eurovision Pre-Partyin Riga, Latvia and gave an interview to Radio International which you can hear on this week's edition of Radio International.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here

Interview with Sanja Vucic ZAA (Serbia 2016):  Representing Serbia at the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 is Sanja Vucic ZAA who will be performing the song "Goodbye (Shelter)" competing in Semi Final 2 on Thursday, 12 May 2016. JP met the young bubbly singer at the Eurovision Pre Party event in Riga, Latvia for an interview with you can hear on this week's edition of Radio International. You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here


Interview with Rykka (Switzerland 2016):  Switzerland will be represented by Rykka who won the Swiss National Final called "Die Grosse Entscheidungsshow" and with this the right and the ticket to respresent Switzerland at the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 in Semi Final 2 with the song "The last of our kind". Also here JP met the young singer at the Preview Event in Riga, Latvia for a chat and you can hear this interview on this week's edition of Radio International.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here

Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - Eurovision 2016 in Regions: For the next few weeks the Eurovision Experts of Radio International will be taking a look at the 2016 Eurovision entries per region and this week Dermot Manning will be staring with the Southern Eurovision countries that are competing at the Eurovision Song Contest 2016. This week hear the songs from Albania, Cyprus, Greece, Israel, Italy, FYRO Macedonia, Malta and  San Marino. In addition, each week our correspondents will be asked to tell us their personal favourite but also their tip what could actually win the Eurovision Song Contest 2016. Sweden will host the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 in the Globen Arena of Stockholm next May with the Grand Final on the 14 May 2016 and the the Semi Finals on 10 & 12 May 2016, respectively.

Eurovision Under Cover:  This ever so popular feature on Radio International is David Mann's weekly edition of the Eurovision Cover Versions where David Mann from Eurovision Norwich will be intoducing some of the finest Eurovision Song Contest entries covered by other singers. Just be surprised. You will be hearing the actual song that has been on the Eurovision stage followed by the cover. Listeners are encouraged to send in their cover versions they came across over the years to share with the other fans tuning into the show. Alternatively, listeners can send in their request for a particular Eurovision covered song using this link. 


Eurovision Song Contest News: Nathan Waddell joins the team in the studio every week to bring you the news and the latest developments connected to the Eurovision Song Contest such as the National Finals or if it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc Nathan will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International. He also takes a look at some statistics such as viewing figures in the various countries and more. Nathan is website editor with escxtra.com. In addition, Eurovision News is available on www.eurovision.tv, esctoday.com and escflashmalta.com (amongst others.
