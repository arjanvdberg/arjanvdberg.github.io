SHOW CONTENT- Longest Wait to Victory & Eurovision's Repeat Offenders

(Hour 1 and Hour 2) Eurovision Radio International this week airs a special show as Alasdair Rendall and JP have gone through the results (compiled by Stephen Boddington) of the countries taking part in the Eurovision Song Contest to see which country actually has the Longest Wait to Victory. Enjoy Alasdair Rendall and JP guiding you through this special show for the first two hours. 

 

(Hour 3)This week's third hour will continue the mini series of Eurovision's Repeat Offenders where you can find out which Eurovision performers have returned once, twice or even more times to represent their country at the Eurovision Song Contest or even perhaps another country :) 

Due to the special show this week there is no Eurovision News, Interviews, Quiz andother features. Also Radio International cannot take any requests to be played this week - however, you can send the music requests in to CONTACT for next week's show where all is back to normal.

Enjoy this week's special edition of Eurovision Radio International!
