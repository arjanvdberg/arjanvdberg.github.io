Eurovision Song Contest 2014 - Radio International on location in Copenhagen - a recap:  The Team of Radio International while at the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 produced lots of material in terms of interviews with the Eurovision 2014 performers plus some specials which you will hear on Eurovision Radio International during the next weeks curing your Post Eurovision Depression as much as possible. A special thanks go to the team members on location working hard to get this coverage to you. A pile of photos and videos are on our Facebook and YouTube Channel. But here is the link to the Photo Albums where you can enjoy the photos from two weeks of Copenhagen and Eurovision Song Contest 2014. Album 1   |  Album 2    |   Album 3  |   Album 4. A detailed listing of the voting results of the two Semi Finals and Grand Final is available here.   
 
Interview with Andras Kallay-Saunders (Hungary 2014): What an amazing result for Hungary at the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 when Andras Kallay-Saunders represented his country with the song "Running" achieving an amazing fifth position in the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 in Copenhagen. A rather shy singer who grew up in America has recently said he would like to attempt again to win ADAL which is the Hungarian National Final and represent Hungary again at the Eurovision Song Contest. David Mann met the singer for an indebt interview in the Press Centre of the Eurovision Song Contest and a little plus interview when JP bumped into Andras in the Media Lounge and had a little chat with him. Listen to both interviews on this week's edition of Radio International.    You can watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here  
 
Interview with Tijana Dapcevic (FYRO Macedonia 2014): One of the biggest stars from Macedonia represented her country at the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 - Tijana Dapcevic and the song "To the sky" which unfortunately did not make it to the Grand Final of the event. It reached Number 13 in Semi Final 2 when only the Top Ten qualify for the Grand Final. Also here Radio International had the pleasure of two interview sessions with the singer as Eric and JP had the pleasure to meet the singer twice in the Media Lounge of the Eurovision Song Contest 2014. Listen to both clips on this week's edition of Radio International. You can watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here for Interview 1 and here for Interview 2  
 
Interview with Twin Twin (France 2014):  La Republic Francaise was represented by one of the funniest acts in recent years sent in from France to the Eurovision Song Contest - Twin Twin who unfortunately did end up at the anchor position of the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 with the song "Moustache". However, as France is part of the BIG 5 they were already qualified automatically for the Grand Final. Radio International team members had the pleasure to meet the guys from Twin Twin a few times on their way to Copenhagen such as Eurovision in Concert in Amsterdam but also at the London Preview Event earlier on in April. JP interviewed the guys from Twin Twin in the Media Lounge of the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 which you can hear on this week's edition of Radio International. You can watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here  
 
Interview with Pollapoenk (Iceland 2014): Radio International has already broadcast an interview with the funny gang from Iceland however, there is still a little clip left from a small interview with Pollapoenk as Radio International met the group after the Semi Final Winners' Press Conference to share with them the feeling of joy when they qualified Iceland to the Grand FInal of the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 with the song  "No Prejudice" that at the end of the contest reached Number 15. You can watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here  
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - Eurovision Songwriters - This week:  Paul Quintens & Phil van Cauwenbergh:   The Eurovision Song Contest 2014 is over and after Eurovision is before Eurovision. Many countries will have Eurovision events throughout the summer into the autumn to bridge the Post Eurovision Depression. Also Radio International does not stand still and delivers in the weekly three hour show lots of items to make you forget the PED. In the current series the Eurovision Experts of Radio International take a look at the songwriters in Eurovision Song Contest. Continuing this week is Chris Poppe with taking a lock at the works of Paul Quintens & Phil van Cauwenbergh and the nice songs they have added to the ears of the many Eurovision fans. 

Eurovision UnderCover:  Last week, Radio International started a brand new series on the show featuring Eurovision Cover Versions where David Mann from Eurovision Norwich will be intoducing some of the finest Eurovision Song Contest entries covered by other singers. Just be surprised. You will be hearing the actual song that has been on the Eurovision stage followed by the cover. Enjoy the brand new series.  Listeners are encouraged to send in their cover versions they came across over the years to share with the other fans tuning into the show. Alternatively, listeners can send in their request for a particular Eurovision covered song using this link. David Mann's been digging deep in his treasure box for this week's Eurovision Cover song. Don't miss it!
 
New Song Releases by Eurovision Artists:  Radio International is always keen to know how the careers of Eurovision performers develop after their performance at the event.
