Eurovision Song Contest with Radio International and on Oikotimes.TV

Interview with ARD Television Coordinator for Entertainment - Mr. Thomas Schreiber on the preparation of the 2011 Eurovision Song Contest: Last weekend the annual convention of the Eurovision Club (EC) Germany took place in Cologne, Germany. OGAE & EC Germany presidents had the pleasure to interview Mr. Thomas Schreiber during the event and asked questions regarding the upcoming 2011 Eurovision Song Contest. Issues such as accommodation, ticket sales, etc were mentioned. Radio International's News man Luke Fisher interviewed Mr. Schreiber and during this week edition you can hear this particular interview.

Eurovision Interview with Alenka Gotar: (Slovenia 2007):  Only recently OGAE Luxembourg held their annual Eurovision Gala in a city of Roeser in Luxembourg. Amongst the Eurovision performers invited was also the representative of Slovenia at the 2007 Eurovision Song Contest - Alenka Gotar who with her fantastcic voice and entry is to date the only performer that managed to qualify her country for the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest since the inaugeration of the Semi Finals.  In the Grand Final "Cvet z juga" reached Number 15. For further detailed information on Alenka Gotar please consult her website which you can reach here . The Team of Radio International had the great pleasure to meet and interview the singer in Luxembourg. Enjoy listening to it during this week's edition of Radio International.

Euro Friend Song Contest 2010 - HEAT 9: The Qualifiers: Radio International in collaboration with the Euro Friends Song Contest will be announcing during the show this week the ten qualifiers of HEAT 9 of what the international juries have been voting for on the Facebook page. On the Face Book Page of the Euro Friends Song Contest, one could vote for your ten favourite songs in the Eurovision Song Contest  amongst the nineth hundred songs of Europe's biggest music competition.  Two weeks ago Radio International revealed the ten qualifiers of HEAT 8 and now HEAT 9 is closed and the results will be announced during the show by Andy Simon, who is one of the prime organiser of Euro Friends Song Contest 2011.  Heat 10 will start very soon and you can join to vote for your favourite Eurovision songs by clicking on this link which takes you to the Face Book Page of the Event. Remember, you have to be a member in order to join the voting.   
 
Melodifestivalen 2011 - Interviews with the the Winners of the Web Wild Card and "Mr. Melodifestivalen" - Christer Bjoerkman:  On the weekend in Stockholm, Sweden, the voting show took place to decide the two winners of the Web Wild Card to the Melodifestivalen 2011. Lilian Brunell has been at the show and interviewed the winners Julia Alvgard "Better or Worse" and Jonas Matsson "On my Own". In addition, Lilian interviewed Mr. Melodifestivalen - Christer Bjoerkman on his satisfaction of the result. Listen to the interviews during this week's edition of Radio International. 
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight: Germany. In view of the 2011 Eurovision Song Contest from Germany, Radio International's four regular Eurovision Experts will be joining us in turn week by week to review all the German entries of the Eurovision Song Contest from 1956 to 2010. This week Chris Poppe takes a look at the years 1980 to 1984.

Duesseldorf Quiz with Jutta.  To the run up of the 2011 Eurovision Song Contest staged in Germany's city of Duesseldorf and Radio International's own Jutta is originally from Duesseldorf we though to give you some homework in form of a weekly quiz on the next Eurovision Host City. Send your answers to CONTACT on our website not later than midnight the Tuesday following the live broadcast of Radio International. Next week Jutta will do Quiz -2 but now here are again the three questions of the Duesseldorf Quiz 1:

City:
How many districts does Duesseldorf have and in which one is the Esprit Arena located where the 2011 Eurovision Song Contest will be held next May 10, 12 & 14?

Sport:
What is the name of the local soccer team and what is their contribution to the Eurovision Song Contest 2011?

Food:
Next to Altbier, Coffee and tea what is the typical drink of the city of Duesseldorf?
 
Home Composed Song Contest 2010: Home Composed Song Contest 2010: Radio International continues the support of this songwriters competition also in 2010. Austria virtually is hosting this contest this year and visitors to the website of the OGAE Austria could cast their vote until 31 October 2010 to their favourite songs. Now the voting is over and the calculation starts. In the first week of December 2011 the results will be published by OGAE Austria and Radio International will be broadcasting a special on the results.
