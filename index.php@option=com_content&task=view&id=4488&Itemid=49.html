Hightlights of the show

Eurovision Song Contest 2016 not long to go:  The Eurovision Song Contest 2016 in Stockholm, Sweden is just around the corner and many delegations and countries who take part in the contest are on their way to Sweden. This week Eurovsion Radio International has the final show before departing to Stockholm for two weeks. For the next two editions Radio International will broadcast the annual traditional Eurovision 2015 meets Eurovision 2016 on May 04 and 11 from Stockholm in Sweden, but there will also be special PODCASTS available and many more interviews with this year's Eurostars will appear on your YouTube Channel. So keep an eye on that and on our Facebook Group Eurovision Radio International for constant updates. But for the show this week - more Eurostars 2016 in interview bringing you the artists a little bit closer and preparing you for Eurovision 2016. Read on for the details.

While in Stockholm at the 61st Eurovision Song Contest - the Team of Radio International will produce PODCASTS containing many more interviews with this year's Eurovision performers. Watch out the weekly Show Archive and also the Facebook page of Eurovision Radio International for details. 
 
The Radio International Eurovision Song Contest 2016 Chart:  Over the past few days Team Members of Radio International as well as fans to the show have been voting and ranking to songs of the Eurovision Song Contest 2016. On this week's show JP will run down all the songs of this year's contest as voted for. What is topping the list? And then will this match the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 Final result on 14 May 2016. Let's go and see. All very exciting. Check out the chart on this week's edition of the show.   If you can't wait to hear the outcome - you find the complete chart here.
 
Live Interview with Hera Björk (Iceland 2010): A huge Fans' favourite amongst the fans of the Eurovision Song Contest is Iceland's Hera Björk who has been on the Eurovision stage more than once. She was backing vocalist for the Icelandic entries of 2008 (Euroband), 2009 (Yohanna) and 2015 (Maria Olafs) but also represented Iceland in 2010 with the song "Je ne sais quoi" which came 19th in Oslo in the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest. Hera is a very diverse artist and has been invited to many Fan conventions across Europe and beyond plus winning the Vina del Mar International Song Contes 2013 t with the song "Because you can". She also competed in the Danish national final 2009 coming second with the song "Someday". On 09 April 2016 she co-hosted the annual Eurovision in Concert in Amsterdam, The Netherlands and now she will take her new show to Eurovision Town 2016 - Stockholm, Sweden  The show is called "Hera Björk - The Queen of Effing Everything" and will premier in Stockholm. There are two performances planned on Saturda, 07 May 2016 (1730 and 2100 hours CET) in the venue: Playhouse Teater, Drottninggatan 71a, 11136 Stockholm, Sweden. Tickets can be booked here. Radio International is extremely happy to welcome Hera Björk live on the show joining JP to chat about this new show with Hera. 
 
Interview with Michal Szpak (Poland 2016):  Poland's representative to the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 is Michal Szpak who will be performing "The color of your life" in Semi Final 2 at Song 2 in Stockholm on Thursday, 12 May 2016. Michal has been attending many of the Eurovision preview events such as Amsterdam's Eurovision in Concert, The London Pre-view event and Israel calling. Radio International's David Mann in the Radio International Studio 3 in London interviews Michal during the show which you can hear on this week's edition of Radio International.  
 
Interview with Douwe Bob (Netherlands 2016): The Dutch Ambassador to the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 is Amsterdammer Douwe Bob who will perform a country song called "Slow Down" in Semi Final 1 at Song 6. With this he goes into the footsteps of The Common Linnetts who in 2014 almost won the Eurovision Song Contest with the song "Calm after the storm". Douwe Bob was at Eurovision in Concert in Amsterdam on 09 April 2016 where Radio International's David Mann chatted with the young singer about his Eurovision participation for the Netherlands; Listen to this interview on this week's edition of Radio International.   You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview with Francesca Michielin (Italy 2016):  Italy's entry to the  Eurovision Song Contest 2016 is young Francesca Michelien who came through Italy's legendary San Remo Song Festival 2016 and was nominated to represent Italy at this year's biggest European music contest with the song "No degree of Separation" which due to Italy being part of the Big Five will be automatically qualified for the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest 2016.. Alasdair had the pleasure to chat with the Francesca at Eurovision in Concert in Amsterdam on 09 April 2016. You can hear this interview on this week's edition of the show. Due to lack of time during this week's show the interview was not broadcast however You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview with Eneda Tarifa (Abania 2016): With being one of the early countries to select their entry to the Eurovision Song Contest late in 2015 with Festivali i Këngës, Albania will be represented by a Eneda Tarifa who will compete with the song "Fairytale" in Semi Final 2 at Number 18 at the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 in Stockholm. Alasdair Rendall chatted with the singer at the Press Meeting of Eurovision in Concert in Amsterdam on 09 April 2016. Find out all about Minus One in the interview on this week's edition of Radio International. Due to lack of time during this week's show the interview was not broadcast however You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview with Nicki French (United Kingdom 2000 & Host of the London Eurovision Preview Event 2016):  She would "kill" to go back to Eurovision as she told Radio International in previous interviews - Nicki French who represented the United Kingdom at the Eurovison Song Contest 2000 also coming from Stockholm, Sweden from the Globen Arena. Back then she performed the song "Don't play that song again" which came 16th in the final ranking of the night. Five year's earlier - in 1995, she had a chart smash all over Europe with a dance version of Bonnie Tyler's "Total eclipse of the heart". Again Nicki is the favourite presenter to host the Eurovision Preview event in London's Cafe de Paris on 17 April 2016 and David Mann from Radio International had the big pleasure to chat with Nicki on this year's Eurovision Song Contest. Hear this interview on this week's edition of Radio International.
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - Eurovision 2016 in Regions: The Eurovision Experts of Radio International will be taking a look at the 2016 Eurovision entries per region and this week Ross Bennett will be closing out the series and be taking a look at the Eastern Eurovision countries that are competing at the Eurovision Song Contest 2016. This week hear the songs from Armenia, Azerbaijan, Bulgaria, Georgia, Moldova, Romania (though excluded now), Russia and the Ukraine. In addition, each week our correspondents will be asked to tell us their personal favourite but also their tip what could actually win the Eurovision Song Contest 2016. Sweden will host the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 in the Globen Arena of Stockholm with the Grand Final on the 14 May 2016 and the the Semi Finals on 10 & 12 May 2016, respectively.
