Hightlights of the show

Eurovision Song Contest 2016 - The POST EUROVISION DEPRESSION CURE - Dose 5:  The Eurovision Song Contest 2016 is over and the best ever Eurovision Song Contest was staged in the Globen Arena with two Semi Finals and a Grand Final which was held on Saturday 14 May 2016. The 61st Eurovision Song Contest was won by Ukraine's Jamala with the song "1944". Many fans of the Eurovision Song Contest fall into a deep Post Eurovision Depression as it all is over and the fans part company until the next year. Radio International is the perfect Post Eurovision Depression Cure reflecting on the past contest with interviews and reports and of course the music and remixes. The full results of the contest can be viewed by clicking here. 
 
Interview with Amir (France 2016):  A very charismatic singer France sent into the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 who was also tipped to be the winner of the 61st Eurovision Song Contest. Unfortunately, it did not work out this time. Amir represented France with the song "J'ai cherche" came 6th in the Grand Final on Saturday 14 May 2016. Radio International's David Mann had the pleasure to chat with Amir during the rehearsal week.
 
Interview with Iveta Mukuchyan (Armenia 2016):  The musical Ambassador for Armenia at the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 was Iveta Mukuchyan who performed the rather modern composition called "LoveWave" which sucessfully qualified Armenia for the Grand Final and then ranked at a remarkable Number 7 at the end of the exciting voting sequence of Eurovision 2016. Iveta speaks fluent German as she grew up in Hamburg Germany but find out more details about her in the interview that Fernando and JP did with the beautiful singer from Armenia.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview with Ira Losco (Malta 2016):   Already in 2002 a young Ira Losco represented Malta at the Eurovision Song Contest with the song "7th Wonder" that almost won the Eurovision Song Contest back then coming 2nd. In 2016 she returned to the World's biggest music event to once agin represent Malta with the song "Walk on Water" which made it into the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest in Stockholm and at the end of the voting sequence and ranked at Number 12. Fernando and JP  met the singer for a for an interview after her first rehearsal and the Press Conference. You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview with Poli Genova (Bulgaria 2016):  The best ever placed result for Bulgaria at the Eurovision Song Contest as Poli Genova with the song "If love was a crime" came fourth at the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 with a total of 307 points. Poli is not stranger to the fan base of the Eurovision Song Contest since she represented Bulgaria already once before in 2011 with the song "Na inat" which unfortunately at that time did not qualify Bulgaria for the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest and reached Number 12 in the Semi Final 2. However, in 2016 Poli achieved the best ever result for Bulgaria at the Eurovision Song Contest reaching Number 4 with "If love was a crime". Furher Poli also hosted the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2015 last December from Sofia, Bulgaria. Radio International's Eric caught up with the singer during the rehearal week in Stockholm. Listen to this interview on this week's edition of Radio International.
 
Interview with Nika Kocharov and the Young Georgian Lolitaz (Georgia 2016):  Georgia at the Eurovision Song Contest 2016  was represented by Nika Kocharov and the Young Georgian Lolitaz performing the song "Midnight Gold" which sucessfully qualified Georgia for the Grand Final and then at the end of the voting reached Number 20 with the song "Midnight Gold". Fernando and JP from Radio International chatted to the band in the Press Centre after their rehearsal and their Press Conference.  Listen to the interview on this week's edition of Radio International. There was not enough time to broadcast this interview in this week's edition of Radio International and has been rescheduled for airing in next week's edition of the show. Sorry for the inconvenience. 
 
Interview with Serhat (San Marino 2016):  San Marino's Musical Ambassador at the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 was in fact a Turkish Man by the name of Serhat who himself is a huge show host on Turkish television where he hosts amongst others the popuar Turkish version of the game show "Jeopardy". Further Serhat can add to his credit the Number One song not only in Germany but also in other countries with the song "Je m'adore". He was hired to represent San Marino at the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 with the song "I didn't know" which was performed in its disco version. Unfortunately, San Marino did not qualify for the Grand Final in 2016 as the song only made it to Number 12 in Semi Final 1 when only the Top Ten countries advance to the Grand Final. However, a great personality Serhat chatted with JP and Fernando from Radio International telling us about his show buisiness and singing career and his way to Eurovision. Enjoy this great interview with Serhat on this week's edition of Radio International.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview with Minus One (Cyprus 2016):  Cyprus was represented at the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 by the band  Minus One which performed the song "Alter Ego" which successfully qualified Cyprus for the Grand Final and then ranked at Number 21 the end of the Voting Sequence of the Grand Final. Radio International's Fernando and JP met the members of Minus One for an interview in the Press Centre to find out more details. Enjoy listening to this interview which is on air in this week's edition of Radio International.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - The Dreaded Number 2 Position:  Through the Summer 2016 the Radio International's Eurovision Experts came up with the idea to take a look at the dreaded starting position of the Euroision Song Contest from where no country ever won the Eurovision Song Contest. This week Dermot Manning opens up this brand new series and you will be hearing some nice Eurovision oldies as well in this near Eurovision Spotlight. Enjoy the new Radio International Summer series.
