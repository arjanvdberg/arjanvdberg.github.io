The Eurovision Song Contest with Eurovision Radio International - your music requests, comments and ideas: Eurovision Radio International never stops and is keen on keeping the music and news of the Eurovision Song Contest in the ears of the many fans that join the show each and every week via the usual means either on FM, Cable and also via PODCAST, Itunes, LISTEN AGAIN, live broadcast or Live Stream. Hightlights of the show

  Interview with Linda Martin (Eurovision Winner 1992, Ireland): Linda Martin was special guest of honour at the recent edition of the OGAE Germany's annual Eurovision Convention on 21 January 2017 in Munich, Germany. She represented Ireland twice at the Eurovision Song Contest when in 1984 she came 4th with  "Terminal 3" and then subsequently won the contest back in 1992 with the Johnny Logan composed song  "Why me". Marc from Germany's Prince Blog and JP from Radio International chatted with Eurovision Royalty before her soundcheck in the venue. Enjoy the Irish Charme on the show this week and listen to two dance versions of Linda's Eurovision entries. You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Live Interview with Sergiu Bolota (Eurovision 2017, Romanian National Final): The Romanian National Final is scheduled to take place on 05 March 2017 and this week Radio International has the pleasure to be chatting live with one of the contestants  that will be battling it out for the ticket to Kiev and the Eurovision Song Contest 2017. Sergiu Bolota will be competing with the song "We were young" which you will also hear on the show this week. If you want to find out more about Sergiu go to his website - Sergiu Bolota
 
 
Interview with Jon Ola Sand (Executive Supervisor Eurovision Song Contest): David Mann spoke to Jon Ola Sand at the recent edition of Eurovision You Decide, the UK National Final to the Eurovision Song Contest 2017. Listen to this interview on this week's edition of Radio International.
 
 
 
  Interview with Alex Larke formerly of Electro Velvet (United Kingdom 2015):  Radio International's David Mann also spoke 50% of the Duo that represented the United Kingdom at the Eurovision Song Contest 2015, Alex Larke of Electro Velvet. They reached Number 24 at the Eurovision Song Contest 2015 with the song "Still in love with you" in Vienna, Austria. In the meantim, Alex has gone on with his solo project. and has just released his brand new album from which Radio International plays the latest song release called "Shine on".  
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - The National Final Season 2017: The National Selection Process of the songs that will represent their country at the Eurovision Song Contest 2017 is well underway. In the last quarter of 2016 Radio International went through the Eurovision entries of the Ukraine as Ukraine will be hosting the Eurovision Song Contest 2017 in Kiev May 09, 11 and the Grand Final on May 13 following Jamala's victory in Stockholm last May with the song "1944". Like in previous years the Radio International Eurovision Experts will be assessing the National Finals 2017 live on Radio International. Alasdair Rendall continues with the series taking a look at the National Finals 2017.
Eurovision Under Cover:  This ever so popular feature on Radio International is David Mann's weekly edition of the Eurovision Cover Versions where David Mann from Eurovision Norwich will be intoducing some of the finest Eurovision Song Contest entries covered by other singers. Just be surprised. You will be hearing the actual song that has been on the Eurovision stage followed by the cover. Listeners are encouraged to send in their cover versions they came across over the years to share with the other fans tuning into the show. Alternatively, listeners can send in their request for a particular Eurovision covered song using this link.

Eurovision Song Contest News: Nathan Waddell joins the team in the studio every week to bring you the news and the latest developments connected to the Eurovision Song Contest such as the National Finals or if it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc Nathan will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International. He also takes a look at some statistics such as viewing figures in the various countries and more. Nathan is website editor with escxtra.com. In addition, Eurovision News is available on www.eurovision.tv, esctoday.com and escflashmalta.com (amongst others.
 
New Song Releases by Eurovision Artists:  Radio International is always keen to know how the careers of Eurovision performers develop after their performance at the event.
