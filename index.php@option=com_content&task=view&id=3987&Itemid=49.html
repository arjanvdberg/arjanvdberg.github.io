Eurovision's Greatest Hits: On 31 March 2015, the BBC  in collaboration with Swedish TV SVT celebrated the 60th anniversary of the Eurovision Song Contest and  produced a Eurovision Celebration Show staged in London's Eventi, Apollo, Hammersmith. The show is called "Eurovision's Greatest Hits" and featured a large number of previous Eurovision Winning acts such as Loreen, Emmelie de Forest, Lordi, etc. Details of the event can be seen here. To coincide with this special event Radio International will air a large number of Eurovision Greatest Hits during the show but also Radio International aired an interview done in London with John Kennedy O'Connor who published a book  "The Eurovision Song Contest: The Official Celebration" which is out now. In case you missed this interview just visit the Show Archive from 01 April 2015 and listen to the show for the interview. 

Also on this week's show

Eurovision in Concert 2015 - Interviews: On Saturday, 18 April 2015 Eurovision in Concert 2015 took place in Amsterdam's Melkweg Concert Venue with the participation of 26 Eurovision act, being 24 countries who will compete at the Eurovision Song Contest 2015 in Vienna, Austria in May. Then Edsilia Rombley (Netherlands 1998 and 2007) as being the co-host of the concert together with Cornald Maas the Dutch TV Commentator of the Eurovision Song Contest and the charming winners of the Eurovision Song Contest 1985 for Norway - The Bobbysocks. Before the concert in the evening there was a Press event where international and national media could meet the participating artists for interviews and photo sessions. Radio International was also represented and interviewed a large number of artists. Over the next three weeks you will hear the result on the show. See below what is on this week's edition of the show. See the photos of the media event as well as from the Concert in Melkweg as well. For that click here. 
 
Interview with Elnur Hueseynov (Azerbaijan 2015):  Elnur is no stranger to the world of the Eurovision Song Contest as he and his singing partner Samir represented Azerbaijan as the first Azeri entry ever at the Eurovision Song Contest with the song  "Day after Day' which came 8th in the Eurovision Song Contest 2008 in Belgrade, Serbia. In 2015 Azerbaijan once again sends in Elnur Hueseynov on his own with a fantastic power ballade called "Hour of the Wolf" following Dilara Kazimova with "Start a Fire" from 2014 that reached Number 21 in Copenhagen. This year Azerbaijan will start in Semi Final 2 at song Number 11. Thank you to Konstantin for the help in translation during this interview you hear on this week's edition of Radio International. You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview with John Karagiannis (Cyprus 2015): Welcome back Cyprus! As they return to the Eurovision Song Contest with John Karagiannis representing Cyprus in 2015 with the song "One thing I should have done" which will start in Semi Final 2 at Song Number 15. Cyprus took a break of a year. Their last participation was in 2013 in Malmoe with Despina Olympiou with the song "An me Thimase" reaching Number 15 in Semi Final 1. The first time Cyprus did take part in the Eurovision Song Contest was way back in 1981 with the song "Monika" performed by Island reaching Number 6. Now good luck to Johnin Vienna. Find out more about him during the interview on this week's edition of Radio International. You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here  
 
Interview with Ann Sophie (Germany 2015): As one of the big five countries Germany is directly qualified for the Grand Final to be staged on 23 May 2015 in Vienna's Stadthalle. Germany's Musical Ambassador is Ann Sophie who will represent Germany with the song "Black Smoke". Germany took part of the Eurovision Song Contest since the very beginning in 1956 with one year break in 1996. Two victories: Nicole's "Ein bisschen Frieden" in 1982 and Lena's "Satellite" in 2010. Will Ann Sophie be able to achieve a third victory for Germany?? Good luck! Radio International had the pleasure to meet the singer in Amsterdam's Eurovision in Concert for a lovely chat you hear on this week's edition of the show.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here 
 
Interview with Monika Kuszynska (Poland 2015):  After last year's success of Poland at the Eurovision Song Contest with Donatan and Cleo's "My Slowianie" reaching Number 14 in Copenhagen, this year's representative of Poland is Monika Kuszynska who sings "In the Name of Love" which will be closing out Semi Final 2 of the Eurovision Song Contest 2015. Poland debuted at the Eurovision Song Contest in 1994 with Edyta Gorniak and "To nie ja" which straight rocketed to Number 2. Since then this result has never been reached again. Perhaps this year could be the year. Monika will perform in a wheel chair as she is paralyzed for a number of years. Radio International met the singer at Eurovision in Concert 2015 for an interview to find out more about Monika.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview with Morland and Debrah Scarlett (Norway 2015):  Norway won the Eurovision Song Contest three times with Bobbysocks in 1985 "La det swinge", in 1996 with Secret Garden and "Nocturne" and in 2009 with Alexander Rybak's "Fairytale". Carl Espen represented Norway in 2014 with "The Silent Storm" reaching Number 8 and following in the footsteps of Carl Espen is Morland and Debrah Scarlett who will be representing Norway with the song "A Monster like me" in Semi Final 2 at Song Number 6. Find out more about this year's Norwegian entry during the Interview on this week's edition of the show.  Please note this interview has been moved to next week's edition of the show due to lack of broadcast time. 
 
Interview with Monika and Vaidas (Lithuania 2015):  Lithuania's best ever result at the Eurovision Song Contest was in 2006 when LT United reached a remarkable Number 6 in the Grand Final with the song "We are the Winnes of Eurovision". Will "This Time" Lithuania be topping this result with Monika and Vaidas as they represent Lithuania with the song "This Time" opening up Semi Final 2. Radio International meet the singers in Amsterdam during Eurovision in Concert for an interview which you can hear on this week's edition of the show. Please note this interview has been moved to next week's edition of the show due to lack of broadcast time. 
 
Interview with Bojana Stamenov (Serbia 2015): Also Serbia too off for one year and returns to the Eurovision Song Contest 2015 with Bojana Stamenov who will be singing the song "Beauty never lies" in Semi Final 1 at Song Number 9. To date Serbia have one victory at the Eurovision Song Contest when in 2007 Marija Serifovic won the contest with "Molitva" in Helsinki. At Eurovision in Concert 2015 Bojana received the biggest applause from the audience after her performance. So could this be a sign for another victory. Get to know Bojana in the interview Radio International did with the singer during the press meeting session before the concert. You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here  
 
Interview with Elhaida Dani (Albania 2015): 2015 sees the 12th participation of Albania at the Eurovision Song Contest and this honour goes to Elhaida Dani who will representing Albania with the song "I'm alive" in Semi Final 1 at Song Number 14. Albania's highest achievement at the Eurovision Song Contest was in 2012 when Rona Nishliu placed Albania at Number 5 in the Grand Final with the song "Suus" in Baku. Radio International met Elhaida prior to Eurovision in Concert 2015 for an interview you can hear on this week's edition of the show.  Please note this interview has been moved to next week's edition of the show due to lack of broadcast time. 
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - Eurovision 2015 in Regions:  For the next few weeks the Eurovision Experts of Radio International will be taking a look at the 2015 Eurovision entries per region and this week Marcus Keppel Palmer continues with the Eurovision countries that are competing at the Eurovision Song Contest 2015. This week hear the songs from The West: Australia, Belgium, France, Ireland, Netherlands and United Kingdom. In addition, each week our correspondents will be asked to tell us their personal favourite but also their tip what could actually win the Eurovision Song Contest 2015.  

Venezuela's Top 50 Eurovision Songs of 2014:  Every year the Eurovision fans of Venezuela get together and vote for the Eurovision songs of the current year and 2014 was no exception.  The voters not only took into consideration the songs that were in the Semi Finals and Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 but also the National Finals and the Junior Eurovision Song Contest of 2014.  Rafael Vivas sent in the Top 50 chart to Radio International for broadcast.  Over the next weeks Radio International will be broadcasting these songs and running down the chart during the broadcast.  What did Venezuela vote for to be the best song of the 2014 Eurovision year?  Find out on Radio International.
