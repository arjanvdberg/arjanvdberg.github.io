Radio International TV Channel with Chat Room: Radio International launched its own Livestream TV Channel where fans can join and see how we actually do Radio International and listen to the show while chatting with the other Eurovision fans joining the community.  But also you can watch there a re-run of the weekly edition of Radio International. Enjoy and tell your friends to join and be part of the Eurovision Radio International Community!

Please note that many interviews we air are also available as video interview which can be viewed on our Radio International YouTube Channel. Just take a look and enjoy also see the artists talking to us. 
 
Radio International Summer Special:  As a tradition every summer Radio International has live interviews with performers and presenters of the Eurovision Song Contest. This week is the week and for that JP is joined by Eric in the studio and the team is working on a special line-up of artists and presenters to join us for a live interview during this week's edition of Radio International.  Listeners who have questions to any of our guests on this week's show are encouraged to send them in via the Radio International Website by clicking here. 
 
Live Interview with Petra Mede (Eurovision 2013 Host):  The Eurovision Song Contest 2013 took place in the Malmoe Arena in Sweden and was hosted by only one presenter - Petra Mede who hosted the two semi finals and the Grand Final in a very souvereign way. Earlier on in the year the choice of Eurovision 2013 Host fell on Petra who previously also hosted Sweden's biggest TV event - Melodifestivalen. To find out more about the Swedish Comedien please click here. Radio International is extremely happy to have Petra with us now by phone from Sweden. Listeners who have questions to any of our guests are encouraged to send them in via the Radio International Website by clicking here. 
 
Live Interview with Daria Kinzer (Croatia 2011): Croatia was represented by Daria Kinzer at the Eurovision Song Contest 2011 in Duesseldorf, Germany with the song "Celebrate" that unfortunately, did not qualify for the Grand Final of the the contest. But this did not stop Daria from continuing in her music career as she very recently released an album for the German speaking music market entitled "Zwischen Himmel und Erde" and has many TV performances and shows in addition. Let's find out more about Daria and her life after her participation at the Eurovision Song Contest 2011.  Listeners who have questions to any of our guests are encouraged to send them in via the Radio International Website by clicking here. 
 
Live Interview with Stella Jones (Austria 1995):  It was only last week when Radio International aired an interview with a performer from the Eurovision Song Contest 1995 namely Liora who came 8th with the great "Amen".  From the very same Eurovision Song Contest, Radio International is delighted to be chatting live with Stella Jones who back in 1995 represented Austria with the song "Die Welt dreht sich verkehrt" reaching Number 13. Listeners who have questions to any of our guests are encouraged to send them in via the Radio International Website by clicking here.   
 
Interview with Roberto Bellarosa (Belgium 2013): The Kingdom of Belgium was represented by young Roberto Bellarosa at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 with the song "Love Kills" which successfully qualified out of Semi Final 1 for the Grand Final where the song ranked at Number 12. Originating from "The Voice" in Belgium  Roberto was interviewed by David Mann at the hotel of Roberto Bellarosa which airs on this week's edition of Radio International. 
 
There was not enough time to broadcast the below interviews. They will be rescheduled into the future editions of Radio International. 
 
Interview with El Sueno de Morfeo (ESDM)(Spain 2013):  As Spain is also one of the Big Five Eurovision countries and with this directly qualified to the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest. El Sueno de Morfeo represented Spain this year with the song "Contigo Hasta el Final (With you all the way)" and unfortunately only ending up at Number 25 in the Grand Final, although they are big artists in their home country. Eric and Rene chatted with the singers in an interview which you can hear on this week's edition of the show.   You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview with Who See (Montenegro 2013):  Representing  Montenegro at the 58th Eurovision Song Contest was the band Who See with the song "Igranka" which unfortunately, failed to qualify for the Grand Final as it only reached Number 12 in Semi Final 1 in Malmoe. A very modern entry from Montenegro including great effects on stage in a kind of space suits. The Team of Radio International had the pleasure to meet the three peformers as part of the Meet & Greet in the Euroclub after their rehearsal.  You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview with Moje 3 (Serbia 2013):  Serbia was represented at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 by Moje 3 who won BEOSONG with the song "Ljubav je svuda" reaching Number 11 in Semi Final 1 of the contest sadly not qualifying Serbia for the Grand Final which is is the second time in the history of Serbia's participation at the Eurovision Song Contest that the country did not qualify for the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest following Marko Kon & Milan Nikolic with the song "Cipela" back in 2009. Only the Top 10 qualify for the Grand Final. Moje 3 were supported by former Winner of the Eurovosion Song Contest 2007 - Marija Serifovic and the Team of Radio International had the pleasure of interviewing the three ladies of Moje 3 which you will be able to hear on this week's edition of Radio International. This is a second interview done with Moje 3 after Radio International previously already broadcast the first interview. You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - The Favourite Non-Winning Eurovision entries per country:  The Eurovision Song Contest 2013 is over and all our correspondents have given us their views and favourite songs of this year's contest. So time to get into the summer 2013 with a brand new series in which our correspondents in the UK and Ireland look at our favourite non-winning Eurovision entries per participating country. This week Chris Poppe continues the series with the countries beginning with the letter L & M.
 

Eurovision UnderCover:  Last week, Radio International started a brand new series on the show featuring Eurovision Cover Versions where David Mann from Eurovision Norwich will be intoducing some of the finest Eurovision Song Contest entries covered by other singers. Just be surprised. You will be hearing the actual song that has been on the Eurovision stage followed by the cover. Enjoy the brand new series.  Listeners are encouraged to send in their cover versions they came across over the years to share with the other fans tuning into the show. Alternatively, listeners can send in their request for a particular Eurovision covered song using this link. David Mann's been digging deep in his treasure box for this week's Eurovision Cover song. Don't miss it!
 

New Song Releases by Eurovision Artists:  Radio International is always keen to know how the careers of Eurovision performers develop after their performance at the event. The show is in collaboration with many Music Companies that supply Radio International with the latest songs of their signed up artists to bring the muisc closer to the fans. On a weekly basis Radio International receives songs which are being featured on the show. This week new songs by Cascada and the Sunstroke Project.
