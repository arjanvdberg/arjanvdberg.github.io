Interview with Cascada (Germany 2013):  On Thursday, 14 February 2013 Germany selected their entry to the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 for Malmö, Sweden. In a smashing exciting show hosted by Anke Engelke from the TUI Arena in Hannover a mix of Televoting, Jury Voting and Radio Voting the choice fell on to Cascada who are no stranger to the world of Dance and Pop music. Founded in 2004 and fronted by lead-singer Natalia Horler, Cascada had a string of hits in the early 2000s One of their biggest hits was "Every time we touch". More details are available here. Radio International in collaboration with Euro-Friends.com and escXtra.com have a couple of interviews with the lead singer Natalia done right after the Press Conference held after the victory on Thursday. Cascada will be representing Germany at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 in the Grand Final on 18 May 2013 with the song "Glorious". You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Live Interview with Zoë Alexis (Ireland 2013, Pre-Selection):  Ireland will be selecting their entry to the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 this coming Friday, 22 February 2013 in RTE's Late Late Show. Five acts will be competing for the Ticket to Malmö, Sweden where the 58th Eurovision Song Contest will be staged. Amongst the five acts is also this week's guest on Radio International - Zoë Alexis who will be performing the song "Fire" as written by Lauren White, Niall Mooney and Willie Weeks, in the Late Late Show. More details about  the singer Zoë Alexis of course during the interview on Radio International this week where listeners and chatters via the livestream.com/radint channel will have the chance to ask questions to  Zoë as well. For this click on CONTACT.  A small biographie can be found here.
 
Interview with Amber (Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2013): On Saturday, 02 February 2013 Malta selected their entry to the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 with a young medical doctor by the name of Gianluca Bezzina who will be the Ambassador of Malta in Malmoe waving the Maltese Flag and singing the song "Tomorrow" which won Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2013. The full result of the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2013 can be viewed here.
Continuing the series of interviews with Maltese music talent Radio International this week airs an interview with Amber who came 4th in the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2013 with the song "In Control". Amber was part of the Maltese Delegation in Baku being on stage with Kurt Calleja performing "This is the night". Amber's first appearance in the Malta Eurovision Song Contest was in 2011 with two songs and also competed in 2012. Listen to the interview with Amber of this week's edition of Radio International. You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here

Interview with Chris Grech (Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2013):  Following to Amber in the Final Ranking of the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2013 is Chris Grech who as newcomer to this competition came 5th with the song "Never walk away". To find out more about Chris listen to the interview with the young musician also on this week's edition of Radio International.  You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here

 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - The 2013 National Finals with Alasdair Rendall: The National Selection period is in full swing where countries are selecting their entry to the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 that will be staged in Malmö, Sweden on the 14 May 2013 (Semi Final 1),  16 May 2013 (Semi Final 2) and 18 May 2013 (Grand Final) where so far 39 countries will be competing for the trophy and the rights to hold the Eurovision Song Contest 2014. As every year Radio International reviews the National Finals with the regular correspondents and this week it is the turn of Alasdair Rendall who will be taking a look at the selection process of Austria, Germany, Greece and Latvia. Find a full listing of the upcoming National Finals here.

New Song Releases by Eurovision Artists:  Radio International is always keen to know how the careers of Eurovision performers develop after their performance at the event. The show is in collaboration with many Music Companies that supply Radio International with the latest songs of their signed up artists to bring the muisc closer to the fans. On a weekly basis Radio International receives songs which are being featured on the show.
 
 
This week's latest arrival on the show's desk is the new song by Hera Bjoerk which you can hear on Radio International this week. It is called  "Because you can" and details about the song and Itunes download link is available here. Hera is one of the six finalists to compete in the Vina del Mar International Song Contest 2013 with this song.
