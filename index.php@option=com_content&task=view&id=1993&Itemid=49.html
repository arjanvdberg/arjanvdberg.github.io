Eurovision Song Contest with Radio International and on Oikotimes.TV

Eurovision Song Contest Special - Nicki French (United Kingdom 2000): The third hour of Radio International this week  is entirely devoted to Nicki French who represented the United Kingdom at the 2000 Eurovision Song Contest when it was staged in Stockholm's Globen Arena. The song "Don't play that song again" reached Number 16. On 17 July 2010, the Eurovision Club (EC) Germany celebrated their 10th anniversary and Nicki French was invited as special guest of honour. JP did an in-debt interview with the singer who is full of charme and fun. You will like to listen to this interview and listen to some of her other songs.   Click here to take you to the Third Hour of the show for this particular special on Radio International.
 
Eurovision Song Contest Interview - Jill Johnson (Sweden 1998):  Lilian, JP and Eric were in Stockholm for a huge Eurovision event where many Eurovision artists were interviewed. These interviews will be broadcast in the course of the coming weeks. But this week listen to an interview with Jill Johnson who back in 1998 represented her country Sweden at the Eurovision Song Contest with the song "Kärleken är (Love is)" reaching Number 10.

Euro Friend Song Contest 2010 - Start of HEAT 3:  Radio International in collaboration with the Euro Friends Song Contest 2011 announcing the start of HEAT 3 for the international juries to vote on the Face Book Page of the contest. On the Face Book Page of the Euro Friends Song Contest, you vote for your 10 favourite songs in the Eurovision Song Contest amongst the third hundred songs of Europe's biggest music competition.  Last week Radio International revealed the ten qualifiers of HEAT 2 and now HEAT 3 has started and is open to be voted on.  Next week Radio International and Andy from the Euro Friends Song Contest will reveal the ten qualifiers. during the show. Join to vote for your favourite Eurovision songs by clicking on this link which takes you to the Face Book Page of the Event. Remember, you have to be a member in order to join the voting.

Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - Austria to return to the Eurovision Song Contest:  Ross Bennett takes a look at some of the best entries and also at the history of Austria at the Eurovision Song Contest. After Austria's participation in 2007 the country pulled out and decided to return to the Eurovision Song Contest 2011. Welcome back Austria! Good to see you back!
