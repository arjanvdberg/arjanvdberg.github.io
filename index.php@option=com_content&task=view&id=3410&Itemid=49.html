Through 2014 with Eurovision Radio International - your music requests, comments and ideas:  Eurovision Radio International never stops and is keen on keeping the music of the Eurovision Song Contest in the ears of the many fans that join the show each and every week via the usual means either via PODCAST, LISTEN AGAIN, live broadcast or Live Stream. Interview with Eldar Gasimov (Azerbaijan 2011, Eurovision Winner):  Azerbaijan was represented at the Eurovision Song Contest 2011 by El & Nikki and won the competition with the song "Running scared" in Duesseldorf. Since then one more song emerged from both singers together but then El & Nikki have gone their own career paths. A few months ago David Mann met Nikki in London and interviewed her which was aired on Radio International. JP met El or Eldar Gasimov at the Eurovision Club Germany's annual convention last November in Cologne, Germany and interviewed the talented young singer, actor and presenter as we know he co-hosted the Eurovision Song Contest 2012 in Baku. Listen to this interview in this week's edition of Radio International but also hear some of Eldar's new material as well as some Azeri pop songs he performed during the show. You can watch this interview on our YouTube channel by clicking - here
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - The National Final Season 2014:  With a start of a new quarter that means it is also the start of a new feature on Eurovision Radio International. In the last quarter of 2013 Radio International went through the history of Denmark at the Eurovision Song Contest. And over the Christmas Break the national final season has officially begun. Like in previous years the Radio International Eurovision Experts will be assessing the National Finals 2014 live on Radio International. Ross Bennett starts off with the new season taking a look at the National Finals of Albania and the Ukraine. 
   
Eurovision UnderCover:  Last week, Radio International started a brand new series on the show featuring Eurovision Cover Versions where David Mann from Eurovision Norwich will be intoducing some of the finest Eurovision Song Contest entries covered by other singers. Just be surprised. You will be hearing the actual song that has been on the Eurovision stage followed by the cover. Enjoy the brand new series.  Listeners are encouraged to send in their cover versions they came across over the years to share with the other fans tuning into the show. Alternatively, listeners can send in their request for a particular Eurovision covered song using this link. David Mann's been digging deep in his treasure box for this week's Eurovision Cover song. Don't miss it!
 
New Song Releases by Eurovision Artists:  Radio International is always keen to know how the careers of Eurovision performers develop after their performance at the event. The show is in collaboration with many Music Companies that supply Radio International with the latest songs of their signed up artists to bring the muisc closer to the fans. On a weekly basis Radio International receives songs which are being featured on the show. 
 
 
 
Eurovision Song Contest News:  Luke Fisher joins the team in the studio every week to bring you the news and the latest developments connected to the Eurovision Song Contest. Whether it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc Luke will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International. He also takes a look at some statistics such as viewing figures in the various countries and more.
In addition, Eurovision News is available on www.eurovision.tv, escxtra.com, esctoday.com and escflashmalta.com.
