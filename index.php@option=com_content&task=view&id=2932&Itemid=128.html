Eurovision in Concert 2013 - 13 April 2013, Amsterdam, The Netherlands
For the fifth time in a row this great annual event is happening: EUROVISION IN CONCERT 2013 takes place in Amsterdam, The Netherlands with the participation of many Eurovision 2013 performers. A unique chance to meet the contestants already who will be competing at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 in Malmö, Sweden in May 14, 16 & 18.
 


Date:
13 April 2013, 1900 hours CET

Venue:
Melkweg The Max
Lijnbaansgracht 234 A
1017 PH Amsterdam
The Netherlands

Presenters of Eurovision in Concert 2013:
The show will be hosted by Marlayne  who represented the Netherlands at the Eurovision Song Contest 1999 with the song "One good reason" reaching Number 8 and Linda Wagenmakers represented the Netherlands at the Eurovision Song Contest 2000 with the song "No goodbyes" reaching Number 12.
 
SPECIAL GUEST:
And it has been confirmed that Eurovision Song Contest Winner from 1991, and three times participant at the contest for Sweden CAROLA will be Special Guest of the event.  Carola first represented Sweden in the Eurovision Song Contest 1983 reaching number 3 with "Främling". In 1991 she won the Eurovision Song Contest for Sweden with "Captured by a love storm" and in 2006 Carola returned to the contest reaching Number 5 with "Invincible". Your chance to meet Carola at Eurovision in Concert 2013.

Tickets: (SOLD OUT):
Tickets are priced at a approx EURO 30 (including booking fees) per person for the entire event. Please order and pay your ticket via the Official Website of the event,  Print it and bring it with you to the entrance on 13 April 2013.

Please make sure that you do not miss this great event which is organised and sponsored by OGAE Netherlands, Songfestival.web-log.nl, 12 points.tv, EBU-UER, PRINZ-ESC.DE, EUROSONG NEWS, OUT TV and EUROVISION ARTISTS and others.  
Happy Eurovisioning!
