Hightlights of the show

Interview with Krista Siegfrieds (Finland 2013): Finland was represented at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 by the bubbly Krista Siegfrids and the girls performing the song "Marry Me" which came 24th in Malmoe, Sweden. Taking part in the Eurovision Song Contest paved the way for Krista's media and singing career as she also has been hosting the Finish National Final, competing in Sweden's Melodifestivalen 2016 as well as releasing new material and been attending various national Eurovision Conventions. JP of Radio International met Krista again at the recent OGAE UK's EUROBASH in Manchester on 01 Oct 2016 and had the pleasure of a quick update from Krista regarding her singing career and new material.  Enjoy the chat on this week's edition of Radio International as well as listening to her new song "Be real" which was released on 02 November 2016. You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
  Interview with Sebastian Calleja (Winner of the OGAEvision Song Contest 2016, Malta): Big part of the Eurovision Weekend 2016 in Berlin was the first OGAEvision Song Contest which contained singers from 12 countries that competed for the trophy covering a Eurovision song. The quality was of very high standard and the contest was won by 18 year old Sebastian Calleja from sunny Malta who performed his cover of the Israeli Eurovision entry from 2015 by Nadav Guedj "Golden Boy". The prize was also that Sebastian will perform in front of 1700 plus audience on the Eurovision Cruise Helsiki to Tallin on 03 September 2016 organised by OGAE Finland. Radio International's JP also went to the Eurovision Cruise 2016 and caught up with Sebastian after his performance aboard the Baltic Queen. You can hear the interview on this week's edition of Radio International. You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2016: The 14th annual Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2016 is scheduled to take place on Sunday, 20 November 2016 at 1600 hours from the Mediterranean Conference Centre in Valetta, Malta when young artists from 17 countries will be singing their song in this pan-european music event. Detailed information is on the website of the event Junior Eurovision Song Contest or click here. Radio International always has been supporting this event and will also continue in 2016. Listen to some of the Junior Eurovision Highlights as well the currentJunior Eurovision songs on Radio International. The Team will be off to sunny Malta for the event to bring you interviews with the young artists. 
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight XXXL - Modern Talking and Dieter Bohlen's Involvement into the Eurovision Song Contest:  Marcus Keppel-Palmer from BBC Radio Bristol joined JP in the studio for a rather special edition of the Eurovision Spotlight taking a look at the music genius from Germany - Dieter Bohlen who was 50% of Modern Talking in the 80s and 90s but also produced and wrote many songs for other artists such as Bonnie Tyler, El Martino, Errol Brown of Hot Chocolate. Dieter Bohlen also wrote some entries for the Eurovision Song Contest for Germany as well for Austria. The entire third hour is devoted to the music of Dieter Bohlen and Marcus will be chatting about his experience meeting and working together with the one and only Dieter Bohlen.
Eurovision Under Cover:  This ever so popular feature on Radio International is David Mann's weekly edition of the Eurovision Cover Versions where David Mann from Eurovision Norwich will be intoducing some of the finest Eurovision Song Contest entries covered by other singers. Just be surprised. You will be hearing the actual song that has been on the Eurovision stage followed by the cover. Listeners are encouraged to send in their cover versions they came across over the years to share with the other fans tuning into the show. Alternatively, listeners can send in their request for a particular Eurovision covered song using this link.  This week there are two editions of the Eurovision Cover Spot since one of the biggest BBC Radio 2 broadcasters of our time passed away earlier this week in his 90s - Sir Jimmy Young. David Mann from Radio International's Studio 3 in London joins JP on the show for the Tribute Eurovision Cover Spot. 

Eurovision Song Contest News: Nathan Waddell joins the team in the studio every week to bring you the news and the latest developments connected to the Eurovision Song Contest such as the National Finals or if it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc Nathan will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International. He also takes a look at some statistics such as viewing figures in the various countries and more. Nathan is website editor with escxtra.com. In addition, Eurovision News is available on www.eurovision.tv, esctoday.com and escflashmalta.com (amongst others.
