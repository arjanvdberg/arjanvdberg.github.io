Hightlights of the show

Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2016: This week is a rather special edition of Radio International as members of the Team are in Malta - the Home of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2016. The 14th edition of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest will be staged in Mediterranean Conference Centre (MCC) on 20 November 2016, 1600 hours CET. Like in 2015 also this year 17 countries have confirmed participation in the music competition for children. Three countries withdrew who took part in 2015 namely: Montenegro, San Marino and Slovenia while we welcome back Cyprus, Israel and Poland.  The complete list of the 17 countries are: Albania, Armenia, Australia, Belarus, Bulgaria, Cyprus, FYRO Macedonia, Georgia, Ireland, Israel, Italy, Malta, Poland, Russia, Serbia, The Netherlands and Ukraine.

This year's theme song of the event is "#Embrace" sung by last year's Junior Eurovision Song Contest Winner for Malta - Destiny. Consult the official event's website Junior Eurovision Song Contest for all the latest news concerning the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2016 and also you will also find all detailed information about the young artists. JP and David of Radio International will be present at the event and conduct interviews with the young artists which you can see as video interviews which will be made available as soon as ready on this accompanying Eurovision Blog.

This week's show also comes from Malta's MCC with JP and David introducing all the 17 songs and give you background information on the artists but also on the event. In addition, JP and David take you down Memory Lane of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest by playing you some of the highlights of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest past and present. Make sure not to miss this particular radio show as JP and David will be chatting with you on the show's Facebook Group "Eurovision Radio International" from 2000 - 2300 hours CET answering all your questions regarding the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2016. In regards to the great TV Show, should you live in a country that is not taking part in the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2016 and the show is not televised via the national broadcaster then you can find the livestream on the official event's website Junior Eurovision Song Contest Complete details such as lists and Running Order of the 2016 contest you can find on the Wikipedia Page by clicking here. 

Good luck and a lot of fun to all the participants of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2016. 

The normal format of Radio International returns at the same time next week with all the normal show features.
