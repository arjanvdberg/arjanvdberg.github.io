Live Interview with Gianluca Bezzina (Malta 2013):  The last time Malta placed in the Top 10 of the Eurovision Song Contest Final was back in 2005 when Chiara's "Angel" came second. Dr. Gianluca Feelgood - Bezzina and his team managed to qualify Malta for the Grand Final from Semi Final 2 and then also ranked in the Top 10 at Number 8 with the happy-go-lucky song "Tomorrow" which just brings good mood and makes you feel good. Radio International is delighted to welcome Gianluca live on this week's edition of Eurovision Radio International. Listeners joining the show live are encourgaed to send in their questions to Gianluca to CONTACT and JP will then be reading the questions out to the artist. You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here  
 
Interview with Dina Garipova (Russia 2013):  Russia was represented at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 by Dina Garipova from Tartanistan with the song "What if" which reached Number 5 in the Final of the contest after a stunning performance of the team on stage. The Team of Radio International had the big pleasure to meet the artist for an indebt  interview in the Euroclub at the Meet and Greet.  You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here  
 
Interview with Eyþór Ingi Gunnlaugsson (Iceland 2013):  Iceland was represented at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 by Eyþór Ingi Gunnlaugsson from the volcanic island up north. The song is called "Ég Á Líf" and translates to "I have a life" which qualified Iceland for the Grand Final of the 59th Eurovision Song Contest and then it ranked at Number 17 with a total of 47 points. The Team of Radio International had the pleasure to meet the singer for a brief interview and some quick first reaction when he found out Iceland is qualified for the Grand Final. Listen to both of the interviews during this week's edition of the show.  You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here
