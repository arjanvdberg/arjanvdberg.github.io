Interview with Tom Dice (Belgium 2010):  OGAE Germany had their annual convention on 26 January 2013 in Munich, Germany and members of the Team of Radio International were also present to interview the artists and enjoy the performances of Eurovision veteran Sandra Reemer (Netherlands 1972, 1976, 1979 & 1983), Friderika Bayer (Hungary 1994) and Tom Dice who represented Belgium at the Eurovision Song Contest 2010 in Oslo. Tom successfully qualified his country for the Grand Final of that Eurovision Song Contest as he won his Semi Final. Then he achieved a remarkable 6th place in the Grand Final with the song "Me and my guitar". Tom Dice was one of the three star guests of the event in Munich. Find out all about Tom's career progression after his performance in Oslo till now as JP chats with the singer in the venue. You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here  
 
Eurovision Radio Shows in the UK: Interview with Joel Bennett from Oystermouth Radio, Wales:  Over the past few weeks Radio International discovered more radio stations that are broadcasting Eurovision radio programmes. One is called "Eurovision Domination" from Sine Fm in Doncaster, UK hosted by Jake and you can find out more about that particular show by clicking here and the other show which was discovered is "W12P" broadcast on Oystermouth Radio from Mumbles in Wales, United Kingdom. The host of the show is Joel Bennett and Radio International is delighted to chat with Joel during this week's edition of Radio international to find out more about this particuar radio show. Radio International as from now on makes these two shows also availble to the listeners on the website's Show Archive for PODCASTING. If you like to tune in live please find here the website of W12P. During the show this week Joel will be telling us all about his show. 
 
Interview with Deborah C (Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2013):  On Saturday, 02 February 2013 Malta selected their entry to the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 with a young medical doctor by the name of Gianluca Bezzina who will be the Ambassador of Malta in Malmoe waving the Maltese Flag and singing the song "Tomorrow" which won Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2013. The full result of the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2013 can be viewed here.
Continuing the series of interviews with Maltese music talents Radio International this week airs an interview with Deborah C who this year entered the competition with "Love-o-holic" ranking at Number 9 in the Final of the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2013. Deborah C also took part in the Malta Eurovision Song Contest in 2012 together with Leila James with the song "You Make Me Go Uh! Uh!" Listen to the interview which JP did with the Deborah C during the rehearsals. You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here

Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - The 2013 National Finals with Dermot Manning: The National Selection Season 2013 is over and all 39 songs that compete in the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 were chosen. The Eurovision Song Contest 2013 will be staged in Malmö, Sweden on the 14 May 2013 (Semi Final 1),  16 May 2013 (Semi Final 2) and 18 May 2013 (Grand Final) where the 39 countries will be competing for the trophy and the rights to hold the Eurovision Song Contest 2014. Dermot Manning takes a look at the betting odds of winning Eurovision 2013. Which ones are in the Top Five so far. Tune in to find out. 
 
 
Eurovision UnderCover:  Last week, Radio International started a brand new series on the show featuring Eurovision Cover Versions where JP will be intoducing some of the finest Eurovision Song Contest entries covered by other singers. Just be surprised. You will be hearing the actual song that has been on the Eurovision stage followed by the cover. Enjoy the brand new series.  Listeners are encouraged to send in their cover versions they came across over the years to share with the other fans tuning into the show. New Song Releases by Eurovision Artists:  Radio International is always keen to know how the careers of Eurovision performers develop after their performance at the event.
