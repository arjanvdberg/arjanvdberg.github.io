Interview with Linda Martin (Eurovision Winner 1992, Ireland & Ireland 1984):  RTE - the Irish national broadcaster engaged former Eurovision Winner Linda Martin to be part of the Irish Delegation and represent Ireland in all matters of media attention in support of Ryan Dolan - Ireland's hopeful for Eurovision 2013. Linda won the Eurovision Song Contest in 1992 in MALMOE, Sweden with the song "Why me" written by Johnny Logan who himself won Eurovision in 1980 and 1987. Before that Linda was the Runner-up of the Eurovision Song Contest 1984 with the song "Terminal 3". JP and Bert had the pleasure to meet Linda at the Press Centre after a live transmission of Swedish TV and conducted an interview with the great singer from Ireland. You can hear this interview on this week's edition of Radio International.  You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview 2 with Eyþór Ingi Gunnlaugsson (Iceland 2013):  Iceland was represented at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 by Eyþór Ingi Gunnlaugsson from the volcanic island up north. The song is called "Ég Á Líf" and translates to "I have a life" which qualified Iceland for the Grand Final of the 59th Eurovision Song Contest and then it ranked at Number 17 with a total of 47 points. JP had the pleasure to meet the singer for a brief interview and some quick first reaction when he found out Iceland is qualified for the Grand Final. Listen to the interview during this week's edition of the show.  You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview with Ilias of Koza Mostra (Greece 2013):  A couple of hours before the Grand Final kicked off JP of Radio International disguised as a German Cleaner dusting off the Greek Singer Ilias in the Press Centre. What was thought to be a little sketch ended up to be a brief interview with the Ilias, the lead singer of Koza Mostra who was checking his email for good luck wishes from Greece. Check out this fun interview with JP alias the German Cleaner in the Press Centre of Malmoe. Greece reached Numbe 6 in the Grand Final of the Eurovision with the song "Alcohol is free".   You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview with Krista Siegfrids (Finland 2013):  Our rowing reporter from Skandinavia Lilian Brunell (alias Nana Mouskouri) has been out and about and met Finland's hopefull and representative at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 at their hotel in Malmoe for an interview. Check out what the two ladies are up to by listening to the interview on this week's edition of Radio International. Krista Siegfrids represented Finland at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 and managed to qualify her country for the Grand Final where at the end of the show she reached Number 24. You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview with Margaret Berger (Norway 2013):  Norway's ambassador to the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 was Margaret Berger who represented the kingdom with the song "I feed you my love" which came 4th in the Grand Final. Simon from Polish Radio conducted a short interview with the Norwegian Hopeful and kindly submitted an interview with the singer for broadcast on this week's edition of Radio International. 
 
Interview with PeR (Latvia 2013):  Latvia was represented by PeR (Please Explain Rhytm) with a kind of Britpop sounding song called "Here we go" which kicked off Semi Final 2 of the Eurovision Song Contest 2013. Unfortunately, Latvia did not gain enough points in their Semi Final to qualify for the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest 2013. Disappointingly the song only reached Number 17 in their Semi Final, however, they are a great bunch of Party Boys and rocked the Euroclub. Radio International previously aired an interview with PeR courtesy of David Mann when he met the singers as the London Pre-View party. Now find out more about the singers by listening to a second interview done in Malmoe.  You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview with Andrius Pojavis (Lithuania 2013):  For the third time in a row Lithuania managed to qualify for the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest but this time with Andrius Pojavis and the song "Something" reaching Number 22 after the great success of 2012 when Donny Montell's "Love is blind" ranked 14th in the Final in Baku and Evelina Sasenko's "C'est ma vie" from 2011 which ranked at Number 19. Listen to the interview with singer done by the Radio International team at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013.  You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here
