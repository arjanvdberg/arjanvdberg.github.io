 Experience the Eurovision Song Contest with Radio International and Partners

2011 draws to a close and JP takes a last look back to the Junior Eurovision Song Contest. Then there will be another part in the series of Eurovision meeting South Africa and Eric Lehmann will be joining JP for the third hour to take a final look at the most and least successful song per country at the Eurovision Song Contest. 

(Hour 1 of the Show) Final Interviews from the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2011:  Already in the shows after the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2011 you could hear the interviews with the Winners Candy from Georgia and many others. IIn the first hour of the last show of 2011 hear the last interviews of the young performers who competed in Yerevan, Armenia. Listen to interviews with Ivan Ivanov from Bulgaria, Paulina Skrabyte from Lithuania, Kristall from Ukraine, Dorijan Dlaka from the FYRO Macedonia and Amanda Bashmakova from Latvia. For the final ranking of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2011 click here.   
 
(Hour 2 of the Show) Eurovision meets South Africa (Third Series, Part 4):  After the success of the first  and second series Radio International is delighted to present to you the next and third series. This is the third of five parts where Radio International broadcasts a series of shows where you can hear the enthusiasm of South African artists recording Eurovision Song Contest entries into Africaans. This truely is something very special and we wish to thank Roy van der Merve, Neil and Carel van Eden for contacting Radio International to make the Eurovision Fans aware of this ongoing project and the interest int he Eurovision Song Contest in South Africa.
