Eurovision Interview with Kit Rolfe alias Belle & the Devotions: (United Kingdom 1984 - Part 1):  Over a week ago the OGAE UK had their annual EUROBASH in Manchester with a very special guest that represented the United Kingdom at the 1984 Eurovision Song Contest: Belle & the Devotions. The band was fronted by Kit Rolfe who Radio International had a huge pleasure to meet and interview during the Eurobash recently. "Love Games" reached a remarkable 7th place at the 1984 Eurovision Song Contest from Luxembourgh. Listen  Part 1of the interview during this week's edition of Radio International. Part 2 will be broadcast during Radio International on 03 November 2010.
 
Christa Karu live on Radio International this week: In August 2009 a Eurovision Project came into being in Germany called "One World, One Dream" consisting of six young singers from five countries of Europe. Radio International was present at this Eurovision Workshop and has the big pleasure to welcome at long last one of the members of this project to the studios - Christa Karu who is the Estonian part of this Eurovision project.  Christa's songs have been heard various times already on Radio International. Christa also participated at the Home Composed Song Contest 2009 reaching Number 7 with "Stay" and also she is competing in the 2010 edition of the Home Composed Song Contest. Christa is live in the studio with the rest of the Team. Should you wish to find out more about Christa go and visit her website.
 
Euro Friend Song Contest 2010 - HEAT 8 - The Qualifiers:  Radio International in collaboration with the Euro Friends Song Contest announce during the show this week the ten qualifiers of HEAT 4 of what the international juries have been voting for on the Facebook page. On the Face Book Page of the Euro Friends Song Contest, one could vote for your ten favourite songs in the Eurovision Song Contest  amongst the eighth hundred songs of Europe's biggest music competition.  Two weeks ago Radio International revealed the ten qualifiers of HEAT 7 and now HEAT 8 is closed and the results will be announced during the show by Andy Simon, who is one of the prime organiser of Euro Friends Song Contest 2011.  Heat 9 will start very soon and you can join to vote for your favourite Eurovision songs by clicking on this link which takes you to the Face Book Page of the Event. Remember, you have to be a member in order to join the voting.   
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight: Germany. In view of the 2011 Eurovision Song Contest from Germany, Radio International's four regular Eurovision Experts will be joining us in turn week by week to review all the German entries of the Eurovision Song Contest from 1956 to 2010. This week Dermot Manning takes a look at the years 1970 to 1974.

Duesseldorf Quiz with Jutta.  To the run up of the 2011 Eurovision Song Contest staged in Germany's city of Duesseldorf and Radio International's own Jutta is originally from Duesseldorf we though to give you some homework in form of a weekly quiz on the next Eurovision Host City. Send your answers to CONTACT on our website not later than midnight the Tuesday following the live broadcast of Radio International. Here are the three questions of the Duesseldorf Quiz 1:

City:
How many districts does Duesseldorf have and in which one is the Esprit Arena located where the 2011 Eurovision Song Contest will be held next May 10, 12 & 14?

Sport:
What is the name of the local soccer team and what is their contribution to the Eurovision Song Contest 2011?

Food:
Next to Altbier, Coffee and tea what is the typical drink of the city of Duesseldorf?
