Hightlights of the show

Interview with Linda Martin (Eurovision Winner Ireland 1992):  At a recent edition of Eurovision Club Germany's Annual Convention JP from Radio International had the pleasure yet again to meet Ireland's Linda Martin who took part at the Eurovision Song Contest twice. The first time round was back in 1984 when she came second with the song "Terminal 3" and then in 1992 she won the Eurovision Song Contest with the song "Why me" which was written by Johnny Logan. Listen to Linda Martin in interview on this week's edition of Radio International.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Eurovision Champion Chart - Australia, Andorra, San Marino: The Eurovision Champion Chart is produced by Craig Mitchell Johnstone in conjunction with the Face Book Group called "Eurovision Champions". On selected Shows Eurovision Radio International produces various one hour shows with the music of the Eurovision Champion Chart as voted for by the fans of Eurovision. The Eurovision Champion this week runs down the entries from Australia, Andorra and San Marino to the Eurovision Song Contest in the order how the fans voted for the songs from these countries. What will top this particular Eurovision Chart - find out this week on the show.   
 
Eurovision Song Contest 2016 - A look back at the event in Stockholm with David Mann (Part 2 of 5):  Continuing our five part mini series this week reflecting the 61st Eurovision Song Contest in Stockholm, Sweden David Mann has been producing a flash back of the work the Team of Radio International did during the Eurovision week if it is Interviews, Reports, Press Conferences and of course the great music of Eurovision 2016. Don't miss any of this. This is part two and the following threeparts will be broadcast on the show each week. Enjoy this trip down Memory Lane of Eurovision 2016. 
  
Eurovision Song Contest News: Nathan Waddell usually joins the team in the studio every week to bring you the news and the latest developments connected to the Eurovision Song Contest such as the National Finals or if it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc Nathan will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International. He also takes a look at some statistics such as viewing figures in the various countries and more. Nathan is website editor with escxtra.com. In addition, Eurovision News is available on www.eurovision.tv, esctoday.com and escflashmalta.com (among others). Nathan rejoins the team in October.
