Through 2014 National Final Season with Eurovision Radio International - your music requests, comments and ideas:  Eurovision Radio International never stops and is keen on keeping the music of the Eurovision Song Contest in the ears of the many fans that join the show each and every week via the usual means either via PODCAST, LISTEN AGAIN, live broadcast or Live Stream. Interview with Ryan Dolan (Ireland 2013):  Eurovision Club Germany's annual convention took place last November in Cologne, Germany with the participation of Linda Martin (Ireland 1984 & 1992), Natalia Kelly (Austria 2013), Eldar Gasimov (Azerbaijan 2011) and last but not least Ryan Dolan as invited guests. Ryan Dolan respresented Ireland at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 with the song "Only love survives" which unfortunatley, only reached Number 26 in the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 in Malmoe, Sweden. JP met the young Irish singer during the sound check backstage for an indebt interview  which you can hear on this week's edition of the show.  You can watch this interview on our YouTube channel by clicking - here
 
Interview with Lindsay Dracass (United Kingdom 2001):   Back in 2001 the United Kingdom was represented at the Eurovision Song Contest by Lindsay D (whereas D stands for Dracass) and the song "No dream impossible" coming 15th in Copenhagen Parken Stadium. UK Eurovision fans are regularly organising EUROFEST in the Royal Vauxhall Tavern in London and the last event was on Friday, 17 January 2014 where Lindsay Dracass was invited to sing amongst others also her Eurovision entry. Debbie O'Hare from W12P was on location and had the pleasure to chat with Lindsay D about her Eurovision Experience and her career development after. Courtesy of W12P Radio International is extremely happy to air this interview during this week's edition of the show.
 
OGAE Germany's Annual Convention 2014 - Interview with Frank Albers:  OGAE Germany's Annual Convention is scheduled to take place on Saturday 25 January 2014 in Wirtshaus zum Isartal, Munich, Germany with the participation of The Herreys (Sweden 1984, Winners), Jan Johanson (Sweden 1995), Valentina Monetta (San Marino 2012, 2013 & 2014) and Malena Mortensen (Denmark 2002). Special Guest with be NDR's Dr. Thomas Schreiber, Head of German Delegation at the Eurovision Song Contest and member of the Reference Group. Frank Albers - the clubs PR Manager will be talking to Radio International live during the show to tell us all the details of this year's edition of the OGAE Germany Convention. In case you have not yet signed up but would like to take part in this event and meet the artists you still can register via the website of OGAE Germany by clicking here. 
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - The National Final Season 2014:  With a start of a new quarter that means it is also the start of a new feature on Eurovision Radio International. In the last quarter of 2013 Radio International went through the history of Denmark at the Eurovision Song Contest. And over the Christmas Break the national final season has officially begun. Like in previous years the Radio International Eurovision Experts will be assessing the National Finals 2014 live on Radio International. Dermot Manning starts off with the new season taking a look at the National Finals of Albania and the Ukraine. 
   
Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2014 - Your Votes Please: As every year Radio International supports the Malta Eurovision Song Contest in which the Maltese Population and an International Jury select the most suitable song to represent the Maltese Islands at the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 in Copenhagen come May. Like in 2013, pbs uses the same format where this year the national selection consists of a Semi Final with 20 songs that is narrowed down to 14 Finalists out of which the Maltese representative to the Eurovision Song Contest will be selected. Gianluca Bezzina won the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2013 with the song "Tomorrow" which also made it sucessfully to the Final of the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 in Malmoe, Sweden last May. Who will succeed the Doctor Gianluca?:) The Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2014 will be televised on Friday, 07 February 2014 (Semi Final) and Saturday, 08 February 2014 (Final) and will be available to watch online as well. escflashmalta.com will keep you up-to-date with the developments.
 
Your Votes are needed:  Radio International will be conducting a pre-voting amongst the fans, listeners and viewers of the weekly Eurovision radio show. Video clips of all the 20 songs are available online here (as a quick play list on YouTube and if you want the videos in full length please click here) and Radio International would like to involve the listeners and viewers to the weekly show to cast their votes in selecting their favourite ten songs of the 20 Semi Finalists in Eurovision Voting style (12, 10, 8, 7, 6, 5, 4, 3, 2 and 1 point) and submit their choice to Radio International by 29 January 2014, 1200 hours CET. For this please find the Voting Form here on MS EXCEL. Alternatively, send your ranked favourite Top 10 songs to Radio International via this the CONTACTS link. 
 
The result of the voting will be made public on Radio International on 29 January 2014 edition of the show.  We then can compare the result of the the listeners with with what the Maltese population and the international Jury have selected. We look forward to receiving many inputs and thank you for your participation in this voting. We apologize a rather short voting window.
