Radio International goes...
Radio International goes....was born in February 2008 in addition to our regular Radio International radio programme. Radio International goes is a more music based programme featuring Pop and Rock Music Programmes, Specials on events, Artist Profiles, Reviews of the 80s, 70s, 60s, Your Requests and interviews with performers at the Eurovision Song Contest. Also trips down Memory Lane or featuring the profiles of famous artists. Please CONTACT US if you wish to hear a certain format, artist or music and we will be glad to serve you.

Happy Listening
The Team of Radio International



Radio International goes to the San Remo Song Festival (Part 4)
This week Radio International goes once again to the San Remo Song Festival as we continue the series with Part 4.

The programme takes a look at two famous Italians (e.g. Maurizio Fabrizio and Marco Falagiani) who conducted the orchestra during the Eurovision Song Contest and have also played an important part in the San Remo Festival both as composers and producers.

 
Furthermore, you will hear songs of other famous San Remo participants during the years.

Radio International goes to the ZEROs - 2002 & 2003
Welcome to the second edition of this brand new series on Radio International as we go to re-visit the CRAZY ZEROs or NAUGHTIES as they are also known as. JP is joined by Michael Goodrich in bringing you a pick of the best songs that represented their countries at the Eurovision Song Contest during the last 10 years.
 

In this week's edition we take a look at the year 2002 which was staged in Tallin's Saku Suurhaal in Estonia and then the 2003 Eurovision Song Contest came to us from the Skonto Hall in Riga, Latvia. Enjoy listening to this Flashback in Eurovision history with the special stories around these two song contests.
