Interview with Firelight (Malta 2014):  Malta is a very traditional country when it comes to the Eurovision Song Contest and the island is crazy about the contest and named it's national selection process "Malta Eurovision Song Contest" which this year was won by Firelight with the song "Coming Home". Malta qualified for the Grand Final from Semi Final 2 and ranked at Number 23 at the end of the Eurovision Song Contest 2013. Radio International has interviewed Firelight a number of times but this week listen to Eric chatting with the group in the Press Centre of the Eurovision Song Contest 2014.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
 
Interview with The Shin & Mariko (Georgia 2014): The Team of Radio International had the pleasure to meet the entire Team of Georgia for an interview in the Press Lounge of the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 - The Shin & Mariko who represented Georgia with the song "Three Minutes to Earth" which unfortunately, did not qualify for the Grand Final from Semi Final 2 where it ranked at Number 15. The Shin actually lives in Germany for many years - that is what JP found out during the interview amngst other things. Listen to the interview on this week's edition of Radio International. You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
 
Interview with Dilara Kazimova (Azerbaijan 2014):  Azerbaijan won the Eurovision Song Contest 2011 with El & Nikki with "Running scared", in 2012 Sabina Babayeva ranked 4th with "When the music dies" and then another amazing entry in 2013 when young Farid Mammadov performed "Hold me" coming second in Malmoe. This year the choice of Azerbaijan fell on Dilara Kazimova who performed "Start a Fire" which ranked 21st in the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest 2014. The Team of Radio International had the pleasure to meet the singer in the Press Centre in Copenhagen just after the Press Conference. Listen to this interview on this week's edition of the show.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
 
Interview with Cristina Scarlat (Moldova 2014):  Moldova was represented at the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 by Cristina Scarlat performing "Wild Soul" which unfortunately ranked last in Semi Final 1 and with this also did not qualify for the Grand Final. JP met the singer in the Press Lounge for an interview you can hear on this week's edition of Radio International.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
 
Interview with Axel Hirsoux (Belgium 2014): A huge fan of the Eurovision Song Contest and member of the Belgian Jury, a dream came true for Axel Hirsoux when he won EUROSONG 2014 which is the Belgian National Final almost coming to the standard of Sweden's Melodifestivalen. Axel's dream was to sing at the Eurovision Song Contest which he managed to do, although only reached the Semi Final 1 with the song "Mother". After his Press Conference in Copenhagen JP had the pleasure to chat with the Belgian Ambassador to the Eurovision Song Contest 2014. Hear it on this week's edition of the show. Axel will be special guest on the annual Eurovision Club Germany Convention in Cologne, Germany on 22 November 2014. Details.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
 
Interview with Can-Linn & Kasey Smith (Ireland 2014): The Irish Nation was represented by group Can-Linn feat. Kasey Smith with the song "Heartbeat" which unfortunately did not qualify for the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contes 2014 and ranked in Semi Final 2 at Number 12 when only the Top Ten advance into the Grand Final. The Team of Radio International met the gang for an interview in the Press Lounge of the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 right after the Irish Press Conference. Listen to the interview on this week's edition of the show.   You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 

 
Eurovision Song Contest News:  Nathan Waddell joins the team in the studio every week to bring you the news and the latest developments connected to the Eurovision Song Contest. Whether it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc Nathan will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International. He also takes a look at some statistics such as viewing figures in the various countries and more. Nathan is website editor with escxtra.com. In addition, Eurovision News is available on www.eurovision.tv, esctoday.com and escflashmalta.com (amongst others). Due to the summer holiday schedule Nathan returns on 08  October 2014 when the show reverts back to normal.
