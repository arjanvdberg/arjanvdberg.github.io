Home Composed Song Contest 2012 - Your Votes Please
The Home Composed Song Contest 2012 is on the way. 22 songs have been submitted and Radio International form one Jury assembling the votes for the contest being added to the other 22 juries for the contest.

Please find below a link you can download the 22 songs and an MS Excel table which lists all the 22 songs.
 
 
 
Click here to download the songs. 

(please note that the link will expire on 31 Oct 2012)

Listen to the songs and find out your favourite 10 songs which you need to rank from your most favourite to your least favourite song (e.g. the song you like most is Number 1 and song you like least is Number 10). Email your result to CONTACT and we then will combine your result with the result of the other voters and have then the result of the Radio International Jury. That result will then be combined with the 22 other juries of the Home Composed Song Contest 2012.
 
Deadline:
Please note that we need your votes not later than 26 October 2012 after which we will set our ancient calculator in motion and feed it with your votes. 

Further details about the contest are available here - http://www.hcsc2011.eu/

We hope you will all be participating in this and am curious what will be the overall result.
