Eurovision Song Contest with Radio International and on Oikotimes.TV
Eurovision Interview Filipa Azevedo (Portugal 2010):  The Eurovision Party in Setubal took place on 11 September 2010 under open sky with the participation of many national and international Eurovision performers. JP went to this event and brought back some interviews which Radio International will be airing within the next weeks. Filipa Azevedo will start of this series of interviews this week. Filipa represented Portugal at the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest reaching Number 18 with "Ha dias assim" (It's one of those days). 
 
New Release of Malta's Fabrizio Faniello.  On the day of broadcast, Radio International received from Fabrizio Faniello's record company CAP SOUND the brand new song entitled "My heart is aksing you". The song is played on Radio International and CAP SOUND also offered a prize for the successful Quiz Winner: A signed copy of the new single CD of Fabrizio Faniello. The Quiz consists of two questions the answers of which you need to send to Radio International email INBOX by Tuesday, 12 October 2010, 23:59 hours. The questions: 1. How many times did Fabrizio represent Malta at the Eurovision Song Contest - 1, 2 or 3 times? 2. What is the name of Fabrizio's latest song?
 
Euro Friend Song Contest 2010 - Recap of the Qualifiers of HEAT 5 & 6 and start of HEAT 7:  Radio International in collaboration with the Euro Friends Song Contest 2011 announcing the qualifiers of HEAT 5 and HEAT 6 which is a reflection of what the international juries voted for on the Face Book Page of the contest. Due to the Holiday Schedule of Radio International there was no announcement of the Qualifiers on the show which this week will be done as Andy Simon who is one of the prime organisers will be joining us twice on this week's show.  In the meantime HEAT 7 has started and on the Face Book Page of the Euro Friends Song Contest, you can vote for your 10 favourite songs in the Eurovision Song Contest amongst the seventh hundred songs of Europe's biggest music competition.  Join to vote for your favourite Eurovision songs by clicking on this link which takes you to the Face Book Page of the Event. Remember, you have to be a member in order to join the voting.   
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight: Germany. In view of the 2011 Eurovision Song Contest from Germany, Radio International's four regular Eurovision Experts will be joining us in turn week by week to review all the German entries of the Eurovision Song Contest from 1956 to 2010. This week Alasdair Rendall starts with the first lot of songs from 1956 to 1959. 


Eurovision Song Contest News:  Eurovision News. Luke Fisher joins the team in the studio every week to bring you the news and the latest developments connected to the Eurovision Song Contest. Whether it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc Luke will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International. Also check out the occasation appearance of Zoran Kovacic from Serbia on Radio International bringing us some Eurovision News in his style.
In addition, Eurovision News is available on www.eurovision.tv, oikotimes.com, esctime.com, escdaily.com, esctoday.com and escflashmalta.com.
