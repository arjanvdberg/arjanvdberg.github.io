Hightlights of the show

The Eurovision Champion Chart 2017 - Top 50:  With Christmas just being over and now we are heading to the brand new year the final show of 2017 is a special show as well. Tune in to The Eurovision Champion Chart of 2017 with JP from Studio 4 in Germany. The Eurovision Champion Chart is compiled by Craig Mitchell-Johnstone in Cairnes, Australia from the votes of the many Eurovision fans via the Facebook Page Eurovision Champion. Radio International this week plays the Top 50 Eurovision Tunes of all time as being the final show of 2017. The usual format returns again on 10 January 2018 when the show comes again from Studio 1 in the Netherlands. You are cordially invited to this week's special chart edition of Radio International with lots and lots of your favourite Eurovision music.  
 
A Happy New Year:  The Team of Radio International would also take this opportunity to wish our listeners and faithful fans and their families, the Eurovision performers and the Eurovision Family a peaceful, healthy and prosperous Happy New Year 2018 with the hope of a better year than 2017 was for many. Radio International will continue also in 2018 with 52 more editions of the show going into the show's 20th Anniversary next November. On the first show of 2018 on 03 January 2018 Radio International will be there with your Eurovision and New Year's Hangover Cure. The more music edition of the show to ease you into 2018 smoothly. A Happy New Year!
