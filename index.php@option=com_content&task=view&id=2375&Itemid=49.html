Live Interview with Love City Groove (United Kingdom 1995):  Only very recently, the United Kingdom's entry to the 1995 Eurovision Song Contest - Love City Groove - reunited to continue their career. Love City Groove reached Number 10 in the 1995 Eurovision Song Contest with their song "Love City Groove" receiving a total of 76 points and with this a joint Number 10 with Malta's Mike Spiteri. The song also was a chart success in the British Charts reaching Number 7 back in 1995. This week Radio International is extremely delighted to welcome to the show two of the founder members of Love City Groove - Stephen Rudden & Paul Hardy. 
 
Interview with Geir Roenning (Finland 2005):  Radio International's rowing NORDIC reporter Lilian Brunell has been to Finland - actually to spend her holidays there and like last week she bumped into Paradise Oskar and this week she crossed the way of Geir Roenning who is Norwegian from decent but represented Finland at the 2005 Eurovision Song Contest with the song "Why". The song received 50 points ranking it at Number 18 in the 2005 Semi Final. At that time there was only one Semi Final with 25 songs. Listen to Lilian chatting with Geir about his past, present and future career during this week's edition of Radio International.
