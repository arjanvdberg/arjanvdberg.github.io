Pink Nation Eurovision Exclusive 2010 - Antwerp, Belgium (30 April 2010)

After the first successful edition in 2009 All-Entertainment & Bram Bierkens organise the 2010 edition of the Pink Nation Exclusive on Friday, 30 April 2010 in Antwerp, Belgium.

The following national representatives of the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest will be performing their entry to this years event in Oslo:

  • Armenia - Eva Rivas - Apricot Stone
  • Belgium - Tom Dice - Me and my guitar
  • Bosnia & Herzegovina - Vukasin Brajic - Thunder and Lightning
  • Greece - Giorgios Alkaios - Opa
  • Iceland - Hera Börk - Je ne sais quoi  (Participation has been cancelled due to the volcano in Iceland)
  • Ireland - Niamh Kavanagh - It's for you
  • Malta - Thea Garrett - My Dream
  • Serbia - Milan Stankovic - Ovo Je Balkan

Antwerp Engineering Company (AEC) will be the location of the Pink Nation Exclusive 2010. More information how to get there is available on the official website of Pink Nation.
