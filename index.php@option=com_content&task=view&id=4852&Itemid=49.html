Hightlights of the show

The Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2017 with Eurovision Radio International live from Malta:  This week's show comes from Malta in view of the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2017 where the Maltese public wll be selecting the act that will be representing Malta at the Eurovision Song Contest 2017 in Kiev, Ukraine come May. To get a bit of Maltese spirit going you will not only hear the 16 songs that will be competing in the show but also some of the highlights of the Maltese National Finals during the years. Also the famous medley of all the Maltese Eurovision entries will feature in the show of course. Join JP and David in the studio in Malta for the special edition of the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2016 show. You can find the list of the participants here. The normal format of Eurovision Radio International will return next week from the studio in the Netherlands and bringing you interviews from this national final - The Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2017.

Eurovision Song Contest News:  escXtra.com's Nathan Waddell normally joins the team in the studio every week to bring you the news and the latest developments connected to the Eurovision Song Contest. Whether it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc.  Nathan will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International but not this week. For the latest developments in the Eurovision World consult escXtra.com or any of the following sites: www.eurovision.tv, escxtra.com, esctoday.com and escflashmalta.com and many others.
