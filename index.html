Welcome to Eurovision Radio International
Eurovision Radio International is a fast moving, entertaining three hour radio programme offering up-to-date information and entertainment about the Eurovision Song Contest all year round. The show contains interviews with past and present Eurovision performers, News about the Eurovision Song Contest, the Eurovision Quiz, Expert opinions and of course the Listeners' Choices as well. 
 
How to Listen: This weekly radio show is geared for Expatriates, English speaking internationals and especially to the fans of the Eurovision Song Contest, broadcast from the studios of RTV Katwijk in the Netherlands on 106.8FM & Digital Cable Channel Number 916 and via the Internet. (Click to listen for the live radio player). You can also find Radio International (RTV Katwijk) on TuneIn. Simply click on the icon and you should be able to hear the station's stream on the times of live broadcast on Wednesday from 2000 - 2300 hours CET. In addition to the means of FM radio and webradio, Radio International will also be simulcast on the Radio International TV channel (where you can see how we actually do Radio International). You can reach the Livestream channel by clicking here while listening to the weekly edition of Radio International or some previous interviews Radio International has done with Eurovision performers. Radio International would like to encourage you to actively take part in the chat during the show, making more Eurovision Friends and send in your requests and greetings.

Live on Wednesdays 2000 - 2300 hours CET


Normally, the simulcast is from 2000 – 2300 hours CET when Eurovision fans can join in and chat amongst each other via the Chat Room and request their favourite Eurovision entry. Radio International - The Ultimate Eurovision Experience, is extremely happy that the show is also aired on the following radio stations: 

  • RTV Katwijk - The Home Station: Live on Wednesdays from 8-11pmCET
  • Radio International Livestream Channel
  • Radio International's Mix Cloud Channel 
  • Malta's Galaxy 105 Network:  Sundays 1930 - 2230 hours CET
  • TUNEIN: At time of broadcast on Wednesdays from 2000 - 2300 hours CET
  • Twitter / Email: Contact the studio during the live broadcast on Wednesday via Twitter: @RadioInt or via EMAIL
Should you not be able to listen live or missed any of the show fear not as all the shows are available as "Listen Again" in the Show Archive or you can subscribe to the PODCAST. For this check out the Show Archive.  But also Eurovision Radio International is available as download from Itunes.

The Eurovision Radio International Blog which has been accompanying the current website will be the main webpage with PODCAST and audio files availability until further notice. So you won't miss your favourite shows in case you cannot listen live to Radio International.
