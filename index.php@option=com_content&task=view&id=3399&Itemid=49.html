Interview with Herciana Matmuja (Albania 2014): During the Festive Season 2013-2014 Eurovision has not stood still and two national finals were broadcast being the one of Ukraine and the one of Albania in Festival i Kenge. Salman Tanzeem has been on location during the festival in Albania and witnessed the two Semi Finals and the Final. The Winner of the Festival i Kenges 2014 is Herciana Matmuja who not only won the contest in Albania but with this also the ticket to Copenhagen to represent Albania at the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 with the song "Zemërimi i një nate (One nights anger)". After the show Salman had the chance and the pleasure to interview Herciana and Radio International is pleased to air this interview during this week's edition of the show. Thank you Salman! :)
 
Interview with Natalia Kelly (Austria 2013):  Malmoe in Sweden was the host city of the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 after the victory of Loreen taking the Eurovision Song Contest from Baku to Sweden. 39 countries competet for the trophy of the contest and the right to host the show the following year. Austria was represented by Natalia Kelly who reached Number 14 in Semi Final 1 and with this failed to qualify for the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest 2013. The song is called "Shine".However, Natalia left an impression to the members of Eurovision Club Germany who at the end of November 2013 invited her to attend their annual convention for a concert. JP from Radio International was in Cologne for this event and had the pleasure to interview Natalia to find out more about her music career. Listen to this interview in this first regular edition of Radio International in 2014. You can watch this interview on our YouTube channel by clicking - here
 
Interview with Katya Ryabova (Junior Eurovision Russia 2009 & 2011): Once more Radio International takes you into the world of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013 which was held on 30 November 2013 in Kiev, Ukraine with Malta winning it. Like at the adult Eurovision Song Contest previous performers return to support their country's entry. This was also the case for Katya or Ekaterina Ryabova who represented Russia twice at the Junior Eurovision Song Contest. In 2009 it was with "Malenkiy prints" (The little Prince) which came the runner up, second and then in 2011 in Yerevan, Armenia she reached Number 4 with the song "Kak Romeo i Dzhulyetta" (Like Romeo & Juliette). JP had the big pleasure to meet her again for a little interview which was helped with by Michael Komadowski doing the translation. Listen to it on this week's edition of the show. You can watch this interview on our YouTube channel by clicking - here
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - The National Final Season 2014:  With a start of a new quarter that means it is also the start of a new feature on Eurovision Radio International. In the last quarter of 2013 Radio International went through the history of Denmark at the Eurovision Song Contest. And over the Christmas Break the national final season has officially begun. Like in previous years the Radio International Eurovision Experts will be assessing the National Finals 2014 live on Radio International. Ross Bennett starts off with the new season taking a look at the National Finals of Albania and the Ukraine. 
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight XL - The Semi Final entries that should have qualified for the Grand Final:  Returning to the airwaves this week and that in a longer version than the usual slot is Marcus Keppel-Palmer who will be joining JP for the third hour of this week's edition of the show to take a look at some of the great Eurovision entries that actually should have qualified for the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest. Due to the success of this feature and listeners request Bert Ouwerkerk from Utrecht in the Netherlands selected ten songs he thinks should have qualified for the Grand Final. Marcus and JP chat about those entries and of course also play'em. :) Radio International has received already some submissions from listeners and they will be featured in the weeks to come on the show, however, if you would like to submit your choice of Semi Final entries that should have made the Grand Final please send your Top Ten list of songs to CONTACT and let us know why you select each song.
