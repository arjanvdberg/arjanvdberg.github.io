 Hightlights of the Show

Eurovision Radio International Special from Wednesday, 08 March 2017:  In view of Melodifestivalen which is Sweden's national music competition to find the best song of the year. The winner of this competition will be use to represent Sweden at the Eurovision Song Contest. 28 acts went through four Semi Finals and a Second Chance round before getting into the Grand Final of Melodifestivalen 2017 which again is held at the Friends Arena in Solna (Near Stockholm) on Saturday, 11 March 2017 with a live audience of almost 30000 spectators. In 2016, Frans won Melodifestivalen 2016 with the song "If I were sorry" which came 5th in the Eurovision Song Contest 2016. Eurovision Radio International - The Ultimate Eurovision Experience is present at the location and conducted interviews with all the performers plus more. May Sweden and the International Jury select the best song to represent Sweden at the Eurovision Song Contest 2017 in Kiev on 09 May 2017 in Semi Final 1.

To get the fans into the mood for Melodifestivalen 2017, on Europe's Ultimate Eurovision Experience - Radio International this week JP plays some of the highlights of Melodifestivalen from recent years. And in the third hour you will hear all the songs that will be competing in Melodifestivalen 2017 of which the winner will represent Sweden at Eurovision 2017 in Kiev, Ukraine. Click here for the results and the details of Melodifestivalen 2017 Grand Final.
