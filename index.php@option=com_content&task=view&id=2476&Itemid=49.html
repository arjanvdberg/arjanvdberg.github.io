Interview with Anabela (Portugal 1993): On 29 October 2011, OGAE Luxembourgh held their 5th annual EUROGALA in the legendary Grand Theatre of Luxembourg City where the Eurovision Song Contest 1973 and 1984 was staged. This year's edition of the EUROGALA saw the participation of three former Eurovision performers. Luxembourg's Marion Welter, Portugal's Anabella and Spain's D'Nash. As earlier announced you will hear the interviews during the weekly shows of Radio International. The interview with D'Nash was already aired a couple of weeks ago. This week Radio International airs the remaining two interviews of this event. Anabela represented Portugal at the 1993 Eurovision Song Contest with the song "A cidade ate ser dia" which reached Number 10 for Portugal when that particular Eurovision Song Contest came from Ireland. Enjoy?

Interview with Marion Welter (Luxembourg 1992):  The other artist that appeared on the OGAE Luxembough EUROGALA 2011 was on home ground and came 21st with the song "Sou frai" for Luxembourg - Marion Welter. A very charming lady who nowadays works as Radio producer and voice over artist. But more information of course will be available during the interview Radio International did with Marion Welter. To find out more about Marion you can visit her website which is available here.

Second Cherry Song Contest 2011 - The Result:   This annual music event takes took place in a London Pub where fans vote for the songs that were in the National Finals of various countries but did not win the ticket to the Eurovision Song Contest 2011, however, are big fan favourites. Some members of the Team of Radio International were present on the last edition of the event on 12 November 2011. A very exciting voting too place with the result.... listen to the show to find out which Country, Song and Artist have won the Second Cherry Song Contest 2011.   
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - Eurovision's Runner Up with Dermot Manning:  The series continuous with our Eurovision specialists to take a chronological look at the songs that came second in the Eurovision Song Contest during the years from 1956 to now whereas 1956 had no known ranking. Ally joins the team in the studio this week to reflect the years 1987 to 1990. 
 
Euro Friends Song Contest 2012 with Andy Simon: he next season of the Euro Friends Song Contest is under way in search for the ultimate Eurovision song as voted for by the many fans around the world.  Andy  Simon joins us every week to reveal the qualifiers for the next round.  The website of the Euro Friends Song Contest you will find here where you can easily vote for your favourite Eurovision song per country and also see The Euro-Friends  TV Show as hosted by Andy Simon. You can also join the Facebook Group here. This week Andy looks at the next 12 qualifiers from Heats 4 - 15. 
  
Eurovision Song Contest News:  Luke Fisher joins the team in the studio every week to bring you the news and the latest developments connected to the Eurovision Song Contest. Whether it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc Luke will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International.
