Show 23 June 2010
Listen to the Show:       Hour 1     |    Hour 2     |   Hour 3     |    Play List
   
Eurovision Song Contest with Radio International and Oikotimes.   
 
Eurovision Song Contest 2010 - Sharing the Moment:  For the last time in the current series Radio International assesses the Eurovision Song Contest 2010 with our regular Eurovision Experts and of course play your favourites throughout the show. Also there will be interviews to come with the Eurovision performers. This week Alasdair Rendall tells us how he has seen the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest and he will be giving us his assessment of this year's edition. Something new to come in our next regular edition of Radio International on 07 July 2010.
 
Just remember that next week (30 June 2010) Radio International will be starting two hours later and only lasting for two hours - so 2200 - 2400 hours and Radio International will be broadcasting an Anne-Marie David Special.
 
Recently added!
Eurovision 2010 Interview with Tom Dice (Belgium, 2010): During this week's show Radio International received a phone call from Kristof de Smet who attended the Tom Dice Concert in Amsterdam's Paradiso and thought to try to get Tom to talk to us. Kristof successfully could get Tom Dice on the phone for us for an interview. This was not planned this week but a great chance and initiative by one of our faithful listener. A huge THANK YOU Kristof. Listen to Tom's current and future plans and a little bit of how he perceived the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest from his point of view.
 
Eurovision 2010 Interview with Josh Dubovie (United Kingdom, 2010): Radio International already met Josh during "Eurovision in Concert "which was held in April 2010 near Amsterdam, however, JP met the singer again in Oslo at the Welcome Reception of the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest and did an interview. Josh represented the United Kingdom at the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest with the song "That sounds good to me". Listen to this interview during this week's edition of Radio International.
 
Eurovision 2010 Interview Sunstroke Project with Olia Tira (Moldova 2010): Moldova's entry is called "Run away" and was performed by the energizing Sunstroke Project & Olia Tira.  The song reached Number 22 in the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest. Eric Lehmann and JP met the crazy bunch in Oslo and interviewed them which you can during this weeks edition of Radio International.  
 
Eurovision 2010 Interview Malcolm Lincoln (Estonia 2010): Malcolm Lincoln & Man Power 4 represented Estonia at the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest with the song "Siren"which unfortunately did not qualify for the Grand Final of the contest. Estonia reached 14th in Semi Final 1 when only the Top Ten countries advance into the Grand Final. You can hear an interview with the band during this week's edition of Radio International.
