All Request Happy Easter:  And how to celebrate Easter on Eurovision Radio International? Well easy - JP gathers all the many requests that listeners submitted to Radio International over the past weeks. These are the requests for which was no time for during the regular shows of Radio International. And to put things right and what better way to celebrate Easter with your favourite Eurovision music than listening to Radio International this week. It is a rather pure music show this week as most of the Radio International Team has taken this week off to recharge their batteries for the upcoming Eurovision Song Contest and pre-view events. So no Eurovision News, Interviews and Eurovision Spotlight this week. Also David Mann takes a week break with his special slot of Eurovision Covers. All that returns next week as Radio International reverts back to the normal format on 10 April 2013 with Malta's Scar, Portugals Filipa Souza & Gerson Santos and Rene Romkes from Eurovision in Concert 2013 . Don't miss it!    

Eurovision Song Contest News:  Luke Fisher joins the team in the studio every week to bring you the news and the latest developments connected to the Eurovision Song Contest. Whether it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc Luke will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International. He also takes a look at some statistics such as viewing figures in the various countries and more. However this week, he will also be on his well-earned break and returns next week. In the meantime keep yourselves updated with visiting the Eurovision News websites listed below. 
In addition, Eurovision News is available on www.eurovision.tv, escxtra.com, esctoday.com and escflashmalta.com.   
 

Eurovision News Website: escXtra.com:  Luke Fisher is the  Editor - in - Chief of the newly created Eurovision News website called escXtra.com. Check our more and EXTRA news on the Eurovision Song Contest by visiting the website here.
