Eurovision Song Contest 2014 - Radio International on location in Copenhagen - a recap:  The Team of Radio International while at the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 produced lots of material in terms of interviews with the Eurovision 2014 performers plus some specials which you will hear on Eurovision Radio International during the next weeks curing your Post Eurovision Depression as much as possible. A special thanks go to the team members on location working hard to get this coverage to you. A pile of photos and videos are on our Facebook and YouTube Channel. But here is the link to the Photo Albums where you can enjoy the photos from two weeks of Copenhagen and Eurovision Song Contest 2014. Album 1   |  Album 2    |   Album 3  |   Album 4. A detailed listing of the voting results of the two Semi Finals and Grand Final is available here.   
 

Interview with Jan Johansen (Sweden 1995):  The summer month is always a good chance to catch up with the many interviews the team of Radio International do throughout the year and this week there is another big nugget which is going to be aired on the show. It is an interview with Jan Johansen who represented Sweden at the Eurovision Song Contest 1995. He became the winner of Melodifestivalen 1995 and with this the ticket to represent Sweden at the Eurovision Song Contest with the song "Se paa mej" - with Jan Johansen. Sweden came third with a total of 100 points. OGAE Germany invited Jan for their 2014 Fan Club Convention where JP met up with the singer for an indebt interview which was done in Munich earlier on this year. Listen to this interview on this week's edition of Radio International. You can watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - Eurovision Songwriters - This week:  Andre Popp & Pierre Cour:   The Eurovision Song Contest 2014 is over and after Eurovision is before Eurovision. Many countries will have Eurovision events throughout the summer into the autumn to bridge the Post Eurovision Depression. Also Radio International does not stand still and delivers in the weekly three hour show lots of items to make you forget the PED. In the current series the Eurovision Experts of Radio International take a look at the songwriters in Eurovision Song Contest. Continuing this week is Chris Poppe with taking a look at the works of Andre Popp & Pierre Cour and the nice songs they have added to the ears of the many Eurovision fans.
 
 
Malta Summer Hit Song Contest 2014:  On 06 August 2014 edition of Radio International Deo Grech from Malta One Radio talked about the Malta Summer Hit Song Contest 2014 which is now in the preparation stages where the 18 competing songs are being introduced on Malta's F-Living TV channel. Courtesy of Marc Calleja-Bayliss from escflashmalta.com Radio International will introduce the songs on the radio every week. The Grand Final of the contest will be in September and by that time all songs will have been aired on Radio International. 
 

Eurovision UnderCover:  Last week, Radio International started a brand new series on the show featuring Eurovision Cover Versions where David Mann from Eurovision Norwich will be intoducing some of the finest Eurovision Song Contest entries covered by other singers. Just be surprised. You will be hearing the actual song that has been on the Eurovision stage followed by the cover. Enjoy the brand new series.  Listeners are encouraged to send in their cover versions they came across over the years to share with the other fans tuning into the show. Alternatively, listeners can send in their request for a particular Eurovision covered song using this link. David Mann's been digging deep in his treasure box for this week's Eurovision Cover song. Don't miss it!
 
 
New Song Releases by Eurovision Artists:  Radio International is always keen to know how the careers of Eurovision performers develop after their performance at the event. The show is in collaboration with many Music Companies that supply Radio International with the latest songs of their signed up artists to bring the muisc closer to the fans. On a weekly basis Radio International receives songs which are being featured on the show. This week the new single by Thea Saliba from Malta, Dima Bilan alias Alien 24 from Russia.
 
 

Eurovision Song Contest News:  Nathan Waddell fell ill this week and Eurovision Ireland's Editor in Chief Garrett Mulhall stood in and did a fantastic job in delivering this week's Eurovision News. Nathan hopefully is better next week and joins the team in the studio to bring you the news and the latest developments connected to the Eurovision Songesc-n Contest. Whether it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc Nathan will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International. He also takes a look at some statistics such as viewing figures in the various countries and more. Nathan is website editor with escxtra.com. In addition, Eurovision News is available on www.eurovision.tv, esctoday.com and escflashmalta.com (amongst others)
