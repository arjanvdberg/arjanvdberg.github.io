Interview with the Winners of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2011 - Candy from Georgia: On Saturday 03 December 2011 the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2011 was staged in the Armenian Capital Yerevan with the participation of 13 countries competing for the trophy and the title. The Winners once again was Georgia and it is their second victory after 2008. The act is a five peace group called Candy performing the song "Candy Music". Check out the official Scoreboard here and the official website here. JP had the big pleasure to interview the girls before and after their victory. Enjoy this particular interview :)

Interview with the Runner Up of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2011 - Rachel from The Netherlands: The Netherlands almost won the Junior Song Contest 2011 with Rachel and the song called "Teenager". Just five points difference to the Winner Georgia. The Netherlands already won the 2009 Junior Eurovision Song Contest with Ralf Mackenbach and "Click Clack". The Netherlands will host the 2012 edition of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest. Listen to the interview with Rachel during this week's edition of Radio International.

Interview with the Number Three of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2011 - Lidiya from Belarus: The highest ranking ballade during this year's Junior Eurovision Song Contest is the entry of Belarus by Lidiya Zablotskaya called "Angely dobra" (Angel of Goodness). Listen to the interview during the show. 

Interview with the Number Four of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2011 - Katya Ryabova from Russia: The opener of this year's Junior Eurovision Song Contest was Russia's Katya Ryabova who returned to the contest for a second time with the song"Kak Romeo i Dzhulyetta" (Like Romeo & Juliet) ended up fourth. Listen to the interview during the show.

Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - Eurovision's Runner Up with Dermot Manning and Ross Bennett:  The series continuous with our Eurovision specialists to take a chronological look at the songs that came second in the Eurovision Song Contest during the years from 1956 to now whereas 1956 had no known ranking. Ross joins the team in the studio this week to reflect the years 1996 to 1999 and Dermot Manning to check out the years 2000 - 2003. 

Euro Friends Song Contest 2012 with Andy Simon: The next season of the Euro Friends Song Contest is under way in search for the ultimate Eurovision song as voted for by the many fans around the world.  Andy  Simon joins us every week to reveal the qualifiers for the next round.  The website of the Euro Friends Song Contest you will find here where you can easily vote for your favourite Eurovision song per country and also see The Euro-Friends  TV Show as hosted by Andy Simon. You can also join the Facebook Group here. This week Andy looks at the next qualifiers from Heats 19 - 22.
