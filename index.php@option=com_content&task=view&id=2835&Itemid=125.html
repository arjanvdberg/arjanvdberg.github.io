OGAE UK's Eurobash 2012 - A look back at an amzing event
For the many fans that would have loved to be there but could not and for those who were present a nice memory of the event below a little summary of the EUROBASH 2012. A list of links to the videos follows the text below. Enjoy!

On Saturday, 27 October 2012, OGAE UK held their annual EUROBASH in Manchester's Chanellor's Hotel with the participation of Scott Fitzgerald who represented the UK at the Eurovision Song Contest 1988 with the song "Go" coming second by ONE point to Celine Dion who represented and won for Switzerland with the song "Ne partez pas sans moi". Also special guest at EUROBASH was Malta's Glen Vella who represented his country at the Eurovision Song Contest 2011 in Duesseldorf, Germany with the song "One Life".

The many members of OGAE UK and international guests arrived and registered at noon for the event started with Meet and Greet. After the President's Welcome Speech the convention continued with the Quiz presented by Ben Robertson and Alasdair Rendall. This was followed by the Battle of the Bash which was won by Yohanna from Iceland with "Nott".

After a small break for refreshments the Guest List continued where Marcus Keppel-Palmer interviewed Scott Fitzgerald, Glen Vella and Jamie.

Time for Dinner and then the Highlight of the Event - EUROSTARS. 12 Acts performed their favourite Eurovision songs and during the votes were counted the "interval acts" performed on stage a little concert consisting of Glen Vella and Scott Fitzgerald.  Both of course their entry to the Eurovision Song Contest as mentioned above.

After the concert or Interval Act - the Top 5 of the EUROSTARS were called on stage announced by Jamie which was then narrowed down and Glen Vella announced the Top 3 and the Winner was announced by Scott Fitzgerald.

And the Winner of EUROSTARS 2012 is: Vikki Spence as Hera Bjork singing "Je ne sais quoi" (Iceland 2010) Congratulations!
 
After the Winner's Reprise the famous EURODISCO by DJ Dave Simmons took us to the end of the 2012 edition of OGAE UK's Eurobash 2012.
