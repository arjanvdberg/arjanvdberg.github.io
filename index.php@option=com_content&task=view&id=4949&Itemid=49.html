   Experience the Eurovision Song Contest with Radio International

Radio International and Livestream TV with Chat Room: Radio International is aired on their TV channel live every Wednesday where fans can join and see how we actually do Radio International and listen to the show while chatting with the other Eurovision fans joining the community.  Enjoy and tell your friends to join and be part of the Eurovision Radio International Community! Also become part of the Eurovision Radio International Facebook Group which accompanies the show and is available here. It contains lots of Eurovision News from the major Eurovision News Websites and also announcements. 

Please note that many interviews we air are also available as video interview which can be viewed on our Radio International YouTube Channel. Just take a look and enjoy also see the artists talking to us. 

The Eurovision Song Contest with Eurovision Radio International - your music requests, comments and ideas: Eurovision Radio International never stops and is keen on keeping the music and news of the Eurovision Song Contest in the ears of the many fans that join the show each and every week via the usual means either on FM, Cable and also via PODCAST, Itunes, LISTEN AGAIN, live broadcast or Live Stream. You can also find Radio International (RTV Katwijk) on TuneIn. Please feel free to submit your comments, ideas and requests of Eurovision songs and Radio International is delighted to play your requests during the shows. Hightlights of the show

The Eurovision Song Contest 2017:  The Team of Radio International is on location in Kyiv, Ukraine from the 62nd Eurovision Song Contest with 42 countries' artists competing for the trophe - the Chrystal Microphone - and the right to host Eurovision 2018. In this Show Archive Page you find a summary of radio programmes produced while in Kyiv with lots of inserts from the Press Conferences and interviews with the artists that took part at the Eurovision Song Contest 2017. Congratulations to Portugal who won the Eurovision Song Contest 2017 with Salvador Sobral being the lead artist on the performance, however, his sister Luisa Sobral is the genius behind the winning song of Eurovision 2017 entitled "Amar pelos dois (Love for both of us)" and performed the winning reprise together with Salvador at the end of the show.The entire results of the this year's Eurovisison Song Contest can be viewed here - RESULTS OF THE EUROVISION SONG CONTEST 2017
 
Interviews with the 2017 Eurovision Eurostars: In the runup to the Eurovision Song Contest Team Members of Radio International went to the various preview events such as Riga, London and Amsterdam meeting the European Music Ambassadors and conducting interviews with the artists. Listen to the interviews on the show of 03 May 2017.

Interview with Getty Caspers of Teach Inn (Eurovision Winner 1975, Netherlands):  Eurovision In Concert 2015 too place in April 2015 with many of the Eurovision artists of 2015 as well as previous special guests - the Bobbysocks whose interview Radio International aired last week. The evening before the concert was a meet and greet for the fans that had already arrived in Amsterdam. Special guest there was amongst others Getty Kaspers of Teach-In who still holds the last Dutch victory at the Eurovision Song Contest with "Ding-a-dong" in 1975.  The Team of Radio International had the big pleasure of interviewing Getty which you can hear during this week's edition of Radio International.
