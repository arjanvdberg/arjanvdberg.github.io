Experience the Eurovision Song Contest with Radio International and Partners.

(Christmas Eve, 24 December 2014) There is no stopping JP being ready to broadcast the next two special editions of Eurovision Radio International. The Team of Radio International invites you to the weekly Ultimative Eurovision Experience. Apart from the means of FM Radio in the Netherlands on FM 106.8 and the audio stream from the studio via www.radiointernational.tv and on Digital Cable 781 in the Netherlands, the show will also be web-televised via the Radio International TV Channel from the studio and linking up the many international Eurovision fans and friends and play your requests and read your messages out on air. Tell your friends to join in - SPREAD THE NEWS. 

Radio International TV Channel with Chat Room: Radio International has its own Livestream TV Channel where fans can join and see how we actually do Radio International and listen to the show while chatting with the other Eurovision fans joining the community.  But also you can watch there a re-run of the weekly edition of Radio International. Enjoy and tell your friends to join and be part of the Eurovision Radio International Community! 

Season's Greetings - The Radio International Eurovision Christmas Card 2014 - The Top 50 Christmas Chart linked with Eurovision: from the Team of Radio International who invites you to the weekly Ultimative Eurovision Experience and this week to the Radio International Christmas Card. With everyone taking off on their well-earned Christmas Break the Team of Radio International is at your service with probably the finest edition of Eurovision Radio International during 2014. Join JP in the studio on FM, Digital Cable, Internet, Livestream and PODCAST, basically the usual means and enjoy three hours of this special Christmas Card from Radio International. Many Eurovision performers will make an appearance during this show. In fact, The Christmas Top 50 Chart Rundown is aired this time. Wherever, JP finds a Eurovision performer singing the charting Christmas songs it will be aired.

David Mann's Radio International Christmas Card 2014:  All our regular correspondents are off on their Christmas Break apart from Sir David Mann in Studio 3 in London who delivers his Christmas Card to you in Royal British Style. The Eurovision News that develops during the Festive Season can be found at the regular Eurovision News Websites listed below.
 
(New Year's Eve, 31 December 2014) Radio International's Eurovision Hits In Da Mix: Eurovision Radio International's last edition of 2014 will be next week on Wednesday, 31 December 2014 which is another special edition with lots of music. In fact it is Eurovision Hits In Da Mix including a good review of 2014 mixed together back to back and beat to beat with just a little interruption for the top of the hour break. Enjoy three hours of this to take you out of 2014 as this will also be the last show on RTV Katwijk of 2014. On New Year's Eve the show starts at 2100 - 2358 hours CET. But first enjoy Christmas 2014! 

Eurovision Song Contest News:  Nathan Waddell joins the team in the studio every week to bring you the news and the latest developments connected to the Eurovision Song Contest. Whether it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc Luke will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International. However, this week the Team of Radio International takes a couple of weeks off for the Festive Season. Nathan is back on 14 January 2015 for the next and first Eurovision News Bulletin of the Eurovision New 2015.
In the meantime, Eurovision News is available on www.eurovision.tv, escxtra.com, esctoday.com and escflashmalta.com, etc
