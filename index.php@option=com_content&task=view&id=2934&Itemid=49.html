Interview with SINPLUS (Switzerland 2012):  During the Swiss National Final 2013 which was held in Kreuzlingen, Switzerland on 15 December 2012, Radio International had the pleasure to once again meet and interview the brothers Gabriel and Ivan from SINPLUS who represented Switzerland at the Eurovision Song Contest 2012 in Baku, Azerbaijan. Their entry "Unbreakable", unfortunately, only reached Number 11 in the Semi Final failing to qualify Switzerland for the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest 2012. In Kreuzlingen, SINPLUS handed over the responsibility to represent Switzerland at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 to the Heilsarmee or Salvation Army who won the national final 2013. (However, the name of the band and stage outfit has to be changed.) Listen to the interview during this week's edition of the show to find out about how the career of SINPLUS will continue.  Alternatively,  you can watch the video version of the interview by clicking here.
 
OGAE Germany's Annual Convention 2013 - Interview with Frank Albers:  OGAE Germany's Annual Convention is scheduled to take place on Saturday 26 January 2013 in Wirtshaus zum Isartal, Munich, Germany with the participation of Tom Dice (Belgium 2010), Pasha Parfeny (Moldova 2012), Friderika Bayer (Hungary (1994) and Sandra Reemer (Netherlands 1972 & 1976) and guest of honour - Germany's Mr. Eurovision - Ralph Siegel. Frank Albers - the clubs PR Manager will be talking to Radio International live during the show to tell us all the details of this year's edition of the OGAE Germany Convention.  
 
Interview with Natalia Barbu (Moldova 2007):  In September 2012, OGAE Portugal organised their third Eurovision Live Concert together in and with the municipality of Setubal, Portugal just outside of Lisabon with the participation of many Portuguese artists and International Eurovision performers including Katrina of Katrina and the Waves (United Kingdom 1997), Niamh Kavanagh (Ireland 1993 & 2010) and more such as Natalia Barbu who represented Moldova at the Eurovision Song Contest 2007 with the song "Fight" reaching Number 10. The Team of Radio International had the pleasure to meet and interview the singer in the dressing room during the soundcheck of the event. 
 
Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2013 - Can we have your votes please:  As every year Radio International supports the Malta Eurovision Song Contest in which the Maltese Population and an International Jury select the most suitable song to represent the Maltese Islands at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 in Malmö, Sweden come May. Like in 2012, pbs uses the same format where the national selection consist of a Semi Final with 24 artists that is narrowed down to 16 Finalists out of which the Maltese representative to the Eurovision Song Contest will be selected. Kurt Calleja won the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2012 with the song "This is the night" which also made it sucessfully to the Final of the Eurovision Song Contest 2012 in Baku last May. Who will succeed Kurt? The Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2013 will be televised on Friday, 01 February 2013 (Semi Final) and Saturday, 02 February 2013 (Final) and will be available to watch online as well. escflashmalta.com will keep you up-to-date with the developments.  Your Votes are needed:  Radio International will be conducting a pre-voting amongst the fans, listeners and viewers of the weekly Eurovision radio show. Video clips of all the 24 songs are available online here and Radio International would like to involve the listeners and viewers to the weekly show to cast their votes in selecting their favourite ten songs of the 24 Semi Finalists in Eurovision Voting style (12, 10, 8, 7, 6, 5, 4, 3, 2 and 1 point) and submit their choice to Radio International by 01 February 2013. For this please find the Voting Form here on MS EXCEL. Alternatively, send your ranked favourite Top 10 songs to Radio International via this the CONTACTS link. Full details can be seen here.  
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - The 2013 National Finals with Dermot Manning: A brand new year has started and the Eurovision World is already in full swing of selecting their entry to the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 that will be staged in Malmoe, Sweden on the 14 May 2013 (Semi Final 1),  16 May 2013 (Semi Final 2) and 18 May 2013 (Grand Final) where so far 39 countries will be competing for the trophy and the rights to hold the Eurovision Song Contest 2014. As every year Radio International reviews the National Finals with the regular correspondents and this week it is the turn of Dermot Manning who will be taking a look at the selection process of Lithuania and Switzerland. Find a full listing of the upcoming National Finals here.
