Show 09 February 2011 - Malta Eurosong 2011
Friday, 11 February 2011 24 songs will be competing for a place in the Final to take place on Saturday 12 February 2011. It is the Malta Preselection to the Eurovision Song Contest 2011. The Team of Radio International is on location to do interviews and clips for viewers of oikotimes.com and listeners to Radio International.
 
To get you already into the mood for the Semi Final on Friday, it is vital that you listen to this weeks very special edition of Radio International and hear all the 24 songs that are competing for the ticket to Duesseldorf. Also there will be some other highlights during the show. Since the team is on location we cannot take any requests during the show as usual. The normal format of the show returns next week.

For the list of the songs appearing in the order click on Play List below.

May the best song win and enjoy the show with all the 24 contestants and some former Maltese Eurovision nuggets.
