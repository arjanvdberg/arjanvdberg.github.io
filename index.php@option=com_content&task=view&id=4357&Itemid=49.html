Hightlights of the show

Tribute to Sir Terry Wogan (BBC TV Eurovision Commentator):  Sad news hit the Eurovision World and the people in the United Kingdom as on Sunday morning it was announced that one of if not the best broadcaster of our time Sir Terry Wogan had passed away suffering from cancer for a short period of time. He died on 31 January 2016 surrounded by his family. Terry Wogan was the BBC TV's commentator for many years before Graham Norton took over the job. Sir Terry Wogan also hosted many television shows as well radio shows on the BBC such as WAKE UP TO WOGAN or WEEKEND WOGAN. BBC Radio 2 and BBC TV have been broadcasting tribute shows to remember the great broadcaster Sir Terry Wogan. Also Radio International will be paying tribute to Sir Terry Wogan during this week's edition of Radio International produced by David Mann. 

Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2016:  The Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2016 is the national selection of Malta to select their entry to the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 to take place in Stockholm, Sweden come May. On Friday 22 January 2016 20 acts competed for the Top 14 place to advance to the Final which was held on Saturday, 23 January 2016. The Winner of the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2016 is Ira Losco selected by six judges and the Maltese Public by Televoting. The event took place in the legendary Mediterrranean Conference Centre in Valetta, the same venue where Glen Vella won with "One Life" in 2011 and Debbie Scerri with "Let me fly" in 1997 and many others too. The show was organised and broadcast by Malta's Public Broadcasting Services - pbs and and webcast also on eurovision.tv where you still can watch the show in case you missed it. You can find a summary of the production work done by the Team of Radio International on location - by clicking here.  
    
Interviews with Ira Losco (Malta 2002 & 2016 and Winner of the Malta Eurovision Song Contest): The Winner of the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2016 is Ira Losco as selected artist to represent Malta at the Eurovision Song Contest 2016. She won the national final with the song "Chameleon". What needs to be noted though is that the rules of the competition state that the national final is an artist selection and the song can still be changed. Ira already represented Malta at the Eurovision Song Contest 2002 with the song "7th Wonder" coming second in Tallinn, Estonia. In 2014 she hosted the Malta Eurovision Song Contest together with the previous year's winner Gianluca Bezzina. Right after this event the Malta Music Awards took place and Ira Losco was awarded three Malta Music Awards for 2013. The Team of Radio International were in Malta for the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2016 and had the pleasure to meet Ira Losco and chatted with her. You can hear this interview on this week's edition of the show as well as an interview done after the qualified from the Semi Final as well as a clip from the Red Carpet on the night of the national final and then after the Winner's Press Conference. Hear it all on this week's edition of Radio International as the team is back from sunny Malta. With this interview Radio International continues the story from last week and you can hear an interview done with Ira Losco after the Winner's Press Conference and at the Red Carpet that preceeded the Final of the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2016. After the Winner's Press Conference David Mann chatted with the mother of Ira Losco - Margaret which you can hear on this week's edition of Radio International.
 
Interview with Christabelle (Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2016, Number 4): A very energetic song once again performed by Christabelle who became the runner up at the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2015 when "Rush" came second. In 2014 she also took part with the song "Lovetricity" which came 8th. In the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2016 Christabelle reached Number 4 with the song "Kingdom" receiving an enormous applause in the hall right after her performance. You can hear the interview with the singer from Malta on this week's edition of Radio International. Enjoy! You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - The National Final Season 2016: The National Selection Process of the songs that will represent their country at the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 is well under way. In the last quarter of 2015 Radio International went through the best picks of Sweden's National Final - The Melodifestivalen where our Eurovision Experts selected what they thought is the best songs per year. Sweden will host the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 in the Globen Arena of Stockholm next May with the Grand Final on the 14 May 2016 and the the Semi Finals on 10 & 12 May 2016, respectively. Like in previous years the Radio International Eurovision Experts will be assessing the National Finals 2016 live on Radio International. Ross Bennett continues this week with the new season taking a look at the National Finals for the Eurovision Song Contest 2016.
