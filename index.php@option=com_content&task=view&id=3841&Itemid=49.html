Interview with the Winner of the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 - Conchita Wurst (Austria 2014):  Going out with a BANG! of 2014's regular editions of Radio International something special this week. At the recent Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2015 the current reigning champion of the Eurovision Song Contest  2014 - Conchita Wurst was special guest at the event of course singing "Rise like a Phoenix" which received a total of 290 points during the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 which was enough for the victory. But also performed her new song "Heroes". In 2012, Conchita already tried her luck in the national Final of Austria to get the ticket to represent Austria in Baku. But she came only second and the ticket went to the Trackshittaz instead. Courtesy of Garret Mulhall from Eurovision Ireland Radio International is able to air the interview that Garrett did with the bearded lady in the Press Centre of the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2015.  Hear it all on this week's edition of Radio International.
 
MESC 2015 - Interview with Glen Vella and Kevin Borg (Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2015): The Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2015 took place on 21 & 22 November 2014 from the Marsa Ship Yard in Malta with 20 songs entering into the Semi Final then narrowed down to a Top 14 and then on Saturday the winner was the public favourite as well as the jury favourite - Amber Bondin will be representing Malta at the Eurovision Song Contest with the song "Warrior" written by Elton Zarb and Matt Muxu, who also were responsible for the victory of Gaia Cauchi at the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013 with the song "The Start". Continuing on this week's edition of Radio International listen to the interviews with the contestants and this week it is the turn of a fantastic singer from Malta and a great personality, someone tht has been before representing Malta at the Eurovision Song Contest in 2011 with the song "One Life" which reached Number 11 in his Semi Final with just one point missing to get into that Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest - Glen Vella.  Glen decided to return to the competition in Malta and teamed up with singer and songwriter Kevin Borg who wrote the song "Breakaway" for the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2015 to be performed by Glen Vella. An amazing performance with Malta's Southville Dancers and Hula Hoop Acrobat Francis Perreault flown in from Canada to spice up the visual aspects of Glen's entry that successfully qualified for the Final of the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2015 and then came third in the Final after Christabelle's "Rush" and Amber's "Warrior"  Listen to this funny interview on this week's edition of Radio International.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2014 - Interview with Fedrica Falzon (Malta): The Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2014 took place in Malta on 15 November 2014 with debutant Italy winning the event. The song "Tu primo grande amore" was performed so passionately by Vinzenco on stage and won the event with 159 points on the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2014 stage in the Marsa Ship Yard. Following in the footsteps of Gaia Gauchi who won the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013 for Malta in Kiev with the song "The Start" - is Federica Falzon. Having the same vocal teacher as Gaia - Gillian Attard, Federica achieved a remarkable 4th place in the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2014 representing Malta with the song "Diamonds" and blew everybody away during her live performance. Radio International chatted with the young Mezzo Soprano singer at the Welcome Reception of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2014.  Listen to the interview with Federica on this week's edition of Radio International.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2014 - Interview with Josie (Croatia): Coming back to the Junior Eurovision Song Contest this year was Croatia who sent in a very talented young singer with American decent. Josie performed the song "Game over" which unfortunately ended up at the anchor position of Number 16 in the competition. But a very interesting person to speak to with great views. Listen to the interview with Josie on this week's edition of Radio International and get to know more about the singer.   You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2014 - Interview with Julia (The Netherlands ): At the recent edition of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2014 in Malta's Marsa Ship Yard The Netherlands were represented by Julia with the song "Around" which started as last in the contest and then at then end of the competition it came 8th. Radio International had the pleasure to chat with Julia during the rehearsal week when she came to the Press Centre for her Press Conference. Find out more details about Julia in the interview which you can listen to on this week's edition of Radio International.   You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here

Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - Austria in Eurovision:  Continuing this week when Radio International takes you right to the National Selections to the Eurovision Song Contest 2015. Austria initially won the Eurovision Song Contest in 1966 for the very first time with Udo Juergens singing "Merci Cherie" and then the second victory came in 2014 with Conchita Wurst and "Rise like a Phoenix" and with this will be hosting the 2015 edition of the Eurovision Song Contest. As in previous years the Eurovision Spotlight has reflected on the songs of the next host country and this year is no exception. The Radio International Eurovision Experts will be taking a look at Austria's history at the Eurovision Song Contest. The series continues this week with Chris Poppe in the last of the regular editions of Radio International. This feature continues on 14 January 2015. Enjoy the flashback into Austria's entries to the Eurovision Song Contest.
