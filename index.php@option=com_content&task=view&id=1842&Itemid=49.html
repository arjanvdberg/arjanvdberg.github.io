Show 07 April 2010
Listen to the Show:       Hour 1     |    Hour 2     |   Hour 3     |    Play List
 
Eurovision Song Contest with Radio International and Oikotimes.
 
Eurovision Interview with Michael von der Heide (Switzerland 2010): Radio International is delighted to welcome Michael von der Heide who will be representing his country Switzerland at the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest in Oslo with the song "Il pleut de l'or" which will be starting in Semi Final 2 at Number 5. In 1999 he participated in the German national final where he became 5th with the song "Bye Bye Bar". In 2009 he was in the Swiss Jury. 
 
Eurovision Interview with Kuunkuiskaajat (Finland 2010): Courtesy OGAE Finland, Radio International has the pleasure to also have live on the show the Finish representatives to the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest  - Kuunkuiskaajat who with "Työlki Ellää" will be in Semi Final 1 starting from Number 5.  
 
Eurovision Interview with Thea Garrett (Malta 2010): With the friendly help of escmalta.com, Radio International is delighted to welcome to the airwaves young Thea Garrett who will be representing Malta at the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest with the song "My Dream". Thea will be in Semi Final 1 as song Number 11.  
 
Eurovision Interview with Charlie McGettigan (Ireland 1994): In addition to the above mentioned Eurovision performers that Radio International is happy to have on the show for interview within the three hours on this week's edition - the team is also delighted to have the pleasure to speak to 50% of the winning duo of the 1994 Eurovision Song Contest - Charlie McGettigan who together with Paul Harrington won the 1994 Eurovision Song Contest  for Ireland with the great  "Rock'n Roll Kids". Charlie is part of the Expert Panel engaged by escmalta.com to review the Eurovision 2010 entries. He also hosts a Saturday mid-morning radio show in Ireland which you also pick up on the internet. Click here to join him from 1100 - 1300 hours CET.


Eurovision Song Contest 2010 - Share the Moment - Profiles:  All 39 countries have selected their entry to the 55th Eurovision Song Contest which takes place on 25 & 27 May 2010 for  the Semi Finals and the Grand Final on 29 May 2010. In the second editon of the new series this week Radio International takes a weekly look at the countries' entries profiles where you learn more about the performer and the song.  Alasdair Rendall will be continuing with the next lot of countries:   Bulgaria, Croatia, Cyprus, Denmark, Estonia, Finland and France. This way Radio International will bring you closer the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest entries so you are already a bit familiar with the songs when you see them in the Semi Finals and Final. You can also take a look at the videos of the songs available here
 
Radio International Eurovision 2010 Predictions:  Also starting this week each team member of Radio International  will each week be giving us their prediction which countries they think will be ending up in the Top 10 of the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest. Once the 2010 Eurovision Song  Contest Winner has been announced we can see whose prediction is closest. This week Alasdair Rendall's prediction for the Top 10 of the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest looks like this:  10th Ireland, 9th Israel, 8th Croatia, 7th, Azerbaijan, 6th Germany, 5th Greece, 4th Romania, 3rd Turkey, 2nd Norway, 1st France. 
 
Eurovision in Concert 2010: Radio International welcomes in this weeks show Rene Romkes who is one of the key organisers of this huge Eurovision Promotion Event to take place on 24 April 2010 near Zaandam north of Amsterdam. Rene joined us to tell us about the organisation and the ever growing guest list. All the details are available here.
