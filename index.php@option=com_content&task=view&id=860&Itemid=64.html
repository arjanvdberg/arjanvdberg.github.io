Eurovision 2008 Interview with Paolo Meneguzzi (Switzerland)(Part 2)

Radio International already had the pleasure in interviewing Paolo Meneguzzi from Switzerland previously live during our show in April. You can still hear this interview on our website by clicking here.

Then, while in Belgrade, Serbia,  Malcolm Whitehouse and JP of Radio International met Paolo Meneguzzi again for a follow-on interview. Listen to Paolo talking to us about his experience with international music festivals, new album release and his other passion - playing football. 

Unfortunately, Switzerland did not qualify for the Grand Final of the 53rd Eurovision Song Contest. Paolo Meneguzzi performed "Era Stupendo" in the second Semi Final reaching Number 13 with 45 points. However, as a special tread listen to a special JP-Mix of "Era stupendo - We're amazing". Enjoy.
