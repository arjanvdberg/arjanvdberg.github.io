Eurovision Song Contest 2013 - Radio International Pre-Voting: The Results
Thank you to the many fans, listeners and viewers of Radio International who took part in our annual Pre-Voting of the the songs that are competing at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013. Radio International asked the fans to send in their favourite Top 20 of the 39 Eurovision 2013 entries.  At this point Radio International would like to thank Robert Wahlstroem from Gothenburg in Sweden for his active participation in counting all the votes that came in over the past few weeks. Please find below the result published on Radio International's 01 May 2013 of the show.
