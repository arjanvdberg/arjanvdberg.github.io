Show 25 December 2013 - The Christmas Card

Experience the Eurovision Song Contest with Radio International and Partners.

There is no stopping JP being ready to broadcast the next and special edition of Eurovision Radio International. The Team of Radio International invites you to the weekly Ultimative Eurovision Experience. Apart from the means of FM Radio in the Netherlands on FM 106.8 and the audio stream from the studio via www.radiointernational.tv and on Digital Cable 781 & cable 88.1Mhz in the Netherlands, the show will also be web-televised via the Radio International TV Channel from the studio and linking up the many international Eurovision fans and friends and play your requests and read your messages out on air. Tell your friends to join in - SPREAD THE NEWS. The simulcast will be from 2000 – 2300 hours CET when Eurovision fans can join in and chat amongst each other via the Chat Room and request their favourite Eurovision entry. 

Radio International TV Channel with Chat Room: Radio International launched its own Livestream TV Channel where fans can join and see how we actually do Radio International and listen to the show while chatting with the other Eurovision fans joining the community.  But also you can watch there a re-run of the weekly edition of Radio International. Enjoy and tell your friends to join and be part of the Eurovision Radio International Community! 

The Radio International Eurovision Hangover Cure: New Year's Day is always a tough day after all the festivities of ringing out the old and ringing in the new year. So the Team of Radio International thought of a special show to smoothen your awakening and invites you to the weekly Ultimative Eurovision Experience and this week to the Radio International Hangover Cure. With everyone taking off on their well-earned Christmas Break the Team of Radio International is at your service with probably the finest edition of Eurovision Radio International during 2013. Join JP in the studio on FM, Digital Cable, Internet, Livestream and PODCAST, basically the usual means and enjoy three hours of this special Christmas Card from Radio International. Many Eurovision performers will make an appearance during this show singing their favourite Christmas songs spiced with Christmas Comedy spots, and Eurovision entries that have a touch of Christmas in them. Make sure not to miss this special edition on Wednesday, 01 January 2014 from 2000 - 2300 hours CET which will also be the first edition of Eurovision Radio International in 2014.
All our regular correspondents are still off on their Christmas Break as well so there are no special features as such - just YOU, the MUSIC and JP in the hot seat presenting you a nice mix of Eurovision music and Eurovision related music and of course the listeners' requests. The Eurovision News that develops during the Festive Season can be found at the regular Eurovision News Websites listed below. The normal format of Eurovision Radio International returs next week with all back to normality. For what is in store check out this website come Monday. 

Radio International would also like to take this opportunity to wish our faithful listeners, viewers and fans a A Happy New Year and we are looking forward to 2014 when Eurovision Radio International is going strong and also to 52 more great editions of Eurovision Radio International in 2014. Also a special thanks goes to the remote team members of Radio International that have been working hard to deliver input to the show including interviews, ideas. editing, features and expertise. Thank you. 


Eurovision Song Contest News:  Luke Fisher joins the team in the studio every week to bring you the news and the latest developments connected to the Eurovision Song Contest. Whether it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc Luke will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International. However, this week the Team of Radio International takes a couple of weeks off for the Festive Season. Luke is back on 08 January 2014 for the next and firsy Bulletin of the Eurovision New 2014.
In the meantime, Eurovision News is available on www.eurovision.tv, escxtra.com, esctoday.com and escflashmalta.com, etc
