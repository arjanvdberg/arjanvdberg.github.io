Let's step into Christmas 2013 with Radio International:  Eurovision Radio International never stops and is keen on keeping the music of the Eurovision Song Contest in the ears of the many fans that join the show each and every week via the usual means either via PODCAST, LISTEN AGAIN, live broadcast or Live Stream. Interview with Emmelie de Forest (Eurovision Winner 2013, Denmark):  Being a special guest during the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013 - Emmelie de Forest from Denmark flew in to Kiev to perform her Winning Song "Only Teardrops" as part of the Interval Act in the show. Just before Emmelie kindly gave a Press Conference where the 12 young contestants asked questions to the reigning chamption of Eurovision followed by a Press Conference for the media. JP had the pleasure to exchange a few words in an interview after the Press Conference.  You can watch the entire coverage of Junior Eurovision 2013 - click here.
 
Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013: Habemus Winner. The 11th edition of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest was staged at Palace of Ukraine in Kiev, Ukraine on 30 November 2013 with a very first victory for the sunny islands of Malta with Gaia Cauchi winning the competition in a thrilling voting with the song "The Start" followed by Ukraine's Sofia Tarasova and the song "We are one" and then third placed entry was Belarus' Ilya Volkov and the song "Poy so mnoy".
 
The Final Result as displayed on the Scoreboard in the Palace of Ukraine:
 
1.  130 Malta
2.  121 Ukraine
3.  108 Belarus
4.  106 Russia
5.  091 Georgia
6.  069 Armenia
7.  066 Azerbaijan
8.  059 The Netherlands
9.  046 Sweden
10. 042 San Marino
11. 041 Moldova
12. 019 FYR Macedonia
 
All in all the Team of Radio International  on location had the pleasure to do 22 interviews with the participants, artists, Eurovision Winners, directors, etc. Watch out for them to come on the weekly edition of Radio International.
 
 
Junior Eurovision Song Contest Quiz:
Courtesy of Head of Press of the Maltese Delegation Daniel, Radio International has five CD singles of the Winning Song "The Start" by Gaia Gauchi to give away in a Quiz. The quiz went over two weeks where JP asked ten questions. The Quiz closed on 17 December 2013 and a draw was made during this week's edition of Radio International and five right and successful entries each won and will receive the CD single of the the winning song of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013. The five successful winners of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest Quiz are:  Claus Michael Fasting (Norway), Rafael Vivas (Venezuela), Robert Wahlstroem (Sweden), Steph Parker (South Australia) and Dara Robinson (Australia). 
 
Interview with Michele Perniola (Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013, San Marino): Debuting at the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013 is the tiny state of San Marino surrounded by Italy. Native Italian, 15-year-old Michele Perniola represented San Marino with the song called "O-o-O Sole intorno a me (Sunlight all around me)" ranking San Marino at Number 10 in the Final of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013. Michele has been on stages in the USA and also has been performing together with household names in Italy. For details listen to the interview on this week's edition of Radio International. Interview with Vadislav Yakovlev, the Executive Supervisor of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013: The European Broadcasting Union has changed responsibilities and staff for their Eurovision Family of events. Previously, Sietse Bakker was the Executive Supervisor of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest and the the 2012 edition of the show he has handed over this task to Vladislav Yakovlev, while Sietse Bakker fully focusses on the Eurovision Song Contest as Events Manager. Just before the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013 went on air the Team of Radio International had the pleasure to speak to the overall responsible person for the show to see how things are going. Find out details and additional information on an event such as the Junior Eurovision Song Contest during the interview with Vladislav Yakovlev, who one could see on the famous balcony surrounded by a bear and a tiger:) just before the votes were given out.  You can watch the entire coverage of Junior Eurovision 2013 - click here.
 
Interview with Marek Miil (Camera Director at the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013): One of the great pluses to be on location of a huge event such as the Junior Eurovision Song Contest is also the opportunity to meet the many people that are working behind the scenes on very important tasks making the broadcasting of the show possible. Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - Denmark's Eurovision Song Contest History:  A new quarter has begun which also marks the start of a new series called Denmark's Eurovision Song Contest History since Denmark for the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 and with this will be hosting the 2014 edition of the contest in Copenhagen the Team of Radio International thought to continue the tradition to reflect on all the Danish Eurovision entries over the years. Denmark has won the Eurovision Song Contest three time. It was first in 1963 when Grethe and Jorgen Ingmann performed "Dansevise", then the Olson Brothers won in 2000 with the song "Fly on the wings of love" and then in 2013 with Emmelie de Forest and "Only Teardrops". This week Alasdair closes the series looking back at the Eurovision entries from Denmark. The next series our correspondents and experts will be taking on board are the national finals of the various countries selecting their entry to the Eurovision Song Contest 2014.
