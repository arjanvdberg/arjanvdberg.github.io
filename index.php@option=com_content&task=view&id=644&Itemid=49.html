Show 13 February 2008
Listen to the Show:        Hour 1     |    Hour 2     |    Play List  
 
This week, Wednesday, 13 February 2008, 2200 - 2300 hours Radio International gave birth to "Radio International goes...". Every week this new more music based radio programme will feature a variety of special shows taking a closer look at the 1960s - CRAZY ZEROs, Artists Profiles, Eurovision Song Contest Specials with Interviews, a certain genre of music, such as Soul, Pop, Rock, Love Songs. In fact, our first Radio International goes Love Songs aired this week  Wednesday. Next week it will be Radio International goes 80s. Enjoy

This week on the show :


 

Regular items:
European Top 40, Local & European Weather Forecast and Temperature Check around the World, Dutch News Headlines, Birthday File, Concerts , Power Talk Tips, Africa News;

Movies:
A rundown of the movies offered in the cinemas near you.  For more information and which movies are offered where go to the Film Ladder Website by clicking this link. Movie Previews: The Mist, Sweeney Todd, Charlie Wilson's War;

EXPAT/Dutch/Community News:
Eva from TheHagueOnline  updated us on events scheduled in The Hague for EXPATs and international guests in the Netherlands. For further EXPAT Information and updates visit TheHagueOnline. Further News: Air Miles for dog travellers, Tighter Security at Schiphol, European 112 Day;

Restaurant Review:
This week Sally took a closer look at the Restaurant M. Enjoy!

The English Breakfast Team entertains the EXPATs in the Netherlands from the SALTO Radio station in Amsterdam. Tunes in for the latest current affairs items, sports and news. How to listen to THE ENGLISH BREAKFAST visit their their website.

Entertainment:
Classical Concert - Der Rosenkavalier, A very strange lake, Fatboy Oscar, Radio International interviewed the Purple Sex Heads from Germany - find out more about them go and visit their website;

Science and Technology, Space News, Environment:
Take a ride on Cassini , The Race to build the World's largest Ferris Wheel;

Eurovision Song Contest 2008:

The 2008 Eurovision Season is well underway and together with our Eurovision Expert Marcus Keppel-Palmer we now have weekly previews of the songs that are competing in Belgrade at the Eurovision Song Contest 2008 on 20, 22 & 24 May 2008.
On the preview this week we had the songs from Norway, Azerbaidjan and Moldova.
 
All the Eurovision news is available on Eurovision Song Contest and you can find constant updates of the latest Eurovision Song Contest News as they happen on esctoday.com .

Colin Berry, the legendary voice from BBC Radio 2 in the UK now hosts a Saturday Radio Show on BBC Three Counties Radio including a Eurovision Spot. Colin Berry was the spokesperson for the British jury for 25 years.
