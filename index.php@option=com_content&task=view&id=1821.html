Eurovision in Concert - Dutch Promotion Event 2010 (24 April 2010)

(update 06 April 2010)
The 2010 edition of  EUROVISION IN CONCERT is scheduled to take place in Zaanstad / Westzaan on Saturday, 24 April 2010. In order to  point out some Dutch culture to the delegations there will be a an official Welcome and Day Programme for Delegations and  the Media.
Then the big event: the Concert takes place at Lexion Venue, Overtoom 65 - 67 - Westzaan. Doors are open at 20.00 hrs and the concert will start at 21.00 hrs. Hosts of the show: Marga Bult (ESC 1987) and Cornald Maas (Dutch TV Commentator for the Eurovision Song Contest).
