Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2014: This week is a rather special edition of Radio International as some members of the Team are located in the host country of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2014 - Malta. The 12th and 2014 edition of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest is staged at Malta's Marsa Ship Yard on Saturday 15 November 2014 after last year's victory of Gaia Cauchi who won for the Malta with the song "The Start". 16 countries have confirmed their participation of young artists from: Armenia, Belarus, Bulgaria, Croatia, Cyprus, Georgia, Italy, Malta, Montenegro, Russia, San Marino, Serbia, Slovenia, Sweden, The Netherlands and Ukraine enjoying a time #Together at the event. Consult the offical event's website Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2014 for all the latest news concerning the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2014 and also you will also find all detailed information about the young artists. Members of the Team of Radio International will be present at the event and conduct interviews with the young artists which you can hear during the shows after the event although video interviews will be made available as soon as ready on the accompanying Eurovision Blog.
 
Luke Fisher from the EBU co-hosting this week: But this week's show also comes from Malta and JP has a co-host from  the Junior Eurovision Song Contest - Luke Fisher who is also the event's commentator. Luke and JP will be introducing all the 16 songs and give you background inforation on the artists but also on the event. In addition, JP and Luke take you down Memory Lane of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest by playing you some of the highlights of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest past and present. Make sure not to miss this particular radio show but also the TV Show. Should you live in a country that is not taking part in the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2014 and the show is not televised via the national broadcaster then you can find the livestream on the website of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2014. Complete details such as lists and Running Order of the 2014 contest you can find on the Wikipedia Page by clicking here . Good luck and a lot of fun to all the participants of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2014. And next week is another special on Radio International coming on as then it is the run up to the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2015 where Malta selects their entry to the Eurovision Song Contest 2015 in Vienna next May. Don't miss that as well.
