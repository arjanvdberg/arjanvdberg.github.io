Into Winter 2013 with Radio International:  Eurovision Radio International never stops and is keen on keeping the music of the Eurovision Song Contest in the ears of the many fans that join the show each and every week via the usual means either via PODCAST, LISTEN AGAIN, live broadcast or Live Stream. Please feel free to submit your requests of Eurovision songs and Radio International is delighted to play your requests during the shows. Click on CONTACT to submit your request to the programme. 
 
This week is a rather special edition of Radio International as some members of the Team are located in the host city of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013. The 11th and 2013 edition of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest will be staged at Palace of Ukraine in Kiev, Ukraine on 30 November 2013 after last year's victory of Anastasiya Petryk who won for the Ukraine with the song "Nebo". 12 countries have confirmed their participation of young artists from: Armenia, Azerbaijan, Belarus, Belgium, Georgia, Macedonia, Malta, Moldova, Netherlands, Russia, San Marino, Sweden and Ukraine competing for the trophy of the event. Consult the offical event's website Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013 for all the latest news concerning the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013 and also you will also find all detailed information about the young artists. Members of the Team of Radio International will be present at the event and conduct interviews with the young artists which you can hear during the shows around the 04 December 2013.
 
But this week's show also comes from Kiev's Palace of the Ukraine and JP and the team takes you down Memory Lane of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest by playing you all the Winners of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest since the beginning of the event 11 years ago. Also the favourites and hightlights of the contest will feature in the second hour and in the third hour you will be able to hear all the 12 contestants of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013 who will be competing come Saturday, 30 November 2013 for the trophy of the event. Make sure not to miss the radio show but also the TV Show. Should you live in a country that is not taking part in the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013 and the show is not televised via the national broadcaster then you can find the livestream on the website of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013. Complete details such as lists and Running Order of the 2013 contest you can find on the Wikipedia Page by clicking here. Good luck and a lot of fun to all the participants of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013. The normal format of the show returns next week at the usual time.

Eurovision Song Contest News:  Luke Fisher joins the team in the studio every week to bring you the news and the latest developments connected to the Eurovision Song Contest. Whether it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc Luke will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International. He also takes a look at some statistics such as viewing figures in the various countries and more.
In addition, Eurovision News is available on www.eurovision.tv, escxtra.com, esctoday.com and escflashmalta.com.
