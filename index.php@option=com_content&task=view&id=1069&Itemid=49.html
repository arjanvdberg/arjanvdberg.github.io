Show 29 October 2008

Listen to the Show:        Hour 1     |    Hour 2     |   Play List     |    Hour 3 (RIG)     

For our third hour we invite you for a special edition of Radio International goes....as JP and Stefan Engel take a look at the best bits and highlights of the Home Composed Song Contest 2008.
Click here to listen (Hour 3) and find out more of this special Song Contest for song writer.

Radio International from 2000 - 2200 hours   

Regular items:
European Top 40, Local & European Weather Forecast and Temperature Check around the World,  Concerts;

EXPAT/Dutch/Community News:
Eva Lazlo from TheHagueOnline  has the latest updates on events scheduled in The Hague for EXPATs and international guests in the Netherlands. For further EXPAT Information and updates visit TheHagueOnline's website. Listen out for some more news from The Hague throughout the show.  "A Hand in the Hague" helps you as a foreigner to get about in the Dutch City. Visit the website which is of great interests to EXPATS.  Other Expat News: ING Bank requests government financial support.

Get your daily update of the Dutch News in English by subscribing to their Newsletter with a summary of the latest events in and around the Netherlands. Click here for the website.

The English Breakfast Team entertains the EXPATs in the Netherlands from the SALTO Radio station in Amsterdam. Tune in for the latest current affairs items, sports and news. How to listen to THE ENGLISH BREAKFAST visit their their website. 

Science and Technology, Space News, Environment:
Mars Rover update;

Entertainment:
Origins of Halloween;

Interviews:
Marianne Korten joined us for an interview on her project called New Life Rules for 21st Century Women. The Work Shop is Saturday, 15 November 2008 from 0930 - 1730 hours in the Lloyd Hotel & Cultural Embassy, 1019 BN Amsterdam, The Netherlands. If you are interested then listen to the interview during our show and visit these links for more information. www.lloydhotel.com and www.soul-at-work.com.

Geert Kloppenburg told us all about his exciting business in the Hague: The Tuk Tuk Company. A rather different taxi company for our part of the World. Geert tells us all about his business and how the idea evolved from being an idea to a business in The Hague. Further information is available on their website www.tuktukcompany.nl.  

BLAST1386 our friendly Radio Partner:
Kieron Collins from the UK's BLAST1386 presents the 5 o'clock Rock" on Sunday afternoons. Listen to the repeat by clicking here.

Eurovision Song Contest with Radio International:

During the show we have our weekly Eurovision Song Contest Quiz your music requests and emails to the show. Chris Poppe presents the profile of a Mysterious Eurovision Song Contest Winner for listeners to guess who is behind the clues. 
   
For upcoming Eurovision Conventions / Events visit our Eurovision Events Calendar.
 
EurovisiO is a weekly radio show produced in the studios of Oradio in Antwerp, Belgium.  It airs every Monday evening from 2200 - 2300 hours and is presented by Philip Vereyken.  A must for Eurovision freaks. Lots of nice Eurovision music, interviews and news - however, in the Flemish language. You can listen to a repeat of EurovisO by clicking here. 
We had the pleasure of the company of Philip during our show this week where he informed the listeners on the upcoming anniversary show for Monday 03 November 2008 when EurovisiO will be 2 x 107 editions old. Tune into the programme via www.oradio.be from 2000 - 2400 hours when the show will be aired from the Karaoke Bar Bonaparte in the heart of Antwerp, Belgium. 
 
Eurovision Song Contest Interviews the Radio International Team has done with the Eurovision performers can be heard by visiting our Eurovision section of this website.

Malta's Eurovision Radio comes from the sunny island presented by Deo Grech on Super ONE Radio every Tuesday from 2045 - 2145 hours. You can contact Deo during the show and request your favourite Eurovision songs. The show is in Maltese mixed with English. Click here for further information and also if you would like to listen to back issues of Malta's Eurovision Radio.
 
OGAE Malta has added a new section to their website OGAE Malta. It is called "Eurovision...looking back ". Check it out and enjoy your occasional trip back down Memory Lane of the Eurovision Song Contest with OGAE Malta.   
   
Colin Berry, the legendary voice from BBC Radio 2 in the UK now hosts a Saturday Radio Show on BBC Three Counties Radio including a Eurovision Spot. Colin Berry was the spokesperson for the British jury for over 20 years.
