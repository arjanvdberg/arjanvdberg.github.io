Show 25 June 2008

Listen to the Show:        Hour 1     |    Hour 2     |   Play List     |    Hour 3 (RIG)     

In our third hour starting just after the news at 2200 hours "Radio International goes to the Eurovision Song Contest of 2008 (Part 2)" with interviews from Sweden's Charlotte Perrelli, Poland's Isis Gee,  Denmark's Simon Mathew and Kalomira from Greece. Also there is more of your favourite 2008 Eurovision requested songs. So - what are you waiting for - Click on HOUR 3 (RIG) and Enjoy.

Radio International from 2000 - 2200 hours 

Regular items:
European Top 40, Local & European Weather Forecast and Temperature Check around the World,  Birthday File, Concerts;

Movies: A rundown of the movies offered in the cinemas near you.  For more information and which movies are offered where go to the Film Ladder Website by clicking this link.  Made of Honour, Into the Wild;

EXPAT/Dutch/Community News:
Eva from TheHagueOnline has the latest updates on events scheduled in The Hague for EXPATs and international guests in the Netherlands. For further EXPAT Information and updates visit TheHagueOnline's website. Other News: New BENELUX Treaty signed;

Science and Technology, Space News, Environment:
Ground radar helpful to miners;

Entertainment, Music, Interviews:
Massimo Palladino - a young songwriter, singer and musician joined us to talk to us about his musical project and song writing. We had so much success from listeners that they even want to found a "Massimo Palladino Fanclub". Watch out a special edition of  "Radio International goes - Massimo Palladino" soon, where you can explore more of his talents and philosophies.  To already find out more about  Massimo Palladino, please visit his website.

Joao Pereira do Carmo talked to us about EURO2008 - the European Championship  in football and we interviewed him just before the match Germany vs Turkey. Amongst strength and weaknesses of the four last remaining teams Joao also explained us something about the finances on EURO2008. Listen to the interview during HOUR 1 and read more detailed information by clicking here. 

The English Breakfast Team entertains the EXPATs in the Netherlands from the SALTO Radio station in Amsterdam. Tune in for the latest current affairs items, sports and news. How to listen to THE ENGLISH BREAKFAST visit their their website. 

BLAST1386 our friendly Radio Partner:
Kieron Collins from the UK's BLAST1386 presents the 5 o'clock Rock"" on Sunday afternoons. Listen to the repeat by clicking here.

Eurovision Song Contest 2008:
 
The viewers and voters of the 2008 Eurovision Song Contest have selected Dima Bilan from Russia to win the 53rd Eurovision Song Contest. Congratulations!

Our Eurovision Song Contest expert Marcus Keppel-Palmer this week takes a look at some entries of Yugoslavia  featuring the years 1968, 1988 and 1998. Musically, the show is spiced with 90% of Eurovision songs and your requests.Keep sending them in and we will be pleased to play them. And of course participate at our live quiz where you can win some fabulous prizes.
