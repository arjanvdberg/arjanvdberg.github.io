Melodifestivalen 2015 Interviews: Melodifestivalen 2015 took place on Saturday 14 March 2015 in Stockholm's Friends Arena with 12 great artists selected out of the four Semi Finals and the Second Chance round. 28000 people in the Arena and a great pair of show hosts: Sanna Nielsen and Robin Paulsson guiding through the Final of Melodifestivalen. JP and Eric were on location and conducted interviews with many of the artists and producer Christer Bjoerkman. This week's edition of Radio International will be a special on Sweden's selection to the Eurovision Song Contest 2015. Enjoy the show especially this week. 12 acts competed in Sweden's Melodifestivalen 2015 and Eric and JP speed interviewed most of the acts at the Press Conferences after the Dress Rehearsal and after the Grand Final itself. The complete result of Melodifestivalen 2015 can be viewed here. Find a selection of photos from the Melodifestivalen 2015 weekend here.  Last week Radio International already broadcast a number of interviews from Melodifestivalen 2015 such as Mans Zelmerloew who will represent Sweden at the Eurovision Song Contest 2015 with the song "Heroes". If you missed the show last week fear not just follow this link to take you to last week's show in the Show Archive. This week listen to the remaining interviews from Sweden's national Selection - The Melodifestivalen 2015. Also you can watch all the interviews on the Radio International YouTube Channel once they aired on the radio show.
 
Interview with Linus Svenning (Melodifestivalen 2015): In 2014, Linus Svenning already made it to the Final of Melodifestivalen with the song "Broeder" (Brothers)" reaching Number 5. Linus returned in 2015 and made it through the Semi Final stages and the Andra Chansen roun and in the Final he reached Number 6 with the song "Forever starts today" which he performed in English. The Team of Radio International had the pleasure to meet the singer after the Final in Stockholm's Friends Arean for an interview.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here

Interview withe Jessica Andersson (Melodifestivalen 2015): No stranger to the stage of the Eurovision Song Contest as Jessica Andersson already represented Sweden at the contest as part of Fame when in Riga their song "Give me your love" reached Number 5 in the Eurovision Song Contest. Jessica tried it again to win Melodifestivalen in 2006, 2007 and in 2010 and with this represent Sweden again at the Eurovision Song Contest. However, she did not succeed. Then in 2015 once more the made it to the Final of Melodifestivalen with the song "Can't hurt me now" which reached Number 11 in the show. Eric and JP met the singer at the Press Conference after the Dress Rehearsal for an interview. Listen to it on this week's edition of Radio International.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here

Interview with Samir and Viktor and Songwriter Kevin Hoegdahl (Melodifestivalen 2015): Already last week Radio International aired one Interview with Samir and Viktor when Eric and JP met the guys after the Dress Rehearsal. Then again Samir and Viktor were interviewed again for a follow on after the Final of Melodifestivalen 2015. In addition, at the Aftershow Party Eric chatted with the songwriter Kevin Hoegdahl who is the creative mind behind the song "Groupie" which reached Number 8 in the Final. Enjoy these interviews on this week's edition of Radio International. You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here 

Interview with JTR (Melodifestivalen 2015): A complete new boyband on the Swedish music market is JTR which stands for John, Tom and Robin.The band is alsosuccessful in Australia as they were part of the Australian X-Factor. They entered the song "Building it up" into Melodifestivalen 2015 and reached the Final in Friends Area where at the end of the competition they ranked 11th. At the Press Conference after the Dress Rehearsal JP and Eric chatted with the young singers. Enjoy the fresh interview on the show this week. You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here

Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - Eurovision 2015 in Regions:  For the next few weeks the Eurovision Experts of Radio International will be taking a look at the 2015 Eurovision entries per region and this week Ross Bennett will start with the northern countries that are competing at the Eurovision Song Contest 2015. This week hear the songs from Denmark, Iceland, Norway, Sweden and Finland. In addition, each week our correspondents will be asked to tell us their personal favourite but also their tip what could actually win the Eurovision Song Contest 2015. 
 
Eurovision UnderCover:  This ever so popular feature on Radio International is David Mann's weekly edition of the Eurovision Cover Versions where David Mann from Eurovision Norwich will be intoducing some of the finest Eurovision Song Contest entries covered by other singers. Just be surprised. You will be hearing the actual song that has been on the Eurovision stage followed by the cover. Eurovision Song Contest News: Nathan Waddell joins the team in the studio every week to bring you the news and the latest developments connected to the Eurovision Song Contest such as the National Finals or if it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc Nathan will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International. He also takes a look at some statistics such as viewing figures in the various countries and more. Nathan is website editor with escxtra.com.
