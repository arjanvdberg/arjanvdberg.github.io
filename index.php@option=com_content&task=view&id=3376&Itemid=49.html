Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013: Habemus Winner. The 11th edition of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest was staged at Palace of Ukraine in Kiev, Ukraine on 30 November 2013 with a very first victory for the sunny islands of Malta with Gaia Gauchi winning the competition in a thrilling voting with the song "The Start" followed by Ukraine's Sofia Tarasova and the song "We are one" and then third placed entry was Belarus' Ilya Volkov and the song "Poy so mnoy".
 
The Final Result as displayed on the Scoreboard in the Palace of Ukraine:
 
1.  130 Malta
2.  121 Ukraine
3.  108 Belarus
4.  106 Russia
5.  091 Georgia
6.  069 Armenia
7.  066 Azerbaijan
8.  059 The Netherlands
9.  046 Sweden
10. 042 San Marino
11. 041 Moldova
12. 019 FYR Macedonia
 
All in all the Team of Radio International  on location had the pleasure to do 22 interviews with the participants, artists, Eurovision Winners, directors, etc. Watch out for them to come on the weekly edition of Radio International.
 
Interview with Gaia Gauchi - The Junior Eurovision Song Contest Winner 2013 for Malta:  What an amazing result for the sunny islands of Malta with the very first victory at any Eurovision event. Members of the Team of Radio International were in Kiev and witnessed the whole thing. Sadly, every good event comes to an end, however, one still can remember the nice moments and for 11 year old Gaia a dream came true - to win the Junior Eurovision Song Contest. Radio International had the huge pleasure to chat with Gaia live on last week's edition of the show and this week we have coverage from Kiev when Gaia was interviewed before the show and then after the Winners' Press Conference. You can hear both clips on the show this week.

Interview with Zlata Ognevitch (Co-Host of Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013 & representative of Ukraine at Eurovision 2013): At the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 Zlata Ognevitch achieved an amazing 4th place with the song "Gravity" when she was carried on stage of Igor the Giant. The song "Gravity" as well as other music from Zlata's repertoire was part of the Interval Act at the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013 which she co-hosted with Timur Miroshnychenko. Interview with Timur Miroshnychenko (Co-Host of Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013 & 2009):  It was already in 2009 when the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2009 was hosted by Ukraine in Kiev when Timur was the co-host at that time with Ani Lorak who also represented the Ukraine at the Eurovision Song Contest 2008 with the song "Shady Lady". Interview with Mylene and Rosanne (Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013, The Netherlands): "I see double" said JP at the Welcome Reception when the Dutch Twins Mylene and Rosanne crossed the way of JP during the Welcome Reception. Interview with Elias Elffors Elfstroem (Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013, Sweden): At the Welcome Reception of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest the performing young artists picked their starting position and so Elias Elffors Elfstroem from Sweden pulled number 1 as the opening act of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013 with the song "Det aer dir vi ska" maning "That's where we're going". Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - Denmark's Eurovision Song Contest History:  A new quarter has begun which also marks the start of a new series called Denmark's Eurovision Song Contest History since Denmark for the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 and with this will be hosting the 2014 edition of the contest in Copenhagen the Team of Radio International thought to continue the tradition to reflect on all the Danish Eurovision entries over the years. Denmark has won the Eurovision Song Contest three time. It was first in 1963 when Grethe and Jorgen Ingmann performed "Dansevise", then the Olson Brothers won in 2000 with the song "Fly on the wings of love" and then in 2013 with Emmelie de Forest and "Only Teardrops". This week Dermot Manning continuous the series looking back at the Eurovision entries from Denmark.
