All the Eurovision Interviews we have done in 2010 are available in our weekly show. If you do a search on the website for any particular artist you should find it. Eurovision Interview with Annet Artani (Cyprus 2006)
Radio International had the great pleasure to interview Annet Artani who represented Cyprus at the 2006 Eurovision Song Contest with the beautiful song "Why angels cry". Annet joined us during the show by phone from the United States of America. Listener /Viewers emailed in their questions to Annet. We talked about her Eurovision participation, the career afterwards and her involvement in the music of Britney Spears and what are her future career plans. Also there were exclusive songs of Annet which we played on the show. You can find Annet Artani on FaceBook and refer to the interview with Radio International when you contact her. Further information is available on her MySpace page and also Annet has a YouTube TV Channel which you van view here. And you can also follow her on Twitter. In case you missed the live show - here it is - the except of the interview. Enjoy!

Eurovision 2009 Interview with the Lovebugs (Switzerland)

Switzerland were represented at the 2009 Eurovision Song Contest in Moscow, Russia by the Lovebugs. Unfortunately, their song "The Highest Heights" - performed in Semi Final 1 receiving only 15 points ranking Switzerland at Number 14 when only the Top 10 go through into the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest. You can find out more detailed information about the Lovebugs by clicking here.

Eric Lehmann and JP from Radio International had the pleasure to meet the guys from Basel, Switzerland for an interview right after their Press Conference in the Media Centre at the 54th Eurovision Song Contest.
