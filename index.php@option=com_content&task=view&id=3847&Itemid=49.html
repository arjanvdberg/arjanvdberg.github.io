Tribute to Udo Juergens (Winner, Austria 1966):  Just before Christmas 2014 the Music World saw the tragic loss one of the greatest singers of our time - UDO JUERGENS who suprisingly passed away on 21 December 2014 during taking a walk. He had just celebrated his 80th birthday and had planned a tour in 2015. Udo Juergens represented Austria three times at the Eurovision Song Contest in the early days. "Warum nur Warum" ranked 6th in 1964, "Sag ihr ich lass die gruessen" ranking 4th in 1965 and then the very first victory for Austris came in 1966 when Udo Juergens was the Eurovision Song Contest with the song "Merci Cherie". Udo Jürgen Bockelmann wrote over 1000 hits and details of all can be found here. David Mann will be main host of the show this week and brings a special on Udo Juergens during this week's edition of Eurovision Radio International.
