Eurovision Live Concert 2013 in Setubal, Portugal 5-7 Sep 2013
OGAE Portugal in collaboration with the Municipalities of the Portuguese city Setubal near Lisbon are organising their fourth annual Eurovision Live Concert on 05 - 07 September 2013.

This year OGAE Portugal decided to make the event even bigger and that over three days with performances of former national finalists and Eurovision participants of Portugal and the big concert takes place as in the past years on Saturday, 07 September 2013 in the Auditorium Jose Alfonso right in the heart of Setubal and so far confirmed artists are:
 
Eurovision Live Concert 2013 - Artists Line Up on Saturday, 07 September 2013:
  1. Jalisse (Italy 1997) - "Fiumi di Parola"
  2. Roberto Bellarosa (Belgium 2013) - "Love kills"
  3. Birgitta Haukdahl (Iceland 2013) - "Open your heart"
  4. Moran Mazor (Israel 2013) - "Rak Bishvilo"
  5. Vlad Mirita (of Nico & Vlad) (Romania 2008) - "Pe-o margine de lume"
  6. Buket Bengisu from Safir (Turkey 2002) - "Leylaklar soldu kalbinde"
