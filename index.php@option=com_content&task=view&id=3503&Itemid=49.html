Interview with Elaiza (Germany 2014):  Germany held a big National Final yet again after the great success in 2013 which was won by Cascade in Hannover. NDR organised this year's National Final in Cologne's Lanxess Arena with the participation of many well known artists but also brand new musicians who actually won the National Final and with this represent Germany at the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 in Copenhagen, Denmark - Elaiza (Elżbieta "Ela" Steinmetz, Yvonne Grünwald and Natalie Plöger) received the most number of votes for the song "Is it right" and beating Germany's well known Unheilig in the Super Final. The complete result of the National Final in Germany can be seen here. Courtesy of our newsman Luke Fisher and escXtra.com and eurovision.tv listen to the interview with the girls on this week's edition of Radio International. 
 
Eurosong 2014 - Complete Recap:  The Eurosong 2014 (Belgium's National Final) took place Saturday, 16 March 2014 where Belgium together with an International Jury selected their entry for the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 to take place in Copenhagen, Denmark next May. After many casting shows and four Semi Finals and the Grand Final in Antwerp's Sportspaleis Belgium sends into the race Axel Hirsoux to follow the footsteps of Roberto Bellarosa to represent the country at the Eurovision Song Contest 2014. This week on the show listen to some of the interviews the Team of Radio International did at Eurosong 2014. You can see all the photos from the event on our Facebook page by clicking here (will be available soon). See below which artists are being featured on the show this week and enjoy! The entire results of Eurosong Voting are available here.
   
Belgium Eurosong 2014 - Interview with Axel Hirsoux (Belgium 2014): Winning the Belgian National Final 2014 by a storm was Axel Hirsoux and his amazing voice performing "Mother" receiving 234 points from the International Jury and the Televoters in Belgium. The runners up Bandits only received 90 votes. Listen to the interview Radio International did with Axel after his winning performance.   You can watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
 
Belgium Eurosong 2014 - Interview with Bandits:  Young boy band Bandits came second in the Belgian National Final 2014 with the song "One" receiving 90 points from the International Jury and the Belgian public. Inspired by the Beatles and previosuly taken part in the Belgian national selection to the Junior Eurovision Song Contest. Listen to the interviews with the boys on this week's edition of the show.  You can watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 

Belgium Eurosong 2014 - Interview with Yass:  Opening up the Grand Final of Eurosong 2014 was Yass with the song "Need you tonight" coming 4th in in the competition. Listen to the interview with the singer the Team of Radio International did with Yass in the Press Centre after the show where he tells us about his musical impressive background.  You can watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
 
 
Belgium Eurosong 2014 - Interview with Udo: Making it through the Castings and also the Semi Finals into the Grand Final of the Belgian Eurosong 2014 with the song "Hero" is Udo who ended up at Number 6 in the Grand Final of Eurosong 2014. The Team of Radio International met the singer in the Press Centre after the show for an interview. Would he take part again? Find out in listening to this interview on this week's edition of Radio International.  You can watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - The National Final Season 2014:  All the songs to the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 have been selected and on Monday, 17 March 2014 was submission deadline date set by the EBU (European Broadcasting Union). By now all the songs are known. This also marks the end of the current series. Next week a new feature will replace The National Final Seaon 2014 where our Radio International Eurovision Experts will be introducing something new to the show live on Radio International. But for this week Alasdair Rendal closes out the National Final Season 2014 with taking a lot at the songs that were announced and selected by the countries over the past seven days. Listen to the Eurovision 2014 entries from Armenia, Austria, Azerbaijan, Belgium, Georgia, Germany, Moldova, Netherlands, Norway, Portugal, Russia and San Marino.
