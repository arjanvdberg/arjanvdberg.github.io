Eurovision Song Contest with Radio International and on Oikotimes.TV
Eurovision Interview with Paula Seling (Romania 2010):  Just last weekend JP went to Romania and had a great pleasure to meet Paul Seling who together with singing partner Ovi represented Romania at the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest with the song “Playing with Fire” reaching a fantastic third place in Oslo. JP went to the recording studio of Paula for this interview. Listen to it during this week’s show.
 
Euro Voice Song Contest 2010 (Austria 2010):  Something new that will be held on 23 & 24 September 2010 in Athens, Greece and that is the first ever Euro Voice Song Contest. Austria is one of the countries being represented at this event with the song “Time to believe” performed by Tafkanik. Austria’s Representative will be joining the team of Radio International to tell us more about this new Song Contest and on his particpation in it. Euro Voice 2010 will be hosted by Pamela Andersson – so well worth to watch it. You can follow Tafkanik's rehearsal and trip to Athens by clicking here.
 
Euro Friend Song Contest 2010 - Start of HEAT 5:  Radio International in collaboration with the Euro Friends Song Contest 2011 announcing the start of HEAT 5 for the international juries to vote on the Face Book Page of the contest. On the Face Book Page of the Euro Friends Song Contest, you vote for your 10 favourite songs in the Eurovision Song Contest amongst the fourth hundred songs of Europe's biggest music competition.  Join to vote for your favourite Eurovision songs by clicking on this link which takes you to the Face Book Page of the Event. Remember, you have to be a member in order to join the voting.    
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight -  Ireland with Dermot Manning: Our Irish voice on the team rejoins us this week to recap on the Eurovision Song Contests that were hosted in Ireland. This week Dermot takes a look at the Eurovision Song Contest from 1994, 1995 and 1997 and you will hear Dermot’s pick of the songs from these contests.
 
Eurovision Song Contest News:  Eurovision News. Luke Fisher joins the team in the studio every week to bring you the news and the latest developments connected to the Eurovision Song Contest. Whether it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc Luke will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International.
In addition, Eurovision News is available on www.eurovision.tv, oikotimes.com, esctime.com, escdaily.com, esctoday.com and escflashmalta.com.
