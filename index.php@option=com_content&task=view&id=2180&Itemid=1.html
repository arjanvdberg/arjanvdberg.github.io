Eurovision in Concert 2011 - 09 April 2011, Amsterdam, The Netherlands

For the third time in a row this great annual event is happening: EUROVISION IN CONCERT 2011 takes place in Amsterdam, The Netherlands with the participation of many Eurovision 2011 performers. A unique chance to meet the contestants already who will be competing at the 2011 Eurovision Song Contest in Duesseldorf, Germany May 10, 12 & 14.

Date:
09 April 2011, 1900 hours CET

Venue:

 

CLUB AIR
Amstelstraat 24
Amsterdam, The Netherlands 

Participants List:

  1. Albania - Aurela Gace - Feel the Passion
  2. Armenia - Emmy - Boom Boom
  3. Austria - Nadine Beiler - The secret is love
  4. Belarus - Anastasia Vinnikova - I am a Belarusian
  5. Bulgaria -  Poli Genova - Na Inat
  6. Croatia - Daria Kinzer - Celebrate
  7. Cyprus - Christos Mylordos - San aggelos s'agapisas
  8. Finland - Paradise Oskar - Da da dam
  9. FYROM - Vlatko Ilievski – Rusinka
  10. Greece - Loukas Giorkas Feat. Stereo Mike - Watch my dance
  11. Latvia - Musiqq - Angel In Disguise
  12. Malta - Glen Vella - One Life
  13. Poland - Magdalena Tul - Jestem
  14. Russia - Alexey Vorobyov - Get you
  15. San Marino - Senit - Stand by
  16. Serbia - Nina - Čaroban
  17. Slovenia - Maja Keuc - No One
  18. Switzerland - Anna Rossinelli - In love for a while
  19. Turkey - Yüksek Sadakat - Live It Up
  20. United Kingdom - Blue - I Can
  21. Ukraine - Mike Newton - Angel

 

You can find an updated list in Dutch here. A nice promotion video has been produced and is visible here courtesy of BEA Record. 

Presenters of Eurovision in Concert 2011:
The show will be hosted by a lovely trio consisting of former Commentator of the Eurovision Song Contest: Cornald Maas. German show host and comedian Sascha Korf (regular host of Eurovision Club Germany's annual event) and last and certainly not least the Dutch entrant to the 2003 Eurovision Song Contest - Esther Hart whose "One more night" placed the Netherlands at Number 13.

Tickets:
Tickets are priced at a mere EURO 25 per person for the entire event. Please order and pay your ticket via http://www.paylogictickets.nl/event/47586/. Print it and bring it with you to the entrance on 09 April 2011.

Please make sure that you do not miss this great event which is organised by OGAE Netherlands, BEARECORDS, Songfestival.web-log.nl and esctoday.com.
 
Happy Eurovisioning!
