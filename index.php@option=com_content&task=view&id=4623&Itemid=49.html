Hightlights of the show

Taking you through the Summer of 2016. Enjoy the summer specials that the Team of Radio International are compiling for your listening pleasure including your music requests as well as some interviews with Eurovision performers and beyond.
 
Interview with Sandra Reemer (Netherlands 1972, 1976, 1979 and 1983):  Most of the show this week will be devoted to the 5th edition of the Eurovision Weekend Berlin which happened last weekend in the city centre of Berlin, Germany. This year the partnering country were the Netherlands and the organisers invited two huge Eurovision stars from the Netherlands out of which one was Sandra Reemer who represented the Netherlands three times at the Eurovision Song Contest. In 1972 with "Als het om de liefte gaat" with Andres coming fourth. Then in 1976 Sandra represented the Kingdom of the Netherlands with "The Party is over now" coming nineth and in 1979 she reached Number 12 with "Colorado" as Xandra. In 1983, Sandra Reemer was the backing vocalist when Bernadette represented the Netherlands with the song "Sing me a song" that reached Number 7 in Munich, Germany. Sandra was also a member of the Dutch Divas which as a trio enjoyed successes together with Maggie McNeal (1980) and Marga Bult (Marcha, 1987). She also hosted the Dutch version of "Do you want to bet" on Dutch TV. Radio International's Eric had the pleasure to meet and interview the singer which you can hear on this week's edition of Radio International.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview with Esther Hart: (Netherlands 2003):  Also being guest at the Eurovision Weekend 2016 in Berlin was the Dutch entrant to the Eurovision Song Contest 2003 - Esther Hart who reached Number 13 with the song "One more night". In the same year Esther also had entered a song into the UK National Final called "Wait for the Moment" which was not selected to represent the UK at Eurovision 2003, hence Esther represented the Netherlands in Riga, Latvia. Radio International's Eric chatted with the singer about her Eurovision participation as well as her upcoming projects. You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here

Interview with Laura Pinski (German National Final 2016):  "Unser Song fuer Stockholm" was Germany's selection show to find that song that represented Germany at the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 in Stockholm, Sweden. We know that Jamie-Lee won this national final and represented Germany with the song called "Ghost" coming last at the end of the voting. Laura Pinski, of Polish origin, was competing in this German national final and reached Number 4 with a Ralph Siegel composition called "Under the sun we are one". Laura was also invitedto the Eurovision Weekend to Berlin and Radio International's JP had the pleasure to chat with the lively singer. Listen to this interview on this week's edition of Radio International.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview with Sebastian Calleja (Winner of the OGAEvision Song Contest 2016, Malta): Big part of the Eurovision Weekend 2016 in Berlin was the first OGAEvision Song Contest which contained singers from 12 countries that competed for the trophy covering a Eurovision song. The quality was of very high standard and the contest was won by 18 year old Sebastian Calleja from sunny Malta who performed his cover of the Israeli Eurovision entry from 2015 by Nadav Guedj "Golden Boy". The prize was also that Sebastian will perform in front of 1700 plus audience on the Eurovision Cruise Helsiki to Tallin on 03 September 2016 organised by OGAE Finland. Radio International's JP had the big pleasure to follow and to chat with Sebastian which you can hear on this week's edition of Radio International. See the reprise of the winning performance here. You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
