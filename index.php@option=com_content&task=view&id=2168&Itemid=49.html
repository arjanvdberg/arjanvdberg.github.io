Eurovision Interview with the 3Js(Netherlands 2011): The Dutch National Final was held on Sunday, 30 January 2011 in Hilversum, The Netherlands with the 3Js having been nominated to represent the Netherlands at the 2011 Eurovision Song Contest in Duesseldorf. It was to the jury and to the public to decide which of the five 3Js songs is to go to Germany come May. The result was that the song "Je Vecht nooit alleen" won the jury's and the televote. JP had the pleasure and the chance to meet the guys from Volendam for a very quick interview right after their press conference with the Dutch media. Find out during the interview what the 3Js stand for.  In the picture, 1987 Dutch Representative Marcha or Marga Bult with the 3Js. 
 
Eurovision Interview with Hind (Netherlands 2008): Hind represented the Netherlands at the 2008 Eurovision Song Contest with the song "Your heart belongs to me" which unfortunately failed to qualify the Netherlands to the Grand Final of Europe's biggest music competitation. Hind was also part of the Dutch National Final last Sunday as Jury member. JP took the chance to meet the singer again for a brief interview on her time in Belgrade during Eurovision 2008 and also for a career update. And last but not least.... 
 
Eurovision Interview with Esther Hart (Netherlands 2003): Of course there is almost no Eurovision show with an Interval Act and the organising Dutch TV company TROS roled in some of the former Dutch participants to the Eurovision Song Contest such as Ben Cramer, Marcha, Marlayne, Sieneke and Eshter Hart. JP had the big pleasure to exchange a few words with Esther Hart which you can hear on the show this week. Esther Hart represented the Netherlands in the 2003 Eurovision Song Contest with the quite successful entry "One more night" reaching Number 11 in Riga. 
 
Eurovision Interview with Marty Brem (Austria 1980 & 1981): Marty Brem was one of the special guests during the recent 2011 convention of the OGAE Germany in Munich. Marty Brem was part of the group Blue Danube back in 1980 reaching Number 8 with the song "Du bist Musik". The year after he was nominated by Austrian TV to return and represent Austria one more time as soloist with the song "Wenn Du da bist" reaching Number 17. Radio International's Chris Poppe and JP met the singer for an interview just before he gave his concerts.  Please note that due to heavy number of requests during this week's edition of Radio International it has been decided to postpone the broadcast of the interview to a later date. Apologies for the fans who wanted to hear the interview this week.

Euro Friend Song Contest 2011 - Voting Result of the Second Quarterfinal:  Radio International in collaboration with the Euro Friends Song Contest are in search for the ultimate Eurovision song and will be announcing during the show this week the ten qualifiers of the second quarterfinal which will go through to the Semi Finals. During the past week international juries have been voting for the first lot of songs on the Face Book Page of the Euro Friends Song Contest. Prime Organiser of this event based on the Eurovision Song Contest, Andy Simon joins the team in the studio to reveal the results. You too can be part of the international jury by contacting Andy Simon on this link which takes you to the Face Book Page of the Event. Remember, you have to be a member of this Facebook group in order to join the voting or consult the brand new website.

Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight: National Finals: The national selection season has started and on a weekly basis Radio International will assess the songs with a panel of Eurovision experts - the listeners via the oikotimes.tv chat. For that Dermot Manning will join the team in the studio.
