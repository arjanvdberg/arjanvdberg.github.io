Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2015 - Interview with L-Ahwa alias Gianluca Bezzina and his siblings (Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2015): The Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2015 took place on 21 & 22 November 2014 from the Marsa Ship Yard in Malta with 20 songs entering into the Semi Final then narrowed down to a Top 14 and then on Saturday the winner was the public favourite as well as the jury favourite - Amber Bondin will be representing Malta at the Eurovision Song Contest with the song "Warrior" written by Elton Zarb and Matt Muxu, who also were responsible for the victory of Gaia Cauchi at the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013 with the song "The Start". Continuing on this week's edition of Radio International listen to the interviews with the contestants and this week it is the turn of a singer that already has represented Malta at the Eurovision Song Contest in 2013 with the song "Tomorrow" ranking at Number 8 in the Grand Final in Malmoe, Sweden - The singing Maltese doctor Gianluca Bezzina supported by his brothers and sisters in the formation L-Ahwa tried to obtain the ticket to represent Malta at the Eurovision Song Contest 2015 with the song "Beautiful to me" which in the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2015 only ranked at Number 12 in the Final. Radio International met all of the siblings for an interview which will be broadcast on this week's edition of Radio International.  You will enjoy this one. :) You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here

Interview with Ray Caruana of Live Report (United Kingdom 1989):  OGAE Germany held their annual convention on Saturday, 24 January 2015 in Munich, Germany with the participation of national and international Eurovision performers such as Michael Holm, Krista Siegfrids (Finland 2013), Hersi Matmuja (Albania 2014) and the amazing Kessler Twins (Germany 1959). Parts of the Team of Radio International were at the event and interviewed some of the artists. And one of the special guests OGAE Germany invited for the 2015 convention was Ray Caruana from Live Report reaching a remarkable runner up position in the Eurovision Song Contest 1989 with the song "Why do I always get it wrong". Eric from Radio International had the big pleasure to meet the singer for an interview you can hear on this week's edition of the show. You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - The National Final Season 2015: Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - The National Final Season 2015:  The National Final Season to the Eurovision Song Contest 2015 is well under way.  During the last weeks Radio International went through the history of Austria at the Eurovision Song Contest. Austria will host the Eurovision Song Contest 2015 in the Wiener Stadthalle in Vienna next May with the Grand Final on the 23 May 2015. Like in previous years the Radio International Eurovision Experts will be assessing the National Finals 2015 live on Radio International. Marcus Keppel-Palmer continues the series taking a look at the National Finals and entries Ireland, Hungary, Slovenia, Finland, Moldova, Spain and Sweden.
 
Eurovision UnderCover:  This ever so popular feature on Radio International is David Mann's weekly edition of the Eurovision Cover Versions where David Mann from Eurovision Norwich will be intoducing some of the finest Eurovision Song Contest entries covered by other singers. Just be surprised. You will be hearing the actual song that has been on the Eurovision stage followed by the cover. Eurovision Song Contest News: Nathan Waddell joins the team in the studio every week to bring you the news and the latest developments connected to the Eurovision Song Contest such as the National Finals or if it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc Nathan will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International. He also takes a look at some statistics such as viewing figures in the various countries and more. Nathan is website editor with escxtra.com.
