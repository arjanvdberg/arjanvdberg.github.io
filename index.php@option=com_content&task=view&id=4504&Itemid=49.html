Show 04 May 2016 - Live from Stockholm and the 61st Eurovision Song Contest
Radio International and ESCNEWS Radio & TV with Chat Room: Radio International is aired on their TV channel live every Wednesday where fans can join and see how we actually do Radio International and listen to the show while chatting with the other Eurovision fans joining the community.  But also you can watch there a re-run of the weekly edition of Radio International at various times on ESCNEWS Radio. Enjoy and tell your friends to join and be part of the Eurovision Radio International Community! Also become part of the Eurovision Radio International Facebook Group which accompanies the show and is available here. It contains lots of Eurovision News from the major Eurovision News Websites but also announcements. 
Please note that many interviews we air are also available as video interview which can be viewed on our Radio International YouTube Channel. Just take a look and enjoy also see the artists talking to us. 

The Eurovision Song Contest with Eurovision Radio International - your music requests, comments and ideas: Eurovision Radio International never stops and is keen on keeping the music and news of the Eurovision Song Contest in the ears of the many fans that join the show each and every week via the usual means either on FM, Cable and also via PODCAST, Itunes, LISTEN AGAIN, live broadcast or Live Stream. You can also find Radio International (RTV Katwijk) on TuneIn. Please feel free to submit your comments, ideas and requests of Eurovision songs and Radio International is delighted to play your requests during the shows. Click on CONTACT to submit your request to the programme. You can also reach us on Twitter: @RadioInt

Eurovision 2015 meets Eurovision 2016: As the Team of Radio International is in Stockholm, Sweden at the 61st Eurovision Song Contest this and next week as Radio International broadcasts its traditional programme "Eurovision 2015 meets Eurovision 2016" as a review to last year's Eurovision Song Contest entries and a look ahead of the songs that are competing in 2016. This way you can compare the songs per country. This week it is Part 1 of 2. Part 2 will be broadcast at the same time next week. 
 
Special Edition and additional shows of Eurovision Radio International - Live from Stockholm:  In addition to Eurovision 2015 meets Eurovision 2016 Radio International will be producing additional shows in form of PODCASTS and will be devoted to live coverage from the Press Centre as the Team of Radio International will be chatting with as many of this year's Eurovision artists and more. JP, David, Eric and Lilian and more media colleagues will be giving you impressions from Eurovision Town 2016 - Stockholm.
 
The Eurovision Song Contest 2016:  Initially, 43 countries signed up and declareed their participation at the Eurovision Song Contest 2016, however, on 20 April 2016 Romania's Broadcaster lost the membership with the EBU (European Broadcasting Union) and thus cannot take part at the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 leaving the remaining countries to a number of 42 to  compete for the trophy and the right to host the Eurovision Song Contest 2017. The countries are distributed into two Semi Finals and the Top Ten of both Semi Finals meet in the Grand Final on the Big Five (Germany, Spain, France, United Kingdom, Italy) and the host country Sweden will in the 61st Eurovision Song Contest..
 
Dates:
Semi Final 1 of the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 is scheduled for Tuesday, 10 May 2016.
Semi Final 2 of the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 is scheduled for Thursday, 12 May 2016.
The Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 is scheduled for Saturday, 14 May 2016.
 
All three shows come from the legendary Ericsson Globe or Globen Arena which also was the venue of the Eurovision Song Contest 2000.  Last year's winner Måns Zelmerloew and Petra Mede will be the hosts of the shows and to whom will Måns hand over the trophy to the Winner of the Eurovision Song Contest 2016? :)  May the best song win and now enjoy this week's Eurovision extravaganza with Eurovision Radio International.
