Interview with Emmelie de Forest (Denmark 2013 Eurovision Winner):  Congratulations once again go to Emmelie de Forest from Denmark who won the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 with the song "Only Teardrops". Denmark received a total amount of 281 points when the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 was staged in the Malmoe Arena on 18 May 2013. Juries and Televoters of 38 countries voted for "Only Teardrops" to be Europe's most popular song of 2013 and with this victory Denmark also earned the right to host the Eurovision Song Contest 2014. The preliminary date has been set to 17 May 2014. David Mann had the pleasure to chat with Emmelie de Forest before her victory. Listen to the interview during the week's edition of Radio International.  
 
Interview with Marco Mengoni (Italy 2013): The Winner of the San Remo Song Festival represented Italy at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 with the song "L'essenziale" which ranked at Number 7 in the Grand Final. Italy as well as France, Germany, Spain, United Kingdom as well as the host country are automatically qualified for the Grand Final and thus do not need to go through the Semi Finals. JP and Philip from eurovisiOradio.eu interviewed some of the artists at the Red Carpet of the Welcome Reception in Malmoe and Marco gave us an interesting interview which you can hear in this week's edition of the show.  
 
Interview with Anouk (Netherlands 2013):  The Netherlands were represented at the Eurovision 2013 by superstar Anouk who after nine years successfully qualified the Netherlands for the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 with the song "Birds" which received 114 points and with this a 9th place in the Grand Final. The Netherlands had not qualified for nine years last time being with Reunion and "Without You" at the the Eurovision Song Contest 2004 in Istanbul. The Team of Radio International had the big honour to meet Anouk for a quick chat at the Meet and Greet in the Euroclub of Malmoe. Listen to this chat during this week's edition of the show. You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview with Hannah Manccini (Sloveniva 2013):  Also being interviewed at the Red Carpet event was Hannah from Slovenia who represented her country at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 with the song "Straight into love" which unfortunately ended up at Number 16 in Semi Final 1 of the contest. Hannah has a very interesting background originating from the USA thus when listening to the interview you have an American accent from Hannah. Another interview with Hannah was done in the Press Centre of the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 which Radio International will air at a later date.   You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here  
 
Interview with Andrius Pojavis (Lithuania 2013):  For the third time in a row Lithuania managed to qualify for the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest with Andrius Pojavis and the song "Something" reaching Number 22 after the great success of 2012 when Donny Montell's "Love is blind" ranked 14th in the Final in Baku and Evelina Sasenko's "C'est ma vie" from 2011 which ranked at Number 19. JP and Philip met the singer at the Red Carpet event in Malmoe for a quick chat which Radio International airs on this week's edition of the show.
 
Interview with Moje 3 (Serbia 2013):  Serbia was represented at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 by Moje 3 who won BEOSONG with the song "Ljubav je svuda" reaching Number 11 in Semi Final 1 of the contest sadly not qualifying Serbia for the Grand Final which is is the second time in the history of Serbia's participation at the Eurovision Song Contest that the country did not qualify for the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest following Marko Kon & Milan Nikolic with the song "Cipela" back in 2009. Only the Top 10 qualify for the Grand Final. Moje 3 were supported by former Winner of the Eurovosion Song Contest 2007 - Marija Serifovic and the Team of Radio International had the pleasure of interviewing the three ladies of Moje 3 which you will be able to hear on this week's edition of Radio International.  You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here   |  The second interview is available - here
