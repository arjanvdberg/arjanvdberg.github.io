Interview with Nicki French (United Kingdom 2000): During 2013 and early 2014 the Team of Radio International has been to many Eurovision events besides the Eurovision Song Contest itself and one is bound to bump into previous Eurovision performers. So did David Mann when he met Nicki French at a UK function and had a little interview with her you can hear on the show this week. 

 
Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2014 - Interview with Miriam Christine (Malta 1996): The Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2014 took place on Friday and Saturday, 07 and 07 February 2014 from the Malta Fairs and Convention Centre in Ta'Qali with Firelight winning the ticket to represent Malta at the Eurovision Song Contest 2014. Full result of the voting is available here. This week Radio International offers you the next set of interviews with the contestants of the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2014. Miriam Christine represented Malta back in 1996 at the Eurovision Song Contest with "In a woman's heart" which ranked 10th in Oslo. During the past years Miriam Christine has tried again for a few times to regain the ticket to the Eurovision Song Contest but unfortunately in vain. She did win the Malta Hit Song Contest 2008 with the song "Alone today". Amongst many projects as songwriter, singer and Children's Choir founder on Malta's sister island Gozo she decided to try again her luck at the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2014 with the song "Safe" penned by Emil Calleja Bayliss and Mark Scicluna. Unfortunately, the song could not qualify for the Grand Final of the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2014 and remained in the Semi Final. During the rehearsals Radio International had the pleasure to meet the singer and the songwriters for an interview which you can hear on this week's edition of the show. You can watch this interview on our YouTube channel by clicking - here
 
Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2014 - Interview with Davinia Pace & Muxu:  In the 2013 edition of the Malta Eurovision Song Contest Davinia Pace had a remarkable entry called "Betrayed" ranking at Number 3 at the end of the Final with a song which could be a theme tune to a James Bond Movie. Elton Zarb and Muxu are the composers of the song as well as this year's entry of Davinia which is called "Brand New Day" and ranked at Number 13. Elton and Muxu were also the song writers of the winning song for Malta at the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013 which was won by 11 year old Gaia Cauchi when she won in Kiev with "The Start" last December. JP and David chat with Davinia and Muxu about their entry and the winning song for Junior Eurovision 2013 and all that in the Green Room backstage.  You can watch this interview on our YouTube channel by clicking - here

 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - The National Final Season 2014:  With a start of a new quarter that means it is also the start of a new feature on Eurovision Radio International. In the last quarter of 2013 Radio International went through the history of Denmark at the Eurovision Song Contest. And over the Christmas Break the national final season has officially begun. Like in previous years the Radio International Eurovision Experts will be assessing the National Finals 2014 live on Radio International. Chris Poppe takes a look at the National Finals so far and some of the highlights from Ireland, Estonia, Lithuania, Romania, France, United Kingdom and Sweden.
