Welcome Reception & Red Carpet Interviews:  Every year at the Eurovision Song Contest the host city organises a Welcome Reception and Opening Ceremony which can be watched by the fans and local people and the many journalists and reporters covering the Eurovision Song Contest for their media. There are clips from the Red Carpet event where JP and Philip from eurovisiOradio had the pleasure to co-interview some of the singers of this year's Eurovision Song Contest. Throughout the upcoming editions of Radio International the clips will be injected into the show for your listening pleasure.  This week listen to some small interview clips with Ukrine's Giant Igor, Switzerland's Emil who is with 94 years the oldest member that every stood on the Eurovision stage amongst others. Just stay tuned to find out.
 
Interview with Nodi & Sopho & Thomas G:sson (Georgia 2013): Tipped to be one of the fans' favourites to win Eurovision 2013 Georgia was represented by Sopho and Nodi with a song called "Waterfall" penned by the same composer that wrote the winning song of the Eurovision Song Contest 2012: Loreen's "Euphoria" - Thomas G:sson from Sweden. Though the Swedish vitamin B did not help for a Georgian Victory at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 as "Waterfall" only reached at Number 15 in the Grand Final in the Malmoe Arena. The Team of Radio International had the big pleasure to meet the singers and the composer for an interview which you will hear in this week's edition of Radio International.   You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here for Part 1   |  Part 2
 
Interview with Elitsa Todorova and Stoyan Yankoulov (Bulgaria 2007 & 2013):  Returning to the stage of the Eurovision Song Contest after their Number 5 achievement back in 2007, Elitsa Todorova and Stoyan Yankoulov represented Bulgaria for a second time at the Eurovision Song Contest with the song "Sama Shampioni" which reached only Number 12 in Semi Final two when only the Top 10 countries advance to the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest. Elitsa and Stoyan had a huge success in 2007 at the Eurovision Song Contest when their entry "Water" reached Number 5 and thus was the only time Bulgaria was in the Grand Final of the contest. Listen to our second interview the Team of Radio International did in Malmoe with the superstars from Bulgaria.   You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click herefor Part 1   |  Part 2
 
Interview with Takasa (Ex Heilsarmee)(Switzerland 2013):  Switzerland was one of the first countries selecting their entry to the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 with a band called Heilsarmee (Salvation Army) who according to EBU rules had to change their name in order to participate at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013. The band's name was changed to TAKASA however, the song remained to be "You and me" and competed in Semi Final 2 in which unfortunately Switzerland only archieved Number 13 when only the Top Ten advance to the Grand Final. Our roving reporter from Scandinavia - Lilian Brunell had the opportunity to meet all of the members of the band in their hotel in Malmoe and did an interview which you can hear during this week's edition of Radio International.  You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here
