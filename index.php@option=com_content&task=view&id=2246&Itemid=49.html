Live Interview with Anne-Marie David (Winner 1973, Luxembourg & France 1979): Radio International is extremely honoured to welcome to the airwaves and cyberwaves the winner of the 1973 Eurovision Song Contest for Luxembourg: Anne-Marie David who participated twice at the Eurovision Song Contest. She won in 1973 with "Tu te reconnaitras" but returned to the contest in 1979 when she came third for France with "Je suis l'enfant de soleil". Anne-Marie is also writing songs and reached third place in this year's Cyprus Golden Melody Festival. Find out more from the Grand Dame de la Musique during this week's edition of Radio International. 
 
Live Interview with Jedward (Ireland, 2011): Radio International had received confirmation that John and Edward of Jedward will be joining the team in the Studio live for an interview via phone. Jedward were on their tour bus at that time. Since Jedward won the national final in Ireland Radio International has been flooded with requests for the Irish entry called "Lipstick". Jedward will represent Ireland at the 2011 Eurovision Song Contest in Semi Final 2 at position Number 19. Thank you to all the crazy fans sending in their questions to Jedward during the interview. For Jedward's website click here .
 
Live Interview with Kati Wolf (Hungary, 2011): Also just before the show started Radio International  received the green light to welcome Hungary's Kati Wolf to the airwaves and cyberspace of the show this week. Kati Wolf represents Hungary at the 2011 Eurovision Song Contest in Duesseldorf in Semi Final 1 with the song "What about my dream" which two weeks ago the 43 International Juries voted to be the Radio International 2011 Eurovision Song Contest Winner. Rado International is extremely happy to have Kati on the show. 
 
Live Interview with Glen Vella (Malta 2011): A spontaneous idea during the show - to call up Glen Vella in Malta to see how the preparations are going for the Maltese entrant to this year's Eurovision Song Contest. Radio International caught Glen up in his car in Malta while driving from rehearsals with the backing vocalists and dancers. Good luck Malta!
 
 
Live Interview with A Friend in London (Denmark, 2011): Radio International is delighted to announce that also the Danish representative to the 2011 Eurovision Song Contest have confirmed the interview for this week's edition of the show. "A New Tomorrow" is this year's Danish entry for Duesseldorf competing in Semi Final 2. Tim from the group will be chatting with Eric and JP in the studio. 
 
Interview with Nadine Beiler (Austria 2011): Eurovision in Concert 2011 took place recently and Radio International had the pleasure to meet  and interview the representative from Austria at the 2011 Eurovision Song Contest - Nadine Beiler who competes in Semi Final 2 with the song "The Secret is Love". Welcome back Austria after an absence of three years.

Interview with Anna Rossinelli (Switzerland 2011): Austria held the very first National Final of the 2011 Eurovision Song Contest season when in early December 2010 Austria selected Anna Rossinelli to represent the country at the 2011 Eurovision Song Contest in Duesseldorf in May. Switzerland will compete with "In love for a while" in Semi Final 1.

Eurovision Song Contest - In the Spotlight: 2011 National Finals - Chris's Favourites: Chris Poppe is in for Alasdair Rendall this week and takes a look at some of the highlights of the National Finals of this Eurovision pre-selection Season and his prediction to what will end up in the Semi FInals and the Top 10 of the Grand Final of this year's Eurovision Song Contest.
