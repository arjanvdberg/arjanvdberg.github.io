Hightlights of the show

Eurovision Song Contest 2016:  The Eurovision Song Contest 2016 is over and the best ever Eurovision Song Contest was staged in the Globen Arena with two Semi Finals and a Grand Final which was held on Saturday 14 May 2016. The 61st Eurovision Song Contest was won by Ukraine's Jamala with the song "1944". Many fans of the Eurovision Song Contest fall into a deep Post Eurovision Depression as it all is over and the fans part company until the next year. Radio International is the perfect Post Eurovision Depression Cure reflecting on the past contest with interviews and reports and of course the music and remixes. The full results of the contest can be viewed by clicking here. 
 
Interview with Roberto Bellarosa (Belgium 2013): "Love kills" was Belgium's entry to the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 in Malmoe, Sweden performed by Roberto Bellarosa who successfully qualified Belgium from the Semi Final into the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 coming 12th. For Roberto the Eurovision Song Contest was a jumping board and has been going from strength to strength performing and producing a lot of good music. In the run up to the Semi Finals and the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest there are many parties and fan gatherings where previous Eurovision singers perform once again their Eurovision entry plus more. Stockholm's Euroclub held a few of these parties and Roberto Bellarosa was also part of it. The Team of Radio International had the pleasure to once again meet and interview Roberto which you can hear on this week's edition of the show. The interview was done in Roberto's Hotel conducted by Radio International's David Mann.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Eurovision Weekend in Berlin 2016: The Eurovision Weekend in Berlin takes place from Friday 15 - Sunday 27 July 2016. The event is partnered with OGAE Netherlands and OGAE Germany and international fans are more then welcome to attend. On the guest list is Sandra Reemer who represented the Netherlands three times (1972, 1976 and 1979) and was backing vocalist in 1983. Further there is Esther Hart who represented the Netherlands at the Eurovision Song Contest in 2003 and also on the guest list is Zoe who represented as Fans' Favourite Austria at the Eurovision Song Contest 2016. From the German side Laura Pinski will be performing her song from the German National Final 2016 which was won by Jamie-Lee.  Details of the event and how to get tickets are available here.  
 
Eurovision Spotlight XXL - Male Duets at the Eurovision Song Contest: This is the first of the Radio International summer specials and Marcus Keppel-Palmer returns to the airwaves of Radio International with a special Eurovision Spotlight taking a look at the Male Duets at the Eurovision Song Contest. There are many so the entire third hour of the show is dedicated to the Male Duets but also the regular Eurovision Spotlight Spot in the Second Hour will be featuring the first lot. Looking forward to hear Marcus back on the air of Radio International. 
     
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - The Dreaded Number 2 Position:  Through the Summer 2016 the Radio International's Eurovision Experts came up with the idea to take a look at the dreaded starting position of the Euroision Song Contest from where no country ever won the Eurovision Song Contest. Due to a rather special edition of this week's Eurovison Spotlight with Marcus looking at the Male Duets at the Eurovision Song Contest the current series returns next week with Dermot Manning as the series continues and you will be hearing some nice Eurovision oldies as well in this near Eurovision Spotlight. Enjoy the new Radio International Summer series. 

Eurovision Under Cover:  This ever so popular feature on Radio International is David Mann's weekly edition of the Eurovision Cover Versions where David Mann from Eurovision Norwich will be intoducing some of the finest Eurovision Song Contest entries covered by other singers. Just be surprised. You will be hearing the actual song that has been on the Eurovision stage followed by the cover. Listeners are encouraged to send in their cover versions they came across over the years to share with the other fans tuning into the show. Alternatively, listeners can send in their request for a particular Eurovision covered song using this link. 


Eurovision Song Contest News: Nathan Waddell joins the team in the studio every week to bring you the news and the latest developments connected to the Eurovision Song Contest such as the National Finals or if it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc Nathan will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International. He also takes a look at some statistics such as viewing figures in the various countries and more. Nathan is website editor with escxtra.com. In addition, Eurovision News is available on www.eurovision.tv, esctoday.com and escflashmalta.com (amongst others.
