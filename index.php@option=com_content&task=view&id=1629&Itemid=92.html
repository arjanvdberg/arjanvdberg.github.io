Radio International goes to the 2009 Eurovision Gala of OGAE Luxembourg

The Eurovision Club of Luxembourg, the OGAE Luxembourg held their annual Eurovision Gala on 17 October 2009 and invited Monique Melsen who represented Luxembourg at the Eurovision Song Contest in 1971 reaching Number 13 with the song entitled "Pomme Pomme Pomme".


Another guest on the list was the energetic Soraya who represented Spain at the 2009 Eurovision Song Contest in Moscow reaching only 23rd with the great "La noche es para me". Radio International aired a Soraya Special last week which you can listen to by clicking here.

Another great guest of the night was Susanna George who represented Andorra at the 2009 Eurovision Song Contest with the song "Get a life" which unfortunately failed to qualify Andorra for the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest.

And last but not least Flor-de-Lis singer Daniela from Portugal performed her 2009 entry to the Eurovision Song Contest "Todas ruas do amor" which reached Number 15 in the Final.

In this hour you will hear Susanna and Daniela in interview with Radio International and also some of the fans and performers of the show at the 2009 Eurovision Gala of the OGAE Luxembourg. Enjoy!
