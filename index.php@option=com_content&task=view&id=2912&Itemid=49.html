(26 December 2012) Season's Greetings from the Team of Radio International who invites you to the weekly Ultimative Eurovision Experience and this week to the Radio International Christmas Card. Apart from the means of FM Radio in the Netherlands on FM 106.4 and the audio stream from the studio via www.radiointernational.tv and on Digital Cable 41 in the Netherlands, the show will also be web-televised via  the Radio International TV Channel from the studio and linking up the many international Eurovision fans and friends and play your requests and read your messages out on air. Tell your friends to join in - SPREAD THE NEWS. The simulcast will be from 2000 – 2300 hours CET when Eurovision fans can join in and chat amongst each other via the Chat Room and request their favourite Eurovision entry. 

International TV Channel with Chat Room: Radio International launched its own Livestream TV Channel where fans can join and see how we actually do Radio International and listen to the show while chatting with the other Eurovision fans joining the community.  But also you can watch there the many interviews the Team of Radio International has done over the past weeks and also a re-run of the weekly edition of Radio International. Enjoy and tell your friends to join and be part of the Radio International Community!

Please note that many interviews we air are also available as video interview which can be viewed on our Radio International YouTube Channel. Just take a look and enjoy also see the artists talk to us.  
 

The Radio International Eurovision Christmas Card 2012:  With everyone taking off on their well-earned Christmas Break the Team of Radio International is at your service with probably the finest edition of Eurovision Radio International during 2012. Join JP in the studio on FM, Digital Cable, Internet, Livestream and PODCAST, basically the usual means and enjoy three hours of this special Christmas Card from Radio International. Many Eurovision performers will make an appearance during this show singing their favourite Christmas songs spiced with Christmas Comedy spots, and Eurovision entries that have a touch of Christmas in them. Make sure not to miss this special edition on Wednesday, 26 December 2012 from 2000 - 2300 hours CET which will also be the last edition of Eurovision Radio International in 2012.

All our regular correspondents are off on their Christmas Break as well so there are no special features as such - just YOU the MUSIC and JP in the hot seat presenting you your soundtrack for Christmas 2012. The Eurovision News that develops during the Festive Season can be found at the regular Eurovision News Websites listed below.

 

Radio International would also like to take this opportunity to wish our faithful listeners, viewers and fans a Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year and we are looking forward to 2013 when Eurovision Radio International is going strong and also to 52 more great editions of Eurovision Radio International in 2013. Also a special thanks goes to the remote team members of Radio International that have been working hard to deliver input to the show including interviews, ideas and expertise.

 

The Euro Friends Song Contest 2012 - Grand Final: Looking ahead to the first edition of Eurovision Radio International of 2013. The show is scheduled to be broadcast via the normal means on 02 January 2013 from 2000 - 2300 hours CET. Andy Simon and Dove will be joining JP in the studio of Radio International in the Netherlands and devote the entire three hours to the result of the Euro Friends Song Contest 2012 and more. Make sure you are part of it and don't miss a second :)

 
Eurovision Song Contest News:   During the Christmas Break there will be no Eurovision News presentation by Luke Fisher from escXtra, however, Eurovision News is available on www.eurovision.tv, escxtra.com, esctoday.com and escflashmalta.com.  Luke rejoind the team in the studio on 09 January 2013. 

 
Merry Eurovision Christmas and a Happy Eurovision New Year
The Team of Eurovision Radio International
