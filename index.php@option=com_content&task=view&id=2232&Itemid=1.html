Radio International Eurovision Song Contest 2011
Radio International arranged for 43 international juries to cast their votes per Semi Final and eventually on the Grand Final of this year's Eurovision Song Contest entries. A big Eurovision THANK YOU to the juries who have done a fabulous job and an even bigger THANK YOU to Stephen Boddington who was the main man and the driving force behind this special project and also to our Hit Hunter for his editing expertise in putting the clips together.

Just before the Number 1 was played there was a draw amongst the 43 jury members for the 2011 Eurovision Song Contest CD. The lucky winner was Ervin Juhas who represented the Hungarian Jury. Congratulations Ervin!

Now let's see how close the Radio International result matches with the Eurovision Song Contest 2011 result which takes place in May 10, 12 & 14. May the best song win.
