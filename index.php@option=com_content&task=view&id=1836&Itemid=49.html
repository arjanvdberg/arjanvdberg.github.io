Show 31 March 2010

Eurovision Song Contest with Radio International and Oikotimes.
 
Eurovision Song Contest 2010 - Share the Moment - Profiles:  All 39 countries have selected their entry to the 55th Eurovision Song Contest which takes place on 25 & 27 May 2010 for  the Semi Finals and the Grand Final on 29 May 2010. Starting a new series this week Radio International takes a weekly look at the countries' entries profiles where you learn more about the performer and the song.  Ross Bennett will be starting with the first lot of countries:   Albania, Armenia, Azerbaijan, Belarus, Belgium and Bosnia-Herzegovina. This way Radio International will bring you closer the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest entries so you are already a bit familiar with the songs when you see them in the Semi Finals and Final. You can also take a look at the videos of the songs available here.
 
 
Interview with Sweden's Andreas Lundsted from Alcazar.  Radio International's roving reporter - Lilian Brunell had the pleasure to meet up and interview the male part of the group Alcazar who entered the run for the 2010 Melodifestivalen in Sweden with the song "Headline". Radio International already aired an interview a few weeks ago but this week Andreas Lundsted is engaged to review the Eurovision Song Contest entries for 2010. Thank you Lilian for this extensive interview with Andreas. Listen to the interview in three parts. Also click here to view his profile and if you wish to read the interview he gave to Radio International and Oikotimes - click here
 
Eurovision Interview with Gerard James Borg: At the 2010 Malta Eurosong Radio International had the big pleasure in meeting one of Malta's best song writers - Gerard James Borg who every year submits many entries to the Malta's national selection to the Eurovision Song Contest. He is responsible behind the entries of Morena (2008) - "Vodka" and Claudette Pace (2000) - "Desire" just to mention a few. Listen to the interview on Radio International and find out some interesting facts from Gerard. For detailed information such as discography click here.
 
Eurovision Interview with Flor-de-Lis' Daniela Varela. Eric Lehmann & Rene Romkes went to the National Final 2010 in Portugal to witness the proceedings there and had the great pleasure to meet yet again Portugal's great "Ambassador" at the 2009 Eurovision Song Contest  - Daniela Varela who together with her ensemble Flor-de - Lis reached Number 15 in the Final with the great "Todas As Ruas Do Amor". She handed over the responsibility to Felipa to win Eurovision for Portugal.  Listen to the interview and the blooper at the end :) Thanks Eric, Rene and Daniela.
 
Eurovision News. Luke Fisher joins us on a weekly basis to supply us with the latest happenings in the world of the Eurovision Song Contest. Eurovision News is available on www.eurovision.tv, oikotimes.com, esctime.com, escdaily.com, esctoday.com and escmalta.com.   OGAE Malta has a nice history section.  It is called "Eurovision...looking back ". Thea Saliba from Malta in Interview: Thea will we joining Radio International this week for an interview to update us on her career and her latest upcoming musical projects. Thea Saliba represented Malta at the 2005 Junior Eurovision Song Contest as part of Thea and Friends with the song "Make it right". She has visited the studios of Radio International in August 2009 and performed live on the Northsea Festival in the Netherlands.
