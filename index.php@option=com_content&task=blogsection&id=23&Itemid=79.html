Malta's Eurovision Radio
Favourite Radio stations
Malta's Eurovision Radio is a weekly radio show on Malta's Super One Radio 92.7 FM produced by Deo Grech for OGAE Malta since May 2000. This is the longest running radio show featuring Eurovision music and artists from the history and future of the Eurovision Song Contest. It also covers entries from national finals.

"Eurovision Radio" is broadcast live from the studios of  Super ONE Radio in Malta every Wedneday evening from 2045 - 2145 hours CET and listeners have the opportunity to send in their requests via email: This e-mail address is being protected from spam bots, you need JavaScript enabled to view it

Europe Start Listening Now!
Favourite Radio stations

  EurovisiO Radio was launched in December 2009. It is a 24 hours a day Eurovision Internet Radio Station from the heart of Antwerp in Belgium The last regular EurovisiO Show from Antwerp's O-Radio went out on 29 June 2009. Then Philip Vereyken and his team went into heavy work to launch this brand new Eurovision radio service that broadcasts around the clock with loads of Eurovision music old and new and also covers national finals. There are a lot of news items and interviews with Eurovision performers and lots more. The service will be in Flemish with also English parts plus Radio International will also be broadcast via this radio station. The website is available here which also shows a programme schedule.
