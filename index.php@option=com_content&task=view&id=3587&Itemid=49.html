Interview with Chiara (Malta 1998, 2005 & 2009) on the launch of her new single: Malta's singing Pearl Chiara has been signed by Icelandic record lable Hands Up Music which is the same label that works with Hera Bjoerk (Iceland 2010) and were also behind last year's and this year's Icelandic entries to the Eurovision Song Contest. With this Chiara has changed somehow also the music style a little bit from the classic power ballades to club sounds. We of course remember her from her successes at the Eurovision Song Contest 1998 where she came third with "The one that I love" and then in 2005 when she came second with "Angel" and in 2009 she tried it for a third time with "What if we" which reached Number 22. She had releases with "Forget forgetting me" and a duet with Hera Bjoerk "9 to 5" and also "Zarbun". Chiara came to Copenhagen during the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 and launched her new single "Mermaid in love" during a concert in the Euro Fan Cafe. JP met up with the singer and chatted with Chiara about her new single and career development. Chiara's brand new Facebook Page is available here. Listen to the interview on this week's edition of Radio International.  You can watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview with the Common Linnets (The Netherlands 2014): Following the success of Anouk who represented the Netherlands at the Eurovision Song Contest in 2013 and reaching Number 9 in the Grand Final with the song "Birds, the Netherlands once again send in a strong calibre to the contest namely The Common Linnets who are Ilse de Lange and Waylon representing the kindgom with the song entitled "Calm after the storm" in Semi Final 1 at Song Number 14. The Common Linnets sucessfully qualified for the Grand Final, in fact, they were the winners of their Semi Final and then almost won the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 in Copenhagen, if it were not for Austria's beared lady Conchita Wurst taking the trophy. On the evening of Semi Final 2 Radio International as well as other international medias were invited to a recording studio not far from the Eurovision Venue and were given a mini concert which was followed by an interview session. JP had the pleasure to meet the Common Linnets for an interview which you can hear on this week's edition of Radio International You can watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Conchita Wurst (Austria 2014) on the Graham Norton Show in the UK:  After the Eurovision Song Contest all the huge state medias invite the winning act and there is no exception to this year's winner from Austria - Conchita Wurst who as beared lady won the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 with "Rise like a Phoenix". Last week she appeared on the BBC's Graham Norton Show and Radio International picked up on this and would like to broadcast that little interview on Radio International this week. The interview is freely available on YouTube.
