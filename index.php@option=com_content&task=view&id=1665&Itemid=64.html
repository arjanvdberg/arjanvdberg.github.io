Eurovision 2009 Interview Waldo's People (Finland)
Waldo's People represented Finland at the 54th Eurovision Song Contest which came to you from Moscow's Olympic Indoor Arena on 12, 14 & 16 May 2009. Waldo's People performed "Lose control" and could successfully qualify Finland for the Grand Final and received a total of 22 points ranking Finland at Number 25. JP of Radio International together with Alain interviewed Waldo and one of his people to find out more about them. Click here for further information about Waldo's People.
