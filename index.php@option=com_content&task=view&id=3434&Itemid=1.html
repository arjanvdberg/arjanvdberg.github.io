Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2014 - The Voting Results
Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2014 - The Results: As every year Radio International supports the Malta Eurovision Song Contest in which the Maltese Population and an International Jury select the most suitable song to represent the Maltese Islands at the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 in Copenhagen come May. Like in 2013, pbs uses the same format where this year the national selection consists of a Semi Final with 20 songs that is narrowed down to 14 Finalists out of which the Maltese representative to the Eurovision Song Contest will be selected. Gianluca Bezzina won the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2013 with the song "Tomorrow" which also made it sucessfully to the Final of the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 in Malmoe, Sweden last May. Who will succeed the Doctor Gianluca?:) The Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2014 will be televised on Friday, 07 February 2014 (Semi Final) and Saturday, 08 February 2014 (Final) and will be available to watch online as well. escflashmalta.com will keep you up-to-date with the developments.
 
Listeners and viewers to Eurovision Radio International have been kindly submitting their votes and on the show this week you can hear the result. Below is the ranking according to what the listeners and viewers have been voting for during the past couple of weeks. Let's see how close this is with the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2014 result.
