Radio International TV Channel with Chat Room: Radio International launched its own Livestream TV Channel where fans can join and see how we actually do Radio International and listen to the show while chatting with the other Eurovision fans joining the community.  But also you can watch there a re-run of the weekly edition of Radio International. Enjoy and tell your friends to join and be part of the Eurovision Radio International Community!

Please note that many interviews we air are also available as video interview which can be viewed on our Radio International YouTube Channel. Just take a look and enjoy also see the artists talk to us. 
 
Eurovision in Concert 2013:  A 5th and very successful edition of Eurovision in Concert took place Saturday, 13 April 2013 in Melkweg in Amsterdam, The Netherlands where 25 artists of the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 performed and with this promoted their song with which they compete at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 in Malmoe, Sweden May 14, 16 & 18. The Team of Radio International were on location and conducted interviews with some of the artists within a speed interviewing session during the sound check on Saturday afternoon. This and next week Radio International will focus on broadcasting those interviews. At this point Radio International would like to thank David Mann from Eurovision Norwich and Bert Ouwerkerk for their hard work in assisting Eric and JP during the interviews. You can see all the photos from the interview sessions and the Concert itself by clicking here
 
Interview with Marco Mengoni (Italy 2013):  Italy will be represeneted at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 by Marco Mengoni who won the San Remo Song Festival 2013 in Italy earlier on this year. The song is called "L'essenziale" and since Italy is part of the Big Five of the Eurovision Song Contest and with this directly qualified for the Grand Final for Saturday 18 May 2013. Eric met and interviewed Italy's hopeful for this year's Eurovision Song Contest at Eurovision in Concert 2013 and you can hear it on this week's edition of Radio International. You can also watch the video version of the Interview on our YouTube channel by clicking here
 
Interview with Gianluca Bezzina (Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2013):  On Saturday, 02 February 2013 Malta selected their entry to the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 with a young medical doctor by the name of Gianluca Bezzina who will be the Ambassador of Malta in Malmoe waving the Maltese Flag and singing the song "Tomorrow" which won Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2013. Gianluca was also part of the line up of artists performing his country's choice for the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 at the 5th edition of Eurovision in Concert 2013. Since JP already did a fully blown interview with Gianluca in Malta, this little session is something special. Tune in to find out. You can also watch the video version of the Interview on our YouTube channel by clicking here
 
Interview with Ryan Dolan (Ireland 2013):  Following in the footsteps of Jedward to represent Ireland at the Eurovision Song Contest, Ryan Dolan successfully won the Irish National Final in the Late Late Show and will be the Ambassador of Ireland at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013. His song "Only love survives" will be in Semi Final 1 starting at lucky Number 17. JP & Eric met Ryan at Euovision in Concert 2013. You can also watch the video version of the Interview on our YouTube channel by clicking here
 
Interview with Nodi & Sopho (Georgia 2013): Georgia will be represented by Nodi & Sopho with the beautiful ballade called "Waterfall" which will be starting in Semi Final 2 at position Number 15 at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013. Both singers are huge artists in Georgia as a duo but also as soloists. Find out more about the singers by listening to the interview JP did with Georgia's hopefuls at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013.  You can also watch the video version of the Interview on our YouTube channel by clicking here
 
Interview with Moran Mazor (Israel 2013):  Born in Georgia but now living in Israel Moran Mazor will be representing Israel at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 in Semi Final 2 at Number 10 with the song "Rak Bishvilo" which translates to  "Only for him". Eric met the singer in Amsterdam at Eurovision in Concert for an interview.  You can also watch the video version of the Interview on our YouTube channel by clicking here
 
Interview with Gerard Joling (Netherlands 1988):  Special guest at Eurovision in Concert 2013 was Sweden's winner of the 1991 Eurovision Song Contest Carola. Dutch TV took the chance to invite Carola to their weekly TV show called "Life 4 You" which is broadcast out of the legendary Hilverssum studio complex. Also guest was Gerard Joling who represented the Netherlands at the Eurovision Song Contest 1988 with the song "Shangri-la" reaching Number 9 when the Eurovision Song Contest came from Dublin, Ireland with Celine Dion winning for Switzerland with the song "Ne partez pas sans moi".  JP and Eric were backstage with the singer taking the chance of an interview where Gerard also expresses his opinion about the Netherlands at the Eurovision Song Contest.  You can also watch the video version of the Interview on our YouTube channel by clicking here
 
Live Interview with Rick Jacobs - Organiser of the Euro-Fan-Cafe: The Euro-Fan-Cafe is the hang-out for fans, journalists and artists during the days of the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 in Malmoe. Radio International has invited Rick Jacobs from the organising team for an interview what exactly the Euro-Fan-Cafe is and what functions are being planned to entertain the Eurovision Community that will be spending two weeks in Malmoe, Sweden at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013.  
   
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - The 2013 National Finals with Chris Poppe: The National Selection Season 2013 is over and all 39 songs that compete in the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 were chosen. The Eurovision Song Contest 2013 will be staged in Malmö, Sweden on the 14 May 2013 (Semi Final 1),  16 May 2013 (Semi Final 2) and 18 May 2013 (Grand Final) where the 39 countries will be competing for the trophy and the rights to hold the Eurovision Song Contest 2014. Chris Poppe is back from his holidays and will tell us his favourite Eurovision 2013 songs and why.

New Song Releases by Eurovision Artists:  Radio International is always keen to know how the careers of Eurovision performers develop after their performance at the event. The show is in collaboration with many Music Companies that supply Radio International with the latest songs of their signed up artists to bring the muisc closer to the fans. On a weekly basis Radio International receives songs which are being featured on the show. This week new releases by Paula Seling (Romania 2010) and Jezzy Matador & Co (France 2010).
