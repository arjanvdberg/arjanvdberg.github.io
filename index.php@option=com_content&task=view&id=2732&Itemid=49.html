Malta's Fabrizio Faniello releases his European Album in Indonesia:  CAPSOUND is the German Record Label the Maltese Singer Fabrizio Faniello is currently signed with. Due to his mega success in Indonesia his current album "When we dance" has been recorded in the Indonesian language BAHASA and in Indonesia the album is called "Kasmaran". Radio International is proud to play his latest single "Aku Punya Dia" from this album on the show this week. In combination with this  there will be a quiz in which the winner will win a rare copy of the album "Kasmaran" with kind courtesy of CAPSOUND. So make sure you tune in and send your answer to CONTACT by next Tuesday, 24 July 2012. The Quiz Questions are available here.

Interview with Tooji (Norway 2012):  Norway has a record in being on the anchor position at the Eurovision Song Contest and this tradition continued at the 57th Eurovision Song Contest coming from Baku, Azerbaijan. Young energetic Tooji represented Norway with the song "Stay" and after successfully qaulifying for the Grand Final out of the Semi Final Norway ended up at Number 26. Lilian, Roger and JP had the pleasure to meet Tooji for a second interview after his press conference in Baku. Radio International already interviewed Tooji live on the radio on 04 April 2012 in case you wanna flash back and have a listen to that interview.
 
Interview with Izabo (Israel 2012):  Israel was represented at the Eurovision Song Contest 2012 by Izabo. Founded back in 1989 the band enjoys many successes in their home country. The song which Izabo performed at the Eurovision Song Contest is called "Time" and did not do the magic trick and qualify the country for the Grand Final. The song - a 70s retro piece - only reached Number 13 in Semi Final 1. Lilian, Roger and JP met Izabo for an interview right after their first press conference which you can hear in this week's edition of Radio International. Parts of that interview was also televised in Israel as Radio International found out shortly afterwards. Also Shiri from the band gave an interview live on Radio International on 04 April 2012 - in case you want to flash back to that show - you can do that here. 
 
Interview with Litesound (Belarus 2012):  Unfortunately, no success for the Belarussian boyband Litesound who represented Belarus at the Eurovision Song Contest 2012 in Baku, Azerbaijan with the song "We are the heroes" which only reached Number 16 in Semi Final 2 when only the Top 10 advance to the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest. Litesound are brothers Dmitry and Vladimir Karyakin, Jacopo Massa, Alex Kolchin and Ignat Yakovich. On 29 April 2012 Eric and JP previsouly interview Dmitr, Vladimir and Jacopoin Brussels at a promotion event which you can hear here. In Baku, Lilian and JP had the pleasure to meet and interview all five members of the band right after their press conference in the Press Centre. Have a listen to this interview during this week's edition of the show. 
 
More interviews with the 2012 Eurostars will be on the next editions of Radio International. Watch out for them every Wednesday. 
   
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - The Host Entries:  After reflecting on the 57th Eurovision Song Contest over the past weeks Radio International now continuous to present a brand new series with the Eurovision experts in the UK and Ireland to take you through the summer. The new series is taking a look at the entries to the Eurovision Song Contest by the countries that were hosting the contest e.g. this year the Eurovision Song Contest came from Azerbaijan and the Azeri entry = Host Entry is Sabina Babayeva. This week it is the turn of Chris Poppe who will be joining the team in the studio to take a look at the host entries of 1963, 1964, 1973, 1983, 1993 and 2003.
