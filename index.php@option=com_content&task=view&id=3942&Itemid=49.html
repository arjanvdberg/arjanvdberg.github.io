Melodifestivalen 2015 Interviews: Melodifestivalen 2015 took place on Saturday 14 March 2015 in Stockholm's Friends Arena with 12 great artists selected out of the four Semi Finals and the Second Chance round. 28000 people in the Arena and a great pair of show hosts: Sanna Nielsen and Robin Paulsson guiding through the Final of Melodifestivalen. JP and Eric were on location and conducted interviews with many of the artists and producer Christer Bjoerkman. This week's edition of Radio International will be a special on Sweden's selection to the Eurovision Song Contest 2015. Enjoy the show especially this week. 12 acts competed in Sweden's Melodifestivalen 2015 and Eric and JP speed interviewed most of the acts at the Press Conferences after the Dress Rehearsal and after the Grand Final itself. The complete result of Melodifestivalen 2015 can be viewed here. Find a selection of photos from the Melodifestivalen 2015 weekend here. 

Interview with Mans Zelmerloew (Sweden 2015): Who followed Melodifestivalen last Saturday knows that Mans Zelmerloew won the competition with the song "Heroes" and with this will be representing Sweden at the Eurovision Song Contest 2015 in Vienna, Austria and possible follow in the footsteps of Conchita Wurst.  Previously Mans had two attempts in Melodifestivalen with "Cara Mia" in 2007 and "Hope and Glory" in 2009. He also was the host of Melodifestivalen in 2010. Radio International met the singer after the dress rehearsal on Friday night. Listen to this interview and also many more interviews with the competing artists on this week's edition of Radio International. You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here

Interview withe Jon Henrik Fjaellgren (Melodifestivalen 2015): Eric and JP met Jon Henrik at the after show party and chatted with him about his entry and life. The song is called "Jag aer fri" written by Jon Henrik himself and ranked second in Melodifestivalen 2015.




Interview with Eric Saade (Melodifestivalen 2015):  With already being a previous winner of Melodifestivalen back in 2011 Eric Saade represented Sweden at the Eurovision Song Contest 2011 with the song "Popular" ranking third in Duesseldorf. In 2015 Eric Saade returned to Melodifestivalen with the song "Sting" which at the end of the show on Saturday ranked at Number 5. JP met Eric in the Press Centre during the rehearsals on Thursday evening for an interview and also Eric chatted with Eric after the show on Saturday. You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here for the interview before the Final  |  after the Final

Interview with Sanna Nielsen and Robin Paulsson (Hosts of Melodifestivalen 2015): The hosts of this year's Melodifestivalen were absolutely great and a good presentation also lifts the show higher. Sanna Nielsen was the winner of Melodifestivalen 2014 with the song "Undo" and with this Sweden's entry to the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 coming third.  Robin Paulsson is a famous comedian on Swedish television. JP caught up with both during the Thursday night rehearsals. You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here

Interview with Christer Bjoerkman (Executive Producer of Melodifestivalen): The current format of Melodifestivalen is a complete success and was inaugurated the first time I this way back in 2002 and has become the biggest national final of any Eurovision Song Contest country. Christer Bjoerkman who himself represented Sweden at the Eurovision Song Contest in 1992 and also being member of the Reference Group of the Eurovision Song Contest is the Executive Producer of Melodifestivalen. Eric and JP chatted with Christer after the Grand Final of Melodifestivalen to get his assessment of the show. You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here

Further Melodifestivalen 2015 interviews were aired during this week's edition of Radio International with Samir & Viktor, Dinah Nah, Mariette and Magnus Carlsson. Also you can watch all the interviews on the Radio International YouTube Channel once they aired on the radio show.
 
Interview with Mørland & Debrah Scarlett (Norway 2015): While Melodifestivalen 2015 went on in Stockholm's Friends Arena over in Oslo Norway selected their entry to the Eurovision Song Contest 2015. Daniel Koch on behalf of Radio International met up with the winning act Mørland & Debrah Scarlett right after their victory for first reactions on winning the Norwegian final with the song "A Monster like me".
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - The National Final Season 2015:  The National Final Season to the Eurovision Song Contest 2015 is over. Chris Poppe closes out the series taking a look at the final lot of songs that have been selected or nominated in National Finals. Chris will introduce the song from Albania, Armenia, Austria, Australia, Azerbaijan, Israel, Montenegro, Norway, Russia, San Marino and Sweden.
 
Eurovision UnderCover:  This ever so popular feature on Radio International is David Mann's weekly edition of the Eurovision Cover Versions where David Mann from Eurovision Norwich will be intoducing some of the finest Eurovision Song Contest entries covered by other singers. Just be surprised. You will be hearing the actual song that has been on the Eurovision stage followed by the cover. Eurovision Song Contest News: Nathan Waddell joins the team in the studio every week to bring you the news and the latest developments connected to the Eurovision Song Contest such as the National Finals or if it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc Nathan will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International. He also takes a look at some statistics such as viewing figures in the various countries and more. Nathan is website editor with escxtra.com.
