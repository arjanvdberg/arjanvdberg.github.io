Eurovision Song Contest with Radio International and Oikotimes.
   
Eurovision Interview with Eva Rivas (Armenia 2010):  Radio International welcomes the lady from the country of Apricots - Armenia:  Eva Rivas is in interview with Radio International about the song she will be representing her country Armenia with at the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest. "Apricot Stone". The song will be in Semi Final 2 as running number 2.
 
Eurovision Interview with Niamh Kavanagh (Winner of 1993, and Ireland 2010): Radio International is delighted about interview with Niamh Kavanagh, who in 1993 won the Eurovision Song Contest for Ireland. Her entry "In your eyes" received a total of 187 points. In 2010, Niamh becomes a Repeat Offender as she will be representing Ireland once more at the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest with the song "It's for you". Niamh will be in Semi Final 2 as Song Number 12.  
 
Eurovision Interview with Hera Björk (Iceland 2010): Last November Radio International had the great pleasure to meet one of Iceland's top voices - Hera Björk. Hera was backing vocalist for Euroband who represented Iceland at the 2008 Eurovision Song Contest and also the was backing vocalist for Yohanna's "Is it true" in 2009 which became second at the 2009 Eurovision Song Contest in Moscow. But Hera also submitted her own entry to the Danish national selecting also becoming second there with the great "Someday". In November, Hera Björk told Radio International that she had submitted a song for the national final for 2010 with the result that she won it and now will be representing Iceland at the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest in Semi Final 1at Number 17 with the song "Je ne sais quoi". Hera was interviewed by Hans Mekenkamp from Radio Almelo in the Netherlands. Courtesy of Radio Almelo Radio International is pleased to air this interview with Hera Björk.  
 
Eurovision in Concert 2010: Radio International is speaks once again to one of the prime organisers for the upcoming event that is happening this weekend on Saturday, 24 April 2010 near Amsterdam - Eurovision in Concert 2010 with the participation of 17 countries - or more :) We see what the status is for the event and how the day's programme looks like.  For more information click here.     
 
Eurovision Song Contest 2010 - Share the Moment - Profiles:  All 39 countries have selected their entry to the 55th Eurovision Song Contest which takes place on 25 & 27 May 2010 for  the Semi Finals and the Grand Final on 29 May 2010. In the second editon of the new series this week Radio International takes a weekly look at the countries' entries profiles where you learn more about the performer and the song.  Ross Bennett continues with the next lot of countries:   Lithuania, FYRO Macedonia, Malta, Moldova, Netherlands, Norway and Portugal. This way Radio International will bring you closer the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest entries so you are already a bit familiar with the songs when you see them in the Semi Finals and Final. You can also take a look at the videos of the songs available here.
 
Radio International Eurovision 2010 Predictions:  Also starting this week each team member of Radio International  will each week be giving us their prediction which countries they think will be ending up in the Top 10 of the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest. Once the 2010 Eurovision Song  Contest Winner has been announced we can see whose prediction is closest. This week Chris Poppe's prediction for the Top 10 of the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest looks like this:  10th Greece, 9th Serbia, 8th Croatia, 7th Armenia, 6th Azerbaijan, 5th Iceland, 4th Turkey, 3rd Norway, 2nd Ireland, 1st Sweden. 
 
Destination Oslo - An Expert's Opinion - Semi Final 2:  ESCMALTA.com engaged experts to give their opinion about this year’s Eurovision entries. In particular this week and next week Radio International is happy to announce the result of the experts’ opinions during the show of Semi Final 2 - Part 1 (Part 2 is next week). The Experts are former Eurovision performers such as Hanne Haugsand from Charlie McGettigan, Charmed, Paul Giordimaina, Miriam Christine, Sirusho and Pirate of the Sea Roberto Meloni amongst others.
