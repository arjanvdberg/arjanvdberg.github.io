Eurovision Song Contest 2008:

The 2008 Eurovision Season is well underway and together with our Eurovision Expert Marcus Keppel-Palmer we now have weekly previews of the songs that are competing in Belgrade at the Eurovision Song Contest 2008 on 20, 22 & 24 May 2008.

The Previews of the 2008 Eurovision Song Contest entries continues together with Marcus Keppler-Plamer. This week we took a closer look at the song from Bosnia-Herzegowina, Bulgaria, Finland, FYROM and Georgia.
 
Information on Visa for Serbia has been released. The following countries will need to obtain a Visa to enter Serbia. OGAE Serbia is kindly offering their help in issuing invitation letters. Please contact OGAE Serbia via their website for further information. Here are the countries needing a Visa: Turkey, Albania, Azerbaijan, Georgia, Armenia, Moldova, Ukraine, Kazakhstan, Lebanon, South Africa.
No Visa is needed for visitors from all EU countries and Norway, Iceland, Switzerland, Andorra, Liechtenstein, Monaco, San Marino, Russia, Belarus, Croatia, Macedonia, Bosnia, Montenegro, Australia, New Zealand and USA.
