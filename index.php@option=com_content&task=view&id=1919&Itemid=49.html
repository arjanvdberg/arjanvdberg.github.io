Show 16 June 2010
Listen to the Show:       Hour 1     |    Hour 2     |   Hour 3     |    Play List
   
Eurovision Song Contest with Radio International and Oikotimes.
   
Interview with Fabrizio Faniello: News has reached Radio International on the brand new release of the new song of Malta's Fabrizio Faniello who represented his country at the Eurovision Song Contests 2001 ("Another Summer Night", Number 9) and 2006 ("I do", Number 24). After some years of absence from the music scene Fabrizio is back with his brand new offering called "I no can do". Radio International is happy to have the interview with the singer live during our radio show this week. To find out more about Fabrizio Faniello visit his website here
 

Eurovision Song Contest 2010 - Sharing the Moment:  Radio International continues to assess the Eurovision Song Contest with our regular Eurovision Experts and of course play your favourites throughout the show. Also there will be interviews to come with the Eurovision performers. This week experience the way Ross Bennett has experienced the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest as he has been on location heavily working for Radio International and oikotimes.com and he gives us his assessment of this year's edition. 
 
Eurovision 2010 Interview Hera Bjoerk (Iceland 2010): Iceland's entry "Je ne sais quoi" was performed by the great Hera Björk and reached 19th place in the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest. Hera was special guest on the annual Eurovision Convention 2010 of the Dutch Eurovision Artists Foundation on 12 June 2010. JP went and also had the big pleasure to interview the "Icelandic Volcano" on the experience at the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest and her upcoming plans.  You can hear that interview in this week's edition of the show. 
 
Interview with the 2009 Winner of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest: Ralf Mackenbach won the Junior edition of the Eurovision Song Contest 2009 for the Netherlands. Ralf was also at the annual convention of the Eurovision Artists Foundation in Utrecht, The Netherlands,  on 12 June 2010 as a special surprise guest. He performed his winning entry, of course, called "Click Clack, his version of Alexander Rybak's "Fairytale" and his new song.  JP also had the pleasure to interview him after his performance at the convention. Find out more about Ralf by listening to this interview in the show this week.  
 
Eurovision Interview with Alexander Rybak (Norway 2009):  The Winner of the 2009 Eurovision Song Contest - Alexander Rybak - was also present in Oslo to brief the international media on his success and to introduce the trophy of the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest which he handed over to Lena Mayer-Landrut from Germany who won the contest for Germany. JP took the chance of a quick interview to find out more about his upcoming projects and album.  
 
Eurovision News. Luke Fisher joins us on a weekly basis to supply us with the latest happenings in the world of the Eurovision Song Contest. New Release:  Former Maltese entrant to the Junior Eurovision Song Contest (2005) - Thea Saliba has released her brand new song which we had the big pleasure to premier on Radio International this week. The new song is called "I'm your Fan" which was produced and recorded in April 2010. Good luck to Thea and her musical career. To find out more about Thea visit the following website by clicking here.

Your 3 Minutes of Fame on Radio International:  We would also like to know from you your first Eurovision Experience either live in a Final of the Eurovision Song Contest or watching at home or during a Eurovision party. Tell us how you found out about the Eurovision Song Contest. What special things do you remember, liked, disliked and of course your favourite Eurovision Song. We will call you during the show - have a nice chat with you and then play out with your favourite Eurovision song. For that please contact us via CONTACT and let us know your phone number and the song you wish to have played on the show. This will be great fun. We need your participation for this - so please don't be shy. :)  Ervin Juhas from Serbia joins us this week from Serbia to tell us about his greatest Eurovision moments. 

Eurovision Music requests as well as other music requests are most welcome and can be done via our website's CONTACT US page. Let us know what you want to hear and where you are from.
