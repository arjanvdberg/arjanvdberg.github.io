Eurovision Song Contest Interview - Kuunkuiskaajat (Finland 2010):  Radio International's roving reporter Lilian Brunell had the pleasure to meet these crazy fun ladies of Kuunkuiskaajat yet again for a quick follow on interview after Eurovision 2010.  Susan and Johanna from Kuunkuiskaajat represented Finland at the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest just missed by three points the jump into the Grand Final of the 55th Eurovision Song Contest in Oslo, Norway. Their song "Työlki ellää" reached Number 11 in the first Semi Final but that did not let them down as they continued to enjoy the rest of their time in Oslo at the Eurovision Song Contest.  

Euro Friend Song Contest 2010 - Results of Heat 1:  Radio International in collaboration with the Euro Friend Song Contest will reveal the ten qualifiers of Heat 1. For this Radio International once again is joined by the prime organiser of this particular Song Contest based on all the 1195 Eurovision Song Contest entries to date. This annual event was won in 2009 by the runner up of the 2009 Eurovision Song Contest: Iceland's Yohanna and "Is it true". In this week's edition of Radio International Andy Simon will be revealing the ten qualifiers of Heat 1 and it is never to late for listeners to join in the fun and participate in the next heats. Andy will also remind us how the Song Contest works and how interested listeners can actively participate and cast their votes. This is a Face Book event in collaboration with Radio International and you will have to become a member of this particular Face Book Group in order to participate in the voting. Find the link to the Face Book Group here.
 
 
A Eurovision Summer: The summer 2010 is upon us with many heat waves across the lands. Let's cool you with some of the best Eurovision Summer Time tunes. You too can get involved and send in your requests for your favourite Eurovision entry that reminds you of summer time.  To get started Ross Bennett, our Eurovision Song Contest correspondent from the United Kingdom selected some great  Eurovision Summer Time tunes to share with you.

Your 3 Minutes of Fame on Radio International:   This week we are joined by Barrie-John Murphy from the United States of America to tell us his Eurovision experience. But we would also like to know from you your first Eurovision Experience either live in a Final of the Eurovision Song Contest or watching at home or during a Eurovision party. Tell us how you found out about the Eurovision Song Contest. What special things do you remember, liked, disliked and of course your favourite Eurovision Song. We will call you during the show - have a nice chat with you and then play out with your favourite Eurovision song. For that please contact us via CONTACT and let us know your phone number and the song you wish to have played on the show. This will be great fun. We need your participation for this - so please don't be shy. :)
