Show 24 February 2010
Listen to the Show:       Hour 1     |    Hour 2     |   Hour 3     |    Play List
 
Eurovision Song Contest with Radio International and Oikotimes.
 
Every Wednesday from 2000 - 2300 hours Radio International is simulcast live via www.oikotimes.com (TV) where you actually can see us do Radio International. Thank you to all the listener and viewers who join us during the show  sending in their requests and greetings  to other Eurovision fans across Europe.  Join us every Wednesday from 2000 - 2300 hours CET  via the web TV channel of www.oikotimes..com  and enjoy your interaction with the other Eurovision fans and friends in the chatroom while listening to the show.
 
Live Interview with Lauris Reiniks who in 2003 as part of Fly represented Latvia at the Eurovision Song Contest with the great song called "Hello from Mars". Lauris this year will be competing again to represent his country in Oslo at the 55th Eurovision Song Contest. We are glad to have him on our show for an interview. 
 

Malta's Eurosong 2010: The Team of Radio International were on location at the MFCC (Malta's Fairs and Convention Centre) in Ta-Qali and witnessed the proceedings of how the Maltese select their representative for the 55th Eurovision Song Contest. 17 year old Thea Garrett will represent Malta in Oslo with the fabulous song called "My Dream". Glen Vella became second with "Just a little more love". JP is joined by Eric Lehmann from OGAE Luxembourg to together assess the show and also bring you a couple of interviews with the winner - Thea Garrett. After the  Malta Eurosong 2010 show JP & Eric sneaked into the Green Room with a webcam and interviewed various performers and composers. You can see and hear it on oikotimes.tv by clicking here. 
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - National Finals: Our regular Eurovision Spot with our correspondents Marcus, Ross, Chris and Alasdair. For the next few weeks we take a look at the national selection processes in the Eurovision countries. Week by week we will check out the entries of various entries that have qualified in their national finals and give you a taster by playing the clip. Soon we know which songs have been selected by juries and televoters representing their country at the Eurovision Song Contest 2010 in Oslo. This week Marcus Keppel Palmer will be taking a look and play clips of the songs selected this week from Armenia, Poland, FYR Macedonia, Spain and Slovenia.


Interviews with Melodifestivalen participants 2010 in Sweden:  This week sees the final in the series of interviews Radio International's rowing reporter Lilian Brunell in Stockholm has conducted with potential Swedish Representatives at the Eurovision Song Contest 2010. This week listen to the interview with Sibel.
