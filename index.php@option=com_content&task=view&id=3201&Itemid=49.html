A Eurovision Summer with Radio International:  Eurovision Radio International does not stop throughout the summer time and is keen on keeping the music of the Eurovision Song Contest in the ears of the many fans that join the show each and every week via the usual means either via PODCAST, LISTEN AGAIN, live broadcast or Live Stream. LIVE Interview with Gianluca Bezzina (Malta 2013):  After last week's amazing show with live interviews with Daria Kinzer, Stella Jones and Eurovision 2013 Host Petra Mede Radio International is extremely delighted yet again to have Eurovision's Doctor Feelgood live on the show this week for an interview. Gianluca Bezzina and his team archived a remarkable  8th place at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 in Malmoe for Malta with the song "Tomorrow". It is very recently that Gianluca has released his album "Waiting for tomorrow" which also is part of the interview subject on this week's edition of Radio International when chatting with the artist and you get a little pre-listen to the songs as well. If you like the album you can buy it at this link.
 
Interview with Lys Assia (Winner 1956, Switzerland 1957 & 1958): 89 year old Grand Dame of the Eurovision Song Contest - Lys Assia was part of the line up of the Stockholm Pride Eurovision Concert on 01 August 2013 where she performed her winning song of the very first edition of the Eurovision Song Contest 1956 "Refrain". Lys Assia or Rosa Mina Schärer has been the first Swiss artist to enter the Eurovision Song Contest on three consequetive years namely 1956 where she won, but then returned in 1957 and 1958. The Team of Radio International was at the concert on Stockholm and had the pleasure to meet and chat with the Mother of the Eurovision Song Contest. Hear the chat on this week's edition of the show.  You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview Ryan Dolan (Ireland 2013): Also being part of the guest line up of the Stockholm Pride Eurovision Concert was Ireland's Ryan Dolan who with "Only love survives" represented Ireland at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 reaching Number 26 in the Grand Final in Malmoe earlier on this year. Ryan's album "Frequency" is doing extremely well in many European countries but also Down Under in Australia. JP and Eric chatted with the singer at the Concert on his career after the participation of the Eurovision Song Contest. Listen to the interview during this week's edition of Radio International. Fans who would like to meet Ryan as well as Linda Martin (Eurovision Winner 1992) can do that by registering at the upcoming Eurovision Club Germany's Annual Convention in Cologne, Germany on 23 November 2013 by clicking here.    You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview with Roberto Bellarosa (Belgium 2013): The Kingdom of Belgium was represented by young Roberto Bellarosa at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 with the song "Love Kills" which successfully qualified out of Semi Final 1 for the Grand Final where the song ranked at Number 12. Originating from "The Voice" in Belgium  Roberto was interviewed by JP and Eric at this year's Stockholm Pride Eurovision Concert in which the singer talks about his career after the Eurovision Song Contest participation. Also with this interview we see a new look Roberto (see photo) and listen to the interview in this week's edition of Radio International. You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview with Valentina Monetta (San Marino 2012, 2013, 2014):  San Marina is rather new at the Eurovision Song Contest starting to take part in 2008. Valentina Monetta is produced by Germany's Ralph Siegel who at the Eurovision Song Contest 2012 wrote "The Social Network Song" and in 2013 together with Mauro Bellestri the fantastic "Crisalide" that to date is San Marino's best result at the Eurovision Song Contest reaching Number 11 in the Semi Final in Malmoe. Valentina, Ralph and Maura are preparing the participation for a third time for 2014. The San Marinese singer was also part of the Stockholm Pride Eurovision Concert on 01 August 2013 and the Team of Radio International was delighted to interview with singer which you can hear on this week's edition of Radio International.  You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here  
 
Interview with Krista Siegfrids (Finland 2013):  A bundle of energy from Finland and a lot of fun is this year's Finnish entry to the Eurovision Song Contest - Krista Siegfrids who successfully qualified Finland for the Grand Final in Malmoe and then achieved Number 24 with the song "Marry me". The Radio International rowing Skandinavian reporter Lilian Brunell met Krista on the day of the Eurovision Concert being part of the Stockholm Pride and interviewed the singer. You can hear this funny interview on this week's edition of Radio International.   You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here
