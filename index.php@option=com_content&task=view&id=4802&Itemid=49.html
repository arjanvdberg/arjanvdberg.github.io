Hightlights of the show

A Eurovision Christmas 2016:  Since Christmas is just around the corner and Radio International would like to get you into the mood for the Festive Season some special songs are inserted into this week's edition of the show. Eurovision artists are singing our favourite Christmas songs and JP want you to figure out who these artists are. Email into the show and you can win a prize. Every one that takes part and takes a right guess wins something. Among the Christmas bits during the show there will also be the usual things and more. For details see below.  
 
Interview with Aminata (Latvia 2015):   After 20 artists went through two Heats, a Semi Final and a Final the winner of the SUPERNOVA 2015 became Aminata who represented Latvia at the Eurovision Song Contest 2015 in Vienna, Austria with the song "Love injected" which came 6th in the Eurovision Song Contest 2015. Aminta is also a songwriter and has among others also written "Heartbeat" performed by Justs as the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 in Stockholm last May reaching Number 15. Before Eurovision 2016 Aminata went to the London Preview Event where Radio International's David Mann had the pleasure to chat with her.  Listen to this interview on this week's edition of Radio International.
 
Interview with Sean Banan (Melodifestivalen 2012 and 2013, Sweden):  After Melodifestivalen 2016 in the Friends Arena near Stockholm there is always an amazing Aftershow Party where the current and previous artists that took part in Melodifestivalen pay a visit and celebrate the music. Also this year there was no exception as Sean Banan appeared at the Aftershow Party. In 2012 Sean Banan took part for the first time in Melodifestivalen with the song "Sean de Foerste Banan" which unfortunately did not make it into the Final in 2012. However, in 2013 Sean Banan's offering "Copacabanana" made it straight to the Final from the Semi Final. He delivered a brilliant show in the Final and with this ended up at Number 6 of Melodifestivalen 2013. Eric and JP chatted with the funny singer and actor during the Aftershow Party of Melodifestivalen 2016. Enjoy this interview in this week's edition of Radio International.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here  

Interview with Dinah Nah (Melodifestivalen 2015, Number 12):  Like Sean Banan, Dinah Nah was part of the Aftershow Party of Melodifestivalen 2016, although she took part the year previously in 2015 with the song "Make me la la" which came 12th in the Final of Melodifestivalen 2015. Eric had the pleasure to chat with Dinah Nah during the Aftershow Party. Have a listen on the show this week. You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here 
 
Interview with Dunja (Junior Eurovision 2016, Serbia, Number 17): This year, Serbia was represented at the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2016 by the young Dunja and her team with a song that was written by Vladimir Graic called "Uh la la la". Vladimir is a huge composer and musician in Serbia and is responsible among other hits for the winning song of the Eurovision Song Contest 2007 "Molitva" performed by Marija Serifovic. Unfortunately, Dunja only reached Number 17 in the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2016 with this catch ear-worm song "Uh la la la". JP had the pleasure to speak with Dunja and her team after her Press Conference. Listen to this interview on this week's edition of Radio International.  Please note that due to lack of time the broadcast of this interview is postponed to January 2017. Sorry for the inconvenience.

Eurovision 2017 - The National Final Season: Armenia:  JP will be joined on this week's edition of Radio International by Olivier Vanhoutten who will be talking to us about the national selection process for the Eurovision Song Contest 2017 in Armenia. You will hear a couple of tunes being covers of Eurovision songs by the competing acts in the Armenian competing in their National Final. Also we will be going through some of the Armenian Eurovision History. 
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - Chris Poppe's Favourite National Final Songs:  Two weeks ago we finished the mini series on Radio International as our Eurovision correspondents took a look at the Eurovision entries from Ukraine since Ukraine will be hosting the Eurovision Song Contest 2017 following  Jamala's victory in Stockholm at the 61st edition of the contest with the song "1944". Ukraine initially won the Eurovision Song Contest in 2004 after their second attempt as Ukraine only joined the Eurovision family back in 2003. The Winning Song of Ukrain's first victory was "Wild Dances" performed by Ruslana when the Eurovision Song Contest 2004 came from Istanbul, Turkey. Just recently, it was decided that the Eurovision Song Contest 2017 will be hosted once again by Ukraine's capital city Kiev on May 09 & 11 for the Semi Final 1 and 2 and the Grand Final to take place on May 13. Last week Ally Rendall played his favourite entries from the Eurovision Song Contest 2005 as Kiev was the host city back then and this week the Eurovision Spotlight does something completely different. As it being the last Eurovision Spotlight of 2016 Chris Poppe of Radio International will be taking a look at some of his personal favourites of the National Finals to the Eurovision Song Contest. In the first editions of Radio International of 2017 we will be starting the National Final Season 2017. 

Eurovision Under Cover:  This ever so popular feature on Radio International is David Mann's weekly edition of the Eurovision Cover Versions where David Mann from Eurovision Norwich will be intoducing some of the finest Eurovision Song Contest entries covered by other singers. Just be surprised. You will be hearing the actual song that has been on the Eurovision stage followed by the cover. Listeners are encouraged to send in their cover versions they came across over the years to share with the other fans tuning into the show. Alternatively, listeners can send in their request for a particular Eurovision covered song using this link.

Eurovision Song Contest News: Nathan Waddell joins the team in the studio every week to bring you the news and the latest developments connected to the Eurovision Song Contest such as the National Finals or if it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc Nathan will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International. He also takes a look at some statistics such as viewing figures in the various countries and more. Nathan is website editor with escxtra.com. In addition, Eurovision News is available on www.eurovision.tv, esctoday.com and escflashmalta.com (amongst others.
