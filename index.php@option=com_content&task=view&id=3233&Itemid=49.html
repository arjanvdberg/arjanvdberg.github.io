LIVE Interview with Kurt Calleja (Malta 2012):  The sunny country and islands of Malta were represented at the Eurovision Song Contest 2012 by Kurt Calleja and his team on stage with the song "This is the night" qualifying his country for the Grand Final and there reaching Number 21. Kurt has been attempting various times to represent Malta at the Eurovision Song Contest previously but 2012 was the year for him. Radio International is extremely delighted to once again be able to welcome Kurt to the show this week live from Malta and have a chat about his time at Eurovision 2012, afterwards and also the development of his musical career including the release os his new album entitled "Love on Mars" which Kurt will also be chatting about with JP in the studio. You can order the physical album and/or download the Itunes version soon. Contact the Team of Kurt on his facebookpage which you can reach here. 
 
Interview with Alex Panayi (Cyprus 1995 & 2000): The Team of Radio International while being on location at the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 in Malmoe in Sweden had the pleasure to meet accidentally one of the previous Eurovision performers for Cyprus - Alex Panayi - who was hired by one country to do the vocal coaching for their artists. Eric and JP of Radio International took the chance to chat with the singer about his performances at the Eurovision Song Contest whenhe represented Cyprus twice in 1995 with the song "Sti Fotia" reaching Number 9 and then he returned in 2000 for Cyprus together with Christina Argyri performing "Nomiza" reaching Number 20 in the Grand Final. But also he was part of the winning team when Helena Paparizou won the Eurovision Song Contest 2005 with the song "My Number One". Alex was one of the baking vocalists. Listen to this interview during this week's edition on Radio International and information has been released lately that Alex Panayi is also part of the guest list of the 5th Annual Eurovision Live Concert 2013 in Setubal, Portugal on 05 - 07 September 2013 which is your ideal chance to meet the singer.  You can watch this interview also on our YouTube Channel - click here

This week Ross Bennett continues the series with the countries Portugal, Romania, Russia and San Marino.
