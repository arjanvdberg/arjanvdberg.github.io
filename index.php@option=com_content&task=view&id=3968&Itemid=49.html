Eurovision's Greatest Hits: On 31 March 2015, the BBC  in collaboration with Swedish TV SVT celebrated the 60th anniversary of the Eurovision Song Contest and  produced a Eurovision Celebration Show staged in London's Eventi, Apollo, Hammersmith. The show is called "Eurovision's Greatest Hits" and featured a large number of previous Eurovision Winning acts such as Loreen, Emmelie de Forest, Lordi, etc. Details of the event can be seen here . To coincide with this event Radio International last week and also this week will air a large number of Eurovision Greatest Hits during the show but also on last week's show Radio International aired an interview done in London with John Kennedy O'Connor who published a book  "The Eurovision Song Contest: The Official Celebration" which will be published on 20 April 2015. In case you missed this interview just visit the Show Archive from 01 April 2015 and listen to the show for the interview. 

EBU's Lynda Woodruff interviews the Eurovision's Greatest Hits Performers:  One of the funniest characters to appear on the Eurovision screen is EBU's Lynda Woodruff who we met first in 2012. Lynda also appeared in London to interview some of the performing acts in Eurovision Greatest Hits in London. Radio International will air some of that on the show this week. 
 
Live Interview with Martina Majerle (Slovenia 2009 ): Joining the show live from Slovenia via phone will be Martina Majerle who initially is a backing vocalist and with this has been singing with Eurovision artists such as Severina, Doris Dragovic, Sergej Cetkovic, Nina Badric and Novi Fosili just to mention a few. She has been on the Eurovision stage for 7 times first with Claudia Beni in 2003, in 2008 and 2014 for Montenegro amd four times for Slovenia. Then in 2009 she was on the "frontline" with the group Quartissimo singing at the Eurovision Song Contest in Moscow the song "Love Symphony" which ranked 16th in Semi Final 2. Martina Majerle took part at the Slovenian National Final EMA trying to win the ticket to the Eurovision Song Contest 2015 in Vienna with the song "Alive" written by Andre Babic. Radio International is delighted to welome Martina Majerle to the show this week. What a charming lady - you will like listening to this interview with Martina. Her Facebook Page is available here. 

Eurovision in Concert 2015 - Interview with Rene Romkes: The 7th edition of Eurovision in Concert in Amsterdam, Netherlands is going to be held on Saturday, 18 April 2015 in the venue Melkweg with the participation over 20 countries that are competing at the Eurovision Song Contest 2015 in Vienna, Austria come May 2014. Countries that accepted the invitation by the organizers are Albania, Austria, Australia, Azerbaijan, Belarus, Cyprus, France, FYR Macedonia, Georgia, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Israel, Latvia, Lithuania, Moldova, Montenegro, Netherlands, Norway, Serbia, Slovenia, Sweden and Special Guests: Bobbysocks from Norway. Radio International is delighted to be be chatting with one of the prime organisers of the event who makes his annual appearance on the show every year to brief us on the event - Rene Romkes. Further details of the event can be found on the website by clicking - here. 
 
Venezuela's Top 50 Eurovision Songs of 2014:  Every year the Eurovision fans of Venezuela get together and vote for the Eurovision songs of the current year and 2014 was no exception.  The voters not only took into consideration the songs that were in the Semi Finals and Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 but also the National Finals and the Junior Eurovision Song Contest of 2014.  Rafael Vivas sent in the Top 50 chart to Radio International for broadcast.  Over the next weeks Radio International will be broadcasting these songs and running down the chart during the broadcast.  What did Venezuela vote for to be the best song of the 2014 Eurovision year?  Find out on Radio International. Due to lack of time on the show this week there was no chance to play on songs from the list, however, for the future editions of the show the chart will be taken into consideration. 

Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - Eurovision 2015 in Regions:  For the next few weeks the Eurovision Experts of Radio International will be taking a look at the 2015 Eurovision entries per region and this week Dermot Manning continues with the central Eurovision countries that are competing at the Eurovision Song Contest 2015. This week hear the songs from Belarus, Estonia, Germany, Latvia, Lithuania and Poland. In addition, each week our correspondents will be asked to tell us their personal favourite but also their tip what could actually win the Eurovision Song Contest 2015.
