Hightlights of the show

A Eurovision Summer 2017:  The Summer of 2017 has official begun and Radio International wants to keep Eurovision music in the ears of the many fans around the Eurovision. Also there are still some interviews from Eurovision 2017 left for broadcast which you will hear on the show in the weeks to come.The Eurovision Song Contest 2017 took place in Kyiv's International Exhibition Centre, Ukraine on 09 & 11 May 2017 with the two Semi Finals and the Grand Final on Saturday, 13 May 2017. Congratulations go to Salvador and Luisa Sobral for winning the Eurovision Song Contest for Portugal for the very first time with the song "Amar pelos dois (Love for both of us)". Over the next few weeks into the summer of 2017 Radio International provides the perfect Post Eurovision Depression Cure (PED) and broadcasts many of the interviews on the shows to come with the Eurovision artists from Kyiv and more. Enjoy and don't be sad - the next Eurovision is around the corner. :)
 
Live Interview with Jimmie Wilson (San Marino 2017):  Radio International is delighted to welcome to the show live this Wednesday Eurovision representative of San Marino 2017, although originally from Detroit, USA Jimmie Wilson who together with four times San Marinese Eurovision entrant Valentina Monetta, performed the song "Spirit of the Night" which unfortunately did not qualify San Marino for the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest. Jimmie just released his new album and Radio International is dying to find out more about that album but as well Jimmie's time at the Eurovision Song Contest in Kyiv this year. Enjoy this live interview on the show this week. 
 
Eurovision Weekend in Berlin 2017: The Eurovision Weekend in Berlin takes place from Friday 14 - Sunday 16 July 2017. The event is partnered between OGAE Malta and OGAE Germany and international fans are more then welcome to attend. On the guest list is Kurt Calleja (Malta 2012), Claudia Faniello (Malta 2017) and the event is co-hosted by Glen Vella (Malta 2011). Further there will be the runner up to the Germany National Selection "Unser Song 2017" - Axel Maximilian Feige. But also special guest is this year's Musical Ambassador to the Czech Republic: Martina Barta who performed the song "My Turn" at the Eurovision Song Contest 2017 in Kyiv. Salman Tanzem of the organising team in Berlin will be joining JP live during this week's edition of Radio International.  Details of the event and how to get tickets are available here.  

Interview with Norma John (Finland 2017):  One of the most outstanding songs with a mystic touch at the Eurovision Song Contest 2017 came from Finland's band Norma John who represented the song "Blackbird" which unfortunately did not make it into the Grand Final. Finland ranked 12th in Semi Final 1. JP had the pleasure to already chat with Lasse and Lena of Norma John at the London Preview Event earlier on in 2017. Then for a quick update in Kyiv  JP talked to both at the Nordic Party in Kyiv. Listen to the interview from Kyiv on this week's edition of Radio International.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here  
 
Eurovision Spotlight - The Show Starters:  As every year the Radio International Eurovision Experts were brainstorming what should be the summer feature during Eurovision Radio International throughout the summer of 2017 and the team thought let's reflect on the songs that started off the Eurovision Song Contest in each year. 62 editions of the Eurovision Song Contest were staged over the years and there has to be always the first song in the Running Order of the Eurovision Song Contest. This week Ross Bennett continues the new series. It will be nice to hear some long forgotten classics again on the show in the weeks to come.  
 
Eurovision Under Cover:  This ever so popular feature on Radio International is David Mann's weekly edition of the Eurovision Cover Versions where David Mann from Eurovision Norwich will be intoducing some of the finest Eurovision Song Contest entries covered by other singers. Just be surprised. You will be hearing the actual song that has been on the Eurovision stage followed by the cover. Listeners are encouraged to send in their cover versions they came across over the years to share with the other fans tuning into the show. Alternatively, listeners can send in their request for a particular Eurovision covered song using this link.

Eurovision Song Contest News: Nathan Waddell joins the team in the studio every week to bring you the news and the latest developments connected to the Eurovision Song Contest such as the National Finals or if it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc Nathan will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International. He also takes a look at some statistics such as viewing figures in the various countries and more. Nathan is website editor with escxtra.com. In addition, Eurovision News is available on www.eurovision.tv,
