OGAE France Eurovision Convention (17 - 19 April 2009)
Once again, OGAE France is getting ready for its spring events. The first one will be a Europarty in one of the oldest, atypical Parisian discos, Le Tango, on Friday, 17 April 2009. From 2230 - 0500 hours CET, Eurovision songs will be played and a show is planned to be made by the staff. The entrance fee is EURO 8. 
 
Then on Sunday, 19 April 2009 the second event  of the spring meeting with OGAE France gives the opportunity to watch all the videos of the entries taking part in the upcoming Eurovision Song Contest and vote for one’s favourites. The videos will be shown in a big amphitheater on a huge cinema-like screen.
 
Furthermore, Sandrine François (France 2002) will be the special guest.   
 
The programme of the day:
  • 10:00 - Semi final 1    
  • 11:30 - Semi final 2    
  • 12:30 - Voting to select ten songs from each semi final   
  • 14:30 - Final with 25 songs    
  • 16:00 - Discussion with Sandrine François    
  • 17:00 - Voting to elect the French favorite for 2009    
  • 18:00 - End of the day with some Sandrine François’ songs
