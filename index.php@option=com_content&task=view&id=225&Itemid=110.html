Links to other important Eurovision Sites

Please find below a list compiled with the most important Eurovision links you should visit on a regular basis.

  • eurovision.tv   is the official website of the Song Contest.
  • escXtra.com - a great Eurovision News Website with quick updates
  • W12P - Wales 12 Points from Mumbles in Wales - Eurovision Radio Show
  • songcontest.ch - a complete website of all tables and lists. (in the German language)
  • ESCdaily -  Eurovision New Website.
  • ESCtoday.com has the up to the minute Eurovision News 24/7.
  • European Broadcastig Union (EBU).
  • OGAE International Network - a list of all OGAEs and other Fan Clubs of Eurovision.
  • Eurovision Artists (EA) - The Dutch Eurovision Fan Club Website.
  • Eurosong.be - The Belgian Eurovision Website.
  • Eurovivisonary - A nice and new Eurovision News Website operating from Denmark.
  • 12 points.tv (former 3Minutes) - A sparkling Eurovision Website.
  • escflashmalta.com - Malta's biggest Eurovision Website.
  • OGAE UK - The UK's Eurovision Fan Club.
  • BBC Radio 2 Eurovision website.
  • OGAE Germany. The Fan Club Website for the German Fans.
  • Eurovision Club Germany (EC Germany e.V.).
  • Eurovision.de - Germany's NDR TV's website on the Eurovision Song Contest.
  • OGAE Switzerland. That's where it all started in 1956.
  • Eurovision Song Contest - A very informative Swiss/German website.
  • The EUROVISION Database. One of the first great Eurovision websites.
  • Diggiloo Thrush is a site with almost all the lyrics and more information on Eurovision. Nice website!
  • Eurovision Forum in Greece. There you can share your opionion about the great Eurovision Song Contest. 
  • BEA RECORDS is the official Eurovision Record Dealer. Whatever your needs are on Eurovision Music BEA Records will get it for you.
  • Eurovision.web-log.nl which is a Dutch / Belgian Eurovision website .
  • Lilian's Eurovision Blogspot (Sweden / Finland).
  • ESC Radio - broadcasts Eurovision music 24/7.
  • Eurovision Song Contest National Finals Home Page 
  • EurovisiOradio.eu - Eurovision Radio station broadcasting 24 hours a day Eurovision music, news and information. 
  • Eurovision-DVD.com - Is an online shop that sells all sorts of Eurovision DVDs and CD.
  • Eurovision Week - Is a Eurovision Blog from Poland - welcome to the family :)
  • ESC Alltime - Is a Eurovision Blog with video clips per year. 
  • ESC Insight - Is the Home of the unofficial Eurovision Song Contest Podcast - check it out!
  • The ESC Insider - A great Eurovision Blog created by Samantha Ross from the USA!!!
  • ESC Chat - Discovered this lately is another nice Eurovision website to be checked now and then.
  • The Eurovision Experience by Michael Goodrich from the USA.
  • escnation - Eurovision News website but also with chat option and publish events
  • WiwiBloggs - Eurovision news, polls and predictions — with attitude!
  • escXtra - The new Xtra Eurovision News Website
  • Eurofest - A Eurovision Party Website from London Town, UK
  • EurovisionIreland - New website from Ireland founded in 2012
  • Eurovision Canada - A Canadian Perspective on the Eurovision Song Contest
  • Eurovisionlive - This website is one of the oldest ESC websites and offers valuable insight information, video-interviews and a lot of history.
  • Eurovision Norwich - The Eurovision Blog of our own version of "David Colin Berry Mann".

The All EUROVISION HITS DATABASE contains all the Eurovision Hits that ever existed. (without pre-selections).
