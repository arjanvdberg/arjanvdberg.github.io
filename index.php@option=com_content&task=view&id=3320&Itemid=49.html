Through the Autumn 2013 with Radio International:  Eurovision Radio International never stops and is keen on keeping the music of the Eurovision Song Contest in the ears of the many fans that join the show each and every week via the usual means either via PODCAST, LISTEN AGAIN, live broadcast or Live Stream. Julie Zahra (Malta 2004) releases new EP:  Julie Zahra is 50% of the Maltese entry to the Eurovision Song Contest 2004 as Julie and Ludwig performed "On again...off again"  in Istanbul reaching Number 12. Julie has moved to London and is still involved in music and in fact will be releasing a brand new EP on 18 November 2013, however, Radio International had already a glimpse of one of the songs which was played on this week's edition of Radio International. To find out more about the EP please visit Julie's website by clicking here . 
 
Interview with Nikki of El & Nikki (Eurovision Winner 2011):  Our UK rowing reporter David Mann had the big pleasure to meet half of the winning duo from Azerbaijan who won the Eurovision Song Contest 2011 in Duesseldorf with the song "Running scared" and with this taking the Eurovision Song Contest to Baku, Azerbaijan in 2012. El & Nikki are recording material together but also having their own projects producing music and performing at various events. Eldar or El will be part of the Eurovision Club Germany's Annual Convention 2013 in Cologne Germany on 23 November 2013 and Nikki or Nigar lives mainly in London. Earlier on this year David met Nikki at a Eurovision event and did a nice interview with her which Radio International is very happy to share with the listeners during this week's edition of the show. 
 
Interview with Claudia Faniello (Malta Eurovision Song Contest - various): One of Malta's best singers and also sister of Fabrizio Faniello who represented Malta twice at the Eurovision Song Contest in 2001 and 2006 is Claudia Faniello who has been attempting various times to get that ticket to represent the Maltese Islands at the Eurovision Song Contest and with that following in the footsteps of her brother. In total, 11 times she took part in the national selection process with very good results but never made it to the top. Her latest attempt was at the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2013 with the song "When it's time" coming 7th. In 2012, she came second with the song "Pure".  Hopefully, she will be trying again and then have that little bit of additional luck. More details about Claudia Faniello you can find here. The Team of Radio International had the pleasure to meet and interview the singer again with an update of her career which you can hear during this week's edition of Radio International.   You can watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here 
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - Denmark's Eurovision Song Contest History:  A new quarter has begun which also marks the start of a new series called Denmark's Eurovision Song Contest History since Denmark for the Eurovision Song Contest 2013 and with this will be hosting the 2014 edition of the contest in Copenhagen the Team of Radio International thought to continue the tradition to reflect on all the Danish Eurovision entries over the years. Denmark has won the Eurovision Song Contest three time. It was first in 1963 when Grethe and Jorgen Ingmann performed "Dansevise", then the Olson Brothers won in 2000 with the song "Fly on the wings of love" and then in 2013 with Emmelie de Forest and "Only Teardrops". This week Ross Bennett continuous the series looking back at the Eurovision entries from Denmark. 
 
Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013:  The 11th and 2013 edition of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest will be staged at Palace of Ukraine in Kiev, Ukraine on 30 November 2013 after last year's victory of Anastasiya Petryk who won for the Ukraine with the song "Nebo". So far 11 countries have confirmed their participation of young artists from: Armenia, Azerbaijan, Belarus, Belgium, Georgia, Macedonia, Malta, Moldova, Netherlands, Russia, San Marino, Sweden and Ukraine competing for the trophy of the event. Consult the offical event's website Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013 for all the latest news concerning the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013 and also you will also find all detailed information about the young artists. Members of the Team of Radio International will be present at the event and conduct interviews with the young artists which you can hear during the shows around the 04 December 2013. To get us into the mood for the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013 Radio International welcomes JESC music requests from the listeners and viewers. CONTACT us for that.
