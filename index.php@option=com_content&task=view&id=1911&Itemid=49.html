Show 09 June 2010
Listen to the Show:       Hour 1     |    Hour 2     |   Hour 3     |    Play List
 
Eurovision Song Contest with Radio International and Oikotimes.
   
EUROVISION 2010 - We Shared the Moment: Still in celebration mood - for the next few weeks Radio International will be assessing the Eurovision Song Contest with our regular Eurovision Experts and of course play your favourites throughout the show. Also there will be interviews to come with the Eurovision performers. Sadly, for the last time in this slot Marcus Keppel Palmer will be giving us his assessment of this year's edition of the Eurovision Song Contest. Fear not - Marcus returns to the airwaves with a special series of programmes on Radio International. 
 
Eurovision 2010 Interview with Romania's Paula Seling & Ovi: Romania's entry "Playing with fire" was performed by Paula Seling & Ovi and reached a fantastic third place in the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest. You can hear that interview in this week's edition of the show. 
 

Dutch Eurovision Convention - 12 June 2010, Interview with the organiser:   The Foundation of Eurovision Artists - www.eurovisionartists.nl - will be holding their annual Summer's Day to end the 2010 Eurovision Season. The event takes place in Utrecht's Jacobi Theatre. Special guest is Iceland's Hera Björk and a surprise Eurovision Song Contest Winner :) Who will that be ???? Jaap van der Kaaden, the PR Manager and prime organiser of this event is in interview with Radio International to bring you the details. Listen to the show but also further information is available on the website of Eurovision Artists. 
 
Quizz - Europe Start Listening Now: Our colleagues from www.eurovisOradio.eu broadcast 24/7 Eurovision items and Philipe Vereyken hosts "Europe Start Listening Now? on Monday evenings. There will be a special quiz coming up for next Monday's edition where listeners can win the album of Lena "My Cassette Player" by answering the following question: "In the national German Final Lena performed three songs. "Satellite" won, but do you remember the other two songs?"
The second question when answering correct will bring you the double CD of this year's Eurovision Song Contest by simply answering "How many tracks are on this years double CD of the Eurovision Song Contest"?
You have to be in it to win it and you can contact Philip until Monday evening during his show via the email address: This e-mail address is being protected from spam bots, you need JavaScript enabled to view it
 
Eurovision News. Luke Fisher joins us on a weekly basis to supply us with the latest happenings in the world of the Eurovision Song Contest. Eurovision News is available on www.eurovision.tv, oikotimes.com, esctime.com, escdaily.com, esctoday.com and escflashmalta.com.   Listen to a pick of the Eurovision News also on Radio International:
  • Serbia - Milan Stankovic wins the 2010 Barbara Dex Award
  • France - France wants to get rid of the juries
  • European Broadcasting Union - Jury Votes & Televoting  Separations to be published by EBU within two weeks
  • Germany - Lena charts in the UK Charts, so is the French & Belgian entry
  • United Kingdom - BBC Viewing figures down to eight million
  • Azerbaijan - Looking for their 2011 entry come September
  • Eurovision 2010 - In Numbers
This is just a selection. Go to www.oikotimes.com for detailed information.
 
New Release:  Former Maltese entrant to the Junior Eurovision Song Contest (2005) - Thea Saliba has released her brand new song which we had the big pleasure to premier on Radio International this week. The new song is called "I'm your Fan" which was produced and recorded in April 2010. Good luck to Thea and her musical career. To find out more about Thea visit the following website by clicking here.
 
Eurovision Quiz:  Scratch your heads and bite your nails at  our weekly Eurovision Song Contest Quiz. Good luck and win a copy of a past Eurovision Song Contest Final on DVD. The first two right entries naming the five clips with Artist, Song, Country and Year will win. Send your answers via the website's CONTACTs page.
