  It's CHRISTMAS

The Eurovision Radio International Christmas Card 2015: The Christmas Edition of Eurovision Radio International is broadcast on the 23 December 2015 where Santa JP will be delivering the greatest Christmas Songs covered and performed by the finest Stars of the Eurovision Song Contest and beyond. Hear Christmas Classics sung by Johnny Logan, Celine Dion, Il Volo, Mans Zelmerloew, Birthe Kjaer, Chiara, Kurt Calleja, Helena Paparziou, Dana, Ola and many many more. This is the perfect soundtrack to your Christmas 2015. If you cannot be live part of the show you can download the show after the live broadcast via the Show Archive and play it back during the Festive Days. All the other parts of the show such as the interviews with the Eurovision singers, Eurovision News, Coverspot, etc are back in the first edition of Eurovision Radio International on 04 January 2016. 

Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - The Best of Melodifestivalen:  With the victory of Mans Zelmerloew in Vienna winning the Eurovision Song Contest 2015 with the song "Heroes" the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 will be stage in Stockholm's Globen Arena in Sweden next May. Without doubt Swedish Television produces the biggest National Final there is to select the song that represents Sweden at the Eurovision Song Contest filling large halls across the country in the stages of Melodifestivalen - sometimes even bigger than the Eurovision Song Contest itself. In view of the upcoming event in Stockholm the team of Radio International looks back at some of the greatest songs that emerged from the Melodifestivalen over the years. The series continues this week with Alasdair Rendall who closes out the series looking at Melodifestivalen 2010.

Eurovision Song Contest News: Normally, escXtra.com's Nathan Waddell joins the team in the studio every week to bring you the news and the latest developments connected to the Eurovision Song Contest. Whether it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc.  Nathan will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International.
In addition, Eurovision News is available on www.eurovision.tv, escxtra.com, esctoday.com and escflashmalta.com and many others.

Eurovision Radio International on 30 December 2015 - Something Special coming your way: JP is taking a week off for holidays and Sir David Mann is taking over the show single handedly and has a special show for you on 30 December 2015 which is the final edition of Eurovision Radio International for 2015.
In the first two hours of the show David Mann presents "Les Girls", a two-part programme looking back over the sixty editions of the Eurovision Song Contest and playing the songs, not necessarily performed by the ladies, but those which have actually featured girls names in their titles - it's surprising how many there have been! So listen out for the original artists performing Jenny, Elouise, Julie, Mata-Hari, Maria Magdelena, Mary Ann and Lejla amongst many others, plus Mrs Caroline Robinson, Mrs Thompson and Miss Kiss Kiss Bang! This is still to celebrate the 60 years of the Eurovision Song Contest.
Then in the third hour of the show is called "We've Got It Covered" -  and is a special hour-long edition of David's regular Radio International 'Covers Spot', featuring a mixture of some covers that were previously broadcast during the year and some that are getting their first play on the show this week. There's a chart rundown of the Top 5 songs from Eurovision 2015 in Vienna in cover versions and some of the stars of this year's Junior Eurovision Song Contest in Bulgaria perform covers of songs from the adult contests. Sebastian Calleja from Malta has yet another new Eurovision cover for us and, topping the bill for our final show of the year, we have Bing Crosby and Dame Shirley Bassey!
 
The normal format of Eurovision Radio International returns on 06 January 2016 will all its usual features.
