New Songs by Jedward and Hera Bjoerk:  Last week Radio Internationational was extremely happy to air the brand new song by Malta's Glen Vella "Ain't nobody". This week Radio International has received three new songs. Iceland's Hera Bjoerk has released a brand new song called "Feel the love tonight" and Ireland's Jedward have released two new songs this week which we will be airing during this week's edition of Radio International. "Bad Behaviour" and "Biggest Fan" taken from Jedward's upcoming album release. All the songs will be featured on the show this week - certainly not to be missed and we are thrilled to hear your comments on the show. Send us your opinion to CONTACT during the show. 
 
Interview with Amaury Vassili (France 2011): France as beind one of the Big Five Nations at the Eurovision Song Contest have a set place already in the Final and do not need to go through the Semi Finals. In 2011 France was represented by 22 year young Amaury Vassili with the song "Sognu". Unfortunately, "Sognu" did not do as good at the contest as many fans had hoped. Only Number 15 with 82 points was the result achieved by France at the 56th Eurovision Song Contest. However, a great singer - you can check out the website of Amaury Vassili by clicking here. Eric & JP of Radio International had the pleasure to chat with Amaury after his Press Conference but also Edward Montebello met the singer in the Press Centre. Listen to the two interviews clips on this week's edition of Radio International. 
 
Interview with Paradise Oskar (Finland 2011):  Axel Ehnström alias Paradise Oskar represented Finland at the 2011 Eurovision Song Contest withe a self-penned song called "Da da dam". First he successfully qualified Finland for the Grand Final which he then started off as the first song of the evening. Paradise Oskar received a total of 57 points placing him on Number 21 in the Grand Final. Eric & JP had the chance to meet the singer right after his Press Conference for an interview which you can hear during this week's edition of Radio International.   
 
Interview with Witloof Bay (Belgium 2011): On the Easter Weekend 2011 the Belgium Town of Gent welcomed three Eurovision Delegations to the Town Hall and as host country for this event  two members of Witloof Bay  (Nicolas Dorian and Roxorloop) represented the group in the Town Hall of Gent at the Welcome Reception and later showed the international delegations and the media the city of Gent. After the cultural part of the event the artists and media went to a television studio where the artists were interviewed. Radio International took the chance and interviewed Witloof Bay who  represented Belgium at the 2011 Eurovision Song Contest in Semi Final 2 with the song "With love baby" unfortunately, missed to qualify Belgium for the Grand Final of the 56th Eurovision Song Contest by just ONE Point. They came 11th with 53 points.
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight:  Joining JP in the studio this week is the Radio International regular Sir Ross Bennett from the UK. Since this year a duet has won the Eurovision Song Contest - Eldar & Nigar from Azerbaijan with "Running scared" Ross will take a look at other duets that have won the great Eurovision Song Contest over the years. Enjoy Ross presenting it in his particualr style.
