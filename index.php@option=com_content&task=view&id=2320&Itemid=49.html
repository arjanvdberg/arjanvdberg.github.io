Interview with Nikki Kavanagh (Ireland) & Gorgi (Composer & Songwriter): This week Radio International is delighted to interview live during the show one of Ireland's best singers - Nikki Kavangh who has been attempting to represent Ireland at the Eurovision Song Contest. in 2011. Unfortunately, for Nikki she lost out to Jedward this year but surely, she will be back in 2012. Also joining us during that interview her lcomposer and yricist and also a well-known voice to fans of Eurovision - Gorgi. He joins the team in the studio via phone from Greece and Nikki via phone from Ireland. For further information on Nikki Kavangh visit her Myspace page by clicking here.
 
Interview with Emmy (Armenia 2011): Armenia was represented  at the 2011 Eurovision Song Contest by the energetic Emmy. Her entry called "Boom Boom" competed in Semi Final 1 and reach 54 points ranking her at Number 12 which means that she did not qualify her country for the Grand Final. However, a great personality listen to the interview Eric & JP did with Emmy after her first Press Conference in Duesseldorf.  
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - Malta (Part 1):  This week Marcus Keppel Palmer returns to the airwaves of Radio International with a holiday spirit as JP has been on holidays in Malta and Marcus is about to go to Malta and the the Eurovision Spotlight this week takes you to the sunny country of Malta and JP is your travel guide as well. Enjoy!
 
Eurovision Song Contest News:  Luke Fisher joins the team in the studio every week to bring you the news and the latest developments connected to the Eurovision Song Contest. Whether it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc Luke will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International. He also takes a look at some statistics such as viewing figures in the various countries and more.
In addition, Eurovision News is available on www.eurovision.tv, oikotimes.com, escdaily.com, esctoday.com and escflashmalta.com.
