Eurovision Song Contest with Radio International and Oikotimes.   
 
Back to the normal format this week where the Team of Radio International welcomes you to actively take part in our weekly show. Below is what is on this week's edition of Radio International. We also welcome two new members to our team and that is Dermot Manning from Ireland and Michael Goodrich.  Also part of the show is the Eurovision Quiz, your music requests, news and much more. Now enjoy listening to the show! :)
 
Eurovision 2010 Interview with Filipa Azevedo (Portugal, 2010): Continuing to broadcast interviews with some of the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest performers Radio International this week airs and interview with Filipa Azevedo who successfully qualified her country Portugal at this year's Eurovision Song Contest with the great song called "Há Dias Assim".  Portugal reached Number 4 in Semi Final 1 and in the Final Portugal ended up at Number 18. Listen to this interview during this week's edition of Radio International.
 
Eurovision Song Contest in Focus - Ireland:  The most successful country in terms of winning the Eurovision Song Contest is Ireland who took the trophy no less than seven times starting in 1970, 1980, 1987, 1992, 1993, 1994 and 1996 with the performers such as Dana, Johnny Logan, Linda, Martin, Secret Garden, Niamh Kavanagh, Eimear Quinn, Paul Harrington and Charlie McGettigan. That means that Ireland also hosted the contest for seven times. Our latest member in the Eurovision Team on Radio International is Dermot Manning who will be focusing on two particular Eurovision Song Contests namely 1971 and 1981. To find out more about Dermot and get a glimpse of how he looks like visit our Presenters' Page. 
 
Mysterious Eurovision Perfomer:  This popular part of the show returns this week with Chris Poppe where he wraps up a Eurovision performer or group in verbal and musical cluesfor the listeners to guess and win a prize. Who has Chris picked this week???? Give it a try and join us on the show for this exciting part of the programme.

We will call you during the show - have a nice chat with you and then play out with your favourite Eurovision song.
