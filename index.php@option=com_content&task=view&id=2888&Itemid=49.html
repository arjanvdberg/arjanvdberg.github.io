Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2012:  The 10th edition of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest was staged in the Heineken Music Hall in Amsterdam on Saturday, 01 December 2012 wth the participation of young artists from twelve countries: Albania, Armenia, Azerbaijan, Belarus, Belgium, Georgia, Israel, Moldova, Netherlands, Russia, Sweden and Ukraine competing for the trophy of the event which as we now know has been won by Anastasiya Petryk from Ukraine. Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - Sweden at the Eurovision Song Contest:  A brand new series has started last week where Radio International correspondants take a look at the entries of Sweden to the Eurovision Song Contest. Sweden won the Eurovision Song Contest five times kicking off with ABBA's "Waterloo" in 1974, followed by the Herreys ten years on in 1984 with "Diggi-Loo Diggi-Ley". Then with Carola in 1991 when she won with "Captured by a love storm". Swedish success continued in 1999 when Charlotte Nilssson took the honours with "Take me to your heaven" and in 2012 Loreen took the trophy in Baku for the Winning Song "Euphoria". But what about the other great Swedish entries that did not win. It is time to check them out on Radio International over the next few weeks.  This week Chris Poppe joins the team in the studio to take a look at the Swedish entries which will be 2001 till 2004.
