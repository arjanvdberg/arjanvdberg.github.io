Hightlights of the show

Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2015: This week is a rather special edition of Radio International as members of the Team are Sofia, Bulgaria - the Home of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2015. The 13th and 2015 edition of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest will be staged in Sofia, Bulgaria on 22 November 2015 from the Arena Armeec where 17 countries have confirmed participation in the music competition for children including newcomers Australia and Ireland. The complete list of the 17 countries are: Albania, Armenia, Australia, Belarus, Bulgaria, FYRO Macedonia, Georgia, Ireland, Italy, Malta, Montenegro, Russia, San Marino, Serbia, Slovenia, The Netherlands and Ukraine. This year's theme song of the event is "#Discover" sung by last year's Bulgarian representative Krisia. Consult the offical event's website Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2015 for all the latest news concerning the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2015 and also you will also find all detailed information about the young artists. JP and David of Radio International will be present at the event and conduct interviews with the young artists which you can hear during the shows after the event although video interviews will be made available as soon as ready on the accompanying Eurovision Blog .Good Luck to all the 17 young performing delegation and may the best song win. 

This week's show also comes from Sofia with JP and David introducing all the 17 songs and give you background information on the artists but also on the event. In addition, JP and David take you down Memory Lane of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest by playing you some of the highlights of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest past and present. Make sure not to miss this particular radio show as JP and David will be chatting with you on the show's Livestream Channel from 2000 - 2300 hours CET answering all your questions regarding the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2015. In regards to the great TV Show, should you live in a country that is not taking part in the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2015 and the show is not televised via the national broadcaster then you can find the livestream on the website of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2015. Complete details such as lists and Running Order of the 2015 contest you can find on the Wikipedia Page by clicking here. Good luck and a lot of fun to all the participants of the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2015. The normal format of Radio International returns at the same time next week with all the normal show features.
 
Interview with Vincenzo Cantiello (Junior Eurovision Winner 2014, Italy):  In 2014 Italy debuted at the Junior Eurovision Song Contest and also won it with Vincenzo Cantiello and the song "Tu primo grande amore". During the show this week Radio International is delighted to broadcast an interview with the young winner. Earlier on in May of 2015, Vincenzo came to the Eurovision Song Contest in Vienna "Building the Bridge" between Junior Eurovision and the Eurovision Song Contest. On this week's special edition listen to the interview we did with Vincenzo.
