Eurovision Song Contest with Radio International.
 
In our third hour of Radio International the series of Radio International Goes ... to the Eurovision Song Contest 2009 continues also this week. In Part 7 of the series we talk to Waldo's People from Finland who reached Number 25 with "Loose Control". Then we talked to Latvia's Intars Busulis who performed "Probka (Rush Hour)" unfortunately, failed to qualify for the Grand Final. We did a short interview with Belgium's Elvis look-a-like Patrick Ouchene who performed "Copy Cat" and we have the soundbite when Russia's Prime Minister Putin "gatecrashed" the Press Conference of Hungary's Zoli Adok. We also have some faninterview clips - so listen in - it might be YOU on the radio this week. :) All this is spiced with the best of 2009 Eurovision Song Contest entries and some special remixes as well.


Eurovision Song Contest Connections with Alasdair Rendall.  Alasdair takes a look at 35 years of Ralph Siegel songs at the Eurovision Song Contest. The Hit Hunter has been busy with editing many clips together. R rather interesting Eurovision slot with Alasdair Rendall.

Eurovision Radio Shows on other European Radio Stations 

Check out JP's All Eurovision Hit Radio on this website. Follow this link to listen. But there are also other radio stations offering nice Eurovision shows. Here is a small selection. Enjoy. 

EurovisiO is a weekly radio show produced in the studios of Oradio in Antwerp, Belgium.  It airs every Monday evening from 2200 - 2300 hours and is presented by Philip Vereyken.  A must for Eurovision freaks. Lots of nice Eurovision music, interviews and news - however, in the Flemish language. You can listen to a repeat of EurovisO by clicking here. Please note that the team of EurovisO takes a couple of months summer break to return fully recharged with a change. Stay tuned!
 
Malta's Eurovision Radio comes from the sunny island presented by Deo Grech on Super ONE Radio every Tuesday from 2045 - 2145 hours. You can contact Deo during the show and request your favourite Eurovision songs. The show is in Maltese mixed with English. Click here for further information and also if you would like to listen to back issues of Malta's Eurovision Radio. Please note that the team of Malta's Eurovision Radio also takes a couple of months summer break to return fully recharged with a change. Stay tuned!
 
Colin Berry, the legendary voice from BBC Radio 2 in the UK now hosts a Saturday Radio Show on BBC Three Counties Radio including a Eurovision Spot. Colin Berry was the spokesperson for the British jury for over 20 years.
