Interview with Monika and Vaidas (Lithuania 2015): Last weekend was the weekend of the Baltics in terms of selecting their songs for the Eurovision Song Contest 2015. Radio International's Eric was part of the International Jury during the live TV show in Lithuania and together with his Jury colleagues and televoting by the public Monika Linkyte & Vaidas Baumila were selected to represent Lithuania at the Eurovision Song Contest 2015 in Vienna with the song "This Time". Lithuania will compete in Semi Final 2 of the Eurovision Song Contest 2015. Right after the show Eric could chat with the winners and this interview will be broadcast on Radio International this week. Enjoy this charming couple! You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here

Interview with Aminata (Latvia):  Latvia had their National Final called SUPERNOVA on Sunday, 22 February 2015. After 20 artists went through two Heats, a Semi Final and a Final the winner of the SUPERNOVA became Aminata who will represent Latvia at the Eurovision Song Contest 2015 in Vienna come May with the song "Love injected" which is placed in Semi Final 2 of the Eurovision Song Contest 201. Frank Feiler from Join Us On Eurovision met Aminata after she won the National Final SUPERNOVA and conducted a first reaction interview with Latvia's Hopeful for Eurovision 2015. Listen to it on Radio International this week. Also Ian Mueller submitted some interviews from the Latvian Final which will be aired on Radio International. Check it out during the show. Both to Frank and Ian a huge THANK YOU for your kind contributiuon to the show. 
 
Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2015 - Interview with Jessika (Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2015): The Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2015 took place on 21 & 22 November 2014 from the Marsa Ship Yard in Malta with 20 songs entering into the Semi Final then narrowed down to a Top 14 and then on Saturday the winner was the public favourite as well as the jury favourite - Amber Bondin will be representing Malta at the Eurovision Song Contest with the song "Warrior" written by Elton Zarb and Matt Muxu, who also were responsible for the victory of Gaia Cauchi at the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2013 with the song "The Start". Continuing on this week's edition of Radio International listen to the interviews with the contestants and this week it is the turn of another fantastic singer from Malta and a great friend of the show - Jessika (alias Jessica Muscat). The young and popular singer (and language teacher in Malta) attempted various times to represent Malta at the Eurovision Song Contest and again successfully qualified for the Final of the Malta Eurovision Song Contest 2015 and then came nineth in the Final with the song "Fandango". Enjoy listening to this interview with the charming Jessika on this week's edition of Radio International. You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - The National Final Season 2015: Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - The National Final Season 2015:  The National Final Season to the Eurovision Song Contest 2015 is well under way.  During the last weeks Radio International went through the history of Austria at the Eurovision Song Contest. Austria will host the Eurovision Song Contest 2015 in the Wiener Stadthalle in Vienna next May with the Grand Final on the 23 May 2015. Like in previous years the Radio International Eurovision Experts will be assessing the National Finals 2015 live on Radio International. Ross Bennett continues the season taking a look at the National Finals and entries Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania and Sweden.
 
Eurovision UnderCover:  This ever so popular feature on Radio International is David Mann's weekly edition of the Eurovision Cover Versions where David Mann from Eurovision Norwich will be intoducing some of the finest Eurovision Song Contest entries covered by other singers. Just be surprised. You will be hearing the actual song that has been on the Eurovision stage followed by the cover. Listeners are encouraged to send in their cover versions they came across over the years to share with the other fans tuning into the show. Eurovision Song Contest News: Nathan Waddell joins the team in the studio every week to bring you the news and the latest developments connected to the Eurovision Song Contest such as the National Finals or if it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc Nathan will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International. He also takes a look at some statistics such as viewing figures in the various countries and more. Nathan is website editor with escxtra.com. In addition, Eurovision News is available on www.eurovision.tv, esctoday.com and escflashmalta.com (amongst others.
