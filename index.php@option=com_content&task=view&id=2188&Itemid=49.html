Eurovision Interview with Richard Edwards (Malta Preselection 2011): Last we Radio International had the pleasure of having Glen Vella live on the show. Glen is the winner of the Maltese National Selection and with the will represent Malta at the 2011 Eurovision Song Contest come May in Semi Final 1. This week however, Radio International will broadcast an interview with the runner-up of the Maltese National Selection - Richard Edwards. JP had the pleasure to interview the singer during the rehearsals who song "Finally" was the last song in the Semi Final and managed to sore right up to Number 2 in the Final. It was a Head-to-Head race between Richard and Glen until the last remaining vote of Riccardo Cocciante decided as Head of Jury in favour of Glen Vella for Duesseldorf. 
 
Interview with Claas Triebel, co-author of "Ein bisschen Wahnsinn":  OGAE Germany had their annual convention last January and also invited two of the three authors of the book "Ein bisschen Wahnsinn". This book is a rather special one as it features many statistics and rarities  observed at the Eurovision Song Contest such as "the longest and the shortest songs", "male and female performers", "close to victory", "white suits", "barefeet performances", "famous nill point", "most patient countries to victory", "languages used", "glowes", etc This week Radio International has the pleasure to welcome Claas Triebel to the show to find out more about this book and what made them write it. The book is published by Kunstmann in Germany. More information will be available during the interview.

Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight: National Finals: The national selection season well underway and on a weekly basis Radio International will assess the songs with a panel of Eurovision experts. This week Radio International continues to take a look at the songs for the 2011 Eurovision Song Contest from Bosnia & Herzegovina, Georgia, Germany, Italy and Spain. They decided on the weekend and Bulgaria is deciding during this week's edition of the show and Radio International tries to be on the ball to bring you the song as soon as it has been announced.  Of course, there were many national Semi Finals, Second Chance rounds and Heats we take a look at.  For that Alasdair Rendall joins the team in the studio.

Whether it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc Luke will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International.
