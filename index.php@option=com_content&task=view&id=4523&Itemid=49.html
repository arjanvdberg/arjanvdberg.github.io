Hightlights of the show

Eurovision Song Contest 2016 - The POST EUROVISION DEPRESSION CURE:  The Eurovision Song Contest 2016 is over and the best ever Eurovision Song Contest was staged in the Globen Arena with two Semi Finals and a Grand Final which was held on Saturday 14 May 2016. The 61st Eurovision Song Contest was won by Ukraine's Jamala with the song "1944". Many fans of the Eurovision Song Contest fall into a deep Post Eurovision Depression as it all is over and the fans part company until the next year. Radio International is the perfect Post Eurovision Depression Cure reflecting on the past contest with interviews and reports and of course the music and remixes. The full results of the contest can be viewed by clicking here.
 
The Eurovision Song Contest 2016 Top 26: During this week's edition of the show JP runs down the Eurovision 2016 Chart as shown on the big Scoreboard on Saturday night the Grand Final of the Eurovision Song Contest 2016. Listen to JP playing clips of all the songs in the order how they ranked at the end of the voting. 
 
Interview with Jamala (Eurovision 2016 Winner, Ukraine):  At the end of a new and very exciting voting presentation hosted by last year's Eurovision Winner Mans Zelmerloew and Petra Mede the thrill was there till the last moment when Ukraine was announced as the winner of the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 with a total of 534 points for the song "1944" performed by Jamala. Prior the Grand Final Eric chatted with the singer from Ukraine which you can hear on this week's edition of Radio International.
 
Interview with Dami Im (Australia 2016):  In 2015 Australia debuted at the Eurovision Song Contest with Guy Sebastian performing  "Tonight again"coming 5th in Vienna. This year Dami Im bettered that result in becoming the Runner Up of the the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 with the song "Sound of Silence" being the favourite of the Juries receiving 511 points. Listen to a small interview clip done at the Red Carpet. 
 
Interview with Sergey Lazarev (Russia 2016): With the best stage show and the favourite of the Televoting Russia's Sergey Lazarev reach third place at the end of the voting of the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 receiving 491 points for the song "You are the only one". In the rehearsal week the Team of Radio International chatted with Sergey. Listen to this interview on this week's edition of Radio International.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview with Poli Genova (Bulgaria 2016):  The best ever placed result for Bulgaria at the Eurovision Song Contest as Poli Genova with the song "If love was a crime" came fourth at the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 with a total of 307 points. Having interviewed Poli at the preview events Radio International had the pleasure to catch up with the charming and ever smiling singer from Bulgaria who also hosted the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2015 last December. You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview with Amir (France 2016):  A very charismatic singer France sent into the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 who was also tipped to be the winner of the 61st Eurovision Song Contest. Unfortunately, it did not work out this time. Amir represented France with the song "J'ai cherche" came 6th in the Grand Final on Saturday 14 May 2016. Radio International's JP had the pleasure to chat with Amir after his first rehearsal.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Interview with Mans Zelmerloew and Petra Mede (Hosts of Eurovision 2016):  Just before the Dress Rehearsals kicked in a Press Conference was held with the participation of the Hosts of the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 - Petra Mede and last year's Eurovision Winner Mans Zelmerloew. After this Press Conference Radio International had the pleasure to chat with both artists. Listen to that interview as well as their Interval Acts of the 61st Eurovision Song Contest on this week's edition of Radio International.  You can also watch this interview on our YouTube Channel - click here
 
Eurovision Song Contest Spotlight - Eurovision 2016 - An Expert Look Back:  For the next four weeks Radio International's Eurovision Experts will be reflecting on the 61st Eurovision Song Contest and assessing Europe's biggest music show which was also broadcast in China as well as for the first time in the USA. This week Dermot Manning starts with the look back at Stockholm 2016.

Eurovision Under Cover:  This ever so popular feature on Radio International is David Mann's weekly edition of the Eurovision Cover Versions where David Mann from Eurovision Norwich will be intoducing some of the finest Eurovision Song Contest entries covered by other singers. Just be surprised. You will be hearing the actual song that has been on the Eurovision stage followed by the cover. Listeners are encouraged to send in their cover versions they came across over the years to share with the other fans tuning into the show. Alternatively, listeners can send in their request for a particular Eurovision covered song using this link. 


Eurovision Song Contest News: Nathan Waddell joins the team in the studio every week to bring you the news and the latest developments connected to the Eurovision Song Contest such as the National Finals or if it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc Nathan will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International. He also takes a look at some statistics such as viewing figures in the various countries and more. Nathan is website editor with escxtra.com. In addition, Eurovision News is available on www.eurovision.tv, esctoday.com and escflashmalta.com (amongst others.
 
New Song Releases by Eurovision Artists:  Radio International is always keen to know how the careers of Eurovision performers develop after their performance at the event. The show is in collaboration with many Music Companies that supply Radio International with the latest songs of their signed up artists to bring the muisc closer to the fans. On a weekly basis Radio International receives songs which are being featured on the show.
