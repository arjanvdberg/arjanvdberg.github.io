Radio International and ESCNEWS Radio & TV with Chat Room: Radio International is happy to announce the collaboration with ESCNEWS Radio & TV. Radio International is aired on their TV channel live every Wednesday where fans can join and see how we actually do Radio International and listen to the show while chatting with the other Eurovision fans joining the community.  But also you can watch there a re-run of the weekly edition of Radio International at various times on ESCNEWS Radio. Enjoy and tell your friends to join and be part of the Eurovision Radio International Community! Also become part of the Eurovision Radio International Facebook Group which accompanies the show and is available here. It contains lots of Eurovision News from the major Eurovision News Websites but also announcements. 

Please note that many interviews we air are also available as video interview which can be viewed on our Radio International YouTube Channel. Just take a look and enjoy also see the artists talking to us. 

The Eurovision Song Contest with Eurovision Radio International - your music requests, comments and ideas: Eurovision Radio International never stops and is keen on keeping the music and news of the Eurovision Song Contest in the ears of the many fans that join the show each and every week via the usual means either on FM, Cable and also via PODCAST, Itunes, LISTEN AGAIN, live broadcast or Live Stream. You can also find Radio International (RTV Katwijk) on TuneIn. Please feel free to submit your comments, ideas and requests of Eurovision songs and Radio International is delighted to play your requests during the shows. Click on CONTACT to submit your request to the programme. You can also reach us on Twitter: @RadioInt

Holidaying Radio International:  For the next three weeks the Team of Radio International will be on holidays and JP will broadcast three special shows to you continuing this week with more music editions of the show and a different format. Due to the Summer Holiday schedule there is no Eurovision New, Eurovision Clip Quiz and special features with our correspondence. The usual format with its normal features of Eurovision Radio International returns on our show on 07 October 2015. But for now enjoy what is planned for you for the coming shows. Happy Listening.

The Eurovision 2014 Top 40 compiled by the Eurovision Fans in Venezuela:  Every year when presenters go on their well-earned summer vacation we think what to offer the listeners and this year has been extremely creative. Last week we started with taking a look at the 40 most watched interviews on the Eurovision Radio International YouTube Channel. JP runs down the Top 40 chart by playing the songs of the artists being interviewed over the years and also informing you how many people watched each interview.In case you missed last week's show fear not as it is available on the Show Archive - just click here. Early in 2015 Radio International received a Top 40 chart of songs compiled by the members of the Eurovision Fan Club in Venezuela. Each member was ask to rank 25 songs from the Eurovision Song Contest 2014, also including Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2014 as well as National Final entries and songs by 2014 Eurovision artists. And over the next three editions of Eurovision Radio International the first hour is fuly devoted to this Top 40 Chart as JP runs down what the fans in Venezuela have been voting for. What did the fans across the big waters select to be their Number 1 song of 2014. Don't miss this particular show!
 
Eurovision's Greatest Hits - Revisiting 1960 and 1987:  The Eurovision Song Contest has become 60 years old in 2015 and to celebrate this 60th Anniversary Radio International launches a new special series this week where JP will visit a particlar Eurovision year in each hour. So this week in the second hour of the show it will be the Eurovision Song Contest of 1960 and your chance to hear all the songs in the order how they ranked at the end of the voting. Hour 3 will be all the hits from 1987. Enjoy the great Trip down the Eurovision Memory Lane.
 
Eurovision Song Contest News: During the summer months there is the usual lack of Eurovision news but if there is something happening you find it on escXtra.com's. Nathan Waddell re-joins the team in the studio in October to bring you every week to bring you the news and the latest developments connected to the Eurovision Song Contest. Whether it is music releases of Eurovision performers, special concerts, gossip, etc.  Nathan will have it all for us during the weekly edition of Radio International. In addition, Eurovision News is available on www.eurovision.tv, escxtra.com, esctoday.com and escflashmalta.com and many others.
